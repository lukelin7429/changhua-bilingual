About Our School

A Young School with Old Roots

一所田園裡的新興小學

"A field, a small town, a school" — the line we were founded on.

In March 2005, after sixteen years of planning, Hu Bei opened its doors in the north fields of Xihu Township — serving the four villages of Xiliao, Dongliao, Taiping, and Datu. The school's founding line was simple: a field, a small town, a school — a warm place for the children of Xihu to grow up joyful and healthy.

Twenty years on, that small school has grown into a campus humming with track teams, table tennis champions, robotics builders, English reciters, and a rooftop full of solar panels. In December 2025, we celebrated our 20th anniversary. We are no longer new — but we are still young.

2005 年 3 月，歷經 16 年的籌備，湖北國小在溪湖鎮北側的田園間正式開學——學區涵蓋西寮、東寮、太平、大突四個里。建校時的一句話很簡單：「田園、小鎮、學校」——讓溪湖的孩子，在愛心溫暖的園地裡，歡欣愉悅、健康成長。

二十年過去，這所小學長成一座有田徑隊、桌球冠軍、機關王機械人、英語朗誦者，與屋頂上整片太陽光電板的校園。2025 年 12 月，我們剛剛慶祝了創校 20 週年。我們不再是新的，但我們依然年輕。

Four Visions, Four Sides

The Words That Run Our Halls

走廊上的四個字

Energy, Diversity, Gratitude, Creativity — not slogans on a wall, but the four sides of every Hu Bei day. 活力、多元、感恩、創新——不是牆上的標語，而是湖北每一天的四個面向。

E
Energy
活力 · 健康快樂
Bodies that run, throw, kick, skate.

Track and field, table tennis, football, basketball, skating, taekwondo — six sports teams under one elementary roof. Our kids represent Changhua at the national level almost every spring. 田徑、桌球、足球、籃球、溜冰、跆拳道——六支運動隊伍同住一個小學屋頂。每年春天，我們的孩子幾乎都站上全國級的賽場。

D
Diversity
多元 · 學習成長
Many doors, all open.

Vietnamese mooncake workshops, Beiguan opera, farm-to-school lessons, world children's art exhibition, Changhua poetry festival, XR cross-school learning. One school year, many windows on the world. 越南冰皮月餅、北管曲牌、食農教育、世界兒童畫展、彰化詩歌節、XR 跨校共學。一個學年，許多面向世界的窗。

G
Gratitude
感恩 · 心情點滴
A small town that thanks back.

Story-Telling Parents, mother's day family walks, community service, intergenerational respect. A school grateful to its village — and a village that knows the children of the school. 故事爸爸媽媽、母親節親子健走、社區服務、敬老尊賢。一所感謝村庄的學校，一個認得學校孩子的村庄。

C
Creativity
創新 · 卓越新穎
Hands that build the next thing.

Gigo Smart Mechanism King, Changhua Science Fair, Quanta Smart Cup, Scratch competitions, generative AI on canvas tote bags, one-tablet-per-student, 5G smart school. STEM is daily here. 世界機關王大賽、彰化縣科展、廣達遊智盃、Scratch 競賽、生成式 AI 帆布袋、生生用平板、5G 智慧學校。STEM 是我們的日常。

Bilingual · 雙語

Let's Celebrate 外師到校與一年級的英語課

The classroom screen reads "LET'S CELEBRATE." Our visiting foreign English teacher works with low-grade homerooms across the year — less translation, more first contact.

教室螢幕寫著「LET'S CELEBRATE」。湖北的外師全年帶低年級——少一些翻譯，多一些「第一次的英文」。

English · 英語朗讀

The October Recital 校內英語朗讀比賽

Every October, a stage, a microphone, a poem in English. Our students learn early that speaking up is the second half of speaking well.

每年十月，一個舞台、一支麥克風、一首英文詩。我們的孩子很早就知道——「敢唸出來」是「唸得好」的另一半。

Diversity · 多元文化

Vietnamese Snow-Skin Mooncakes 越南美食體驗 · 冰皮月餅

Mid-December: a Vietnamese mother of one of our students brings rice flour, mung bean paste, and pastel dye, and shows the homeroom how a mooncake is made on the other side of the South China Sea.

十二月中，一位湖北學生的越南媽媽帶來糯米粉、綠豆沙、淡彩食用色素，教整班孩子認識「南海另一邊」的中秋。

Farm-to-School · 食農教育

What the Field Teaches 10 月的食農教育

Xihu is grape country, vegetable country, sheep country. Our students go to the field, see the dirt, eat the season, and bring back questions our textbooks haven't asked yet.

溪湖是葡萄之鄉、蔬菜之鄉、羊肉之鄉。湖北的孩子去田裡，看泥土、吃當季、帶回課本還沒問過的問題。

Champions · 全國級

The National Track Meet 全國小學田徑錦標賽

May 2026 — Hu Bei's track team in the National Elementary Track and Field Championship. Six sports teams, hundreds of training hours, one school of two hundred kids on the national startline.

2026 年 5 月，湖北田徑隊站上全國小學田徑錦標賽。六支運動隊伍、上百小時訓練、兩百多人的小學，站到了全國起跑線上。

Identity · 校門意象

The Carp at the Dragon Gate 校門口的「魚躍龍門」

A carved relief at our school gate — the legendary carp that leaps the Dragon Gate and becomes a dragon. Twenty years later, we are still telling that story to every child who walks under it.

校門口的浮雕——傳說中越過龍門的鯉魚化為龍。二十年過去，我們仍然每天和每個從門下走過的孩子，講這個故事。

Come Visit 歡迎蒞校交流

We welcome educators, parents, and friends — from neighbouring schools in Xihu, from across Changhua, and from anywhere in the world — to visit, ask questions, and share what works on a campus built around four visions.

歡迎溪湖鄰近學校、彰化縣內各校、以及世界各地的教育工作者、家長與朋友——蒞校交流、提出問題，把彼此校園裡有效的做法相互傳承下去。
