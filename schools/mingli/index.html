I.

About Mingli

關於明禮 · 田中鎮一所扎根土地、放眼世界的小學
A small school with a big heart — rooted in Tianzhong's rice fields, reaching for the world.
一所小而溫暖的學校——從田中鎮的稻田出發，放眼全世界。

Welcome to Mingli Elementary School, a public primary school nestled in Tianzhong Township — the heart of Changhua County's famous rice-farming region. Our school community of around eighty students may be small, but the learning experiences we offer are as rich as any urban institution.

歡迎來到明禮國小，一所座落在彰化縣田中鎮的公立小學——這裡是台灣著名的稻米產地，也是我們孩子成長的土地。我們大約有八十位學生，雖然規模不大，但學習的豐富程度毫不遜色。

Our curriculum weaves together bilingual education, agricultural experience, ecological awareness, and community connection. Students grow okra and rice, learn traditional crafts like tea ceremony and pottery, and practise English through real-world activities — ordering food, navigating the high-speed rail station, and welcoming international visitors.

我們的課程將雙語教育、農業體驗、生態意識與在地連結融為一體。孩子們種植秋葵與水稻、體驗茶道與陶藝，也在真實情境中練習英文——點餐、高鐵站導覽、接待國際訪客。

In December 2025, Mingli earned the prestigious Taiwan-US Green Flag certification — a testament to our long-term commitment to sustainability, healthy living, and ecological education. We are proud to be an eco-school in the truest sense of the word.

2025 年 12 月，明禮通過了備受肯定的臺美生態學校綠旗認證——是我們長年投入永續教育、健康生活與生態課程的最佳見證。

"Planting living water in every child's life — creating peak experiences."
「為孩子的生命注入活水，創造高峰經驗」
—— 校長 洪士訓 的教育理念
II.

Four Pillars

校長辦學四大主軸 · 在地扎根、放眼世界、終身學習

Principal Hung Shih-Hsun's vision for Mingli rests on three guiding words — root locally, see globally, learn for life. From this foundation, four concrete pillars shape every aspect of school life:

洪士訓校長以「在地扎根、放眼世界、終身學習」為辦學方針，在此基礎上建立四大具體主軸，構成明禮校園生活的完整面貌：

PILLAR 01
🌐
Bilingual & Global
雙語國際 全球鏈結
Rooting bilingual education in daily school life — from campus environment and classroom English to cross-cultural exchanges and international issue discussions. Students build the knowledge, attitude, and global awareness to connect with the world.
PILLAR 02
💡
Technology & Innovation
科技教育 翻轉未來
Mobile learning, cloud resources, and action devices bring innovation into every classroom. Together with the wider community, students develop the problem-solving skills and creative technology literacy they need for tomorrow.
PILLAR 03
📚
Character & Reading
品德為首 閱讀為重
Character education is the foundation of all learning. Alongside it, a rich reading programme — author visits, sustained silent reading, storytelling circles, and family reading partnerships — opens every student's mind to the world of ideas.
PILLAR 04
🌱
School-Based & Community
校本課程 在地共好
Mingli's school-based curriculum draws deeply from Tianzhong's agricultural heritage — rice culture, scarecrow-making, tea ceremony, pottery. Education walks into the community; the community grows into the school.
"Root locally, see globally, learn for life."
「在地扎根、放眼世界、終身學習」—— 洪士訓校長的辦學方針。
III.

Green Flag School

臺美生態學校 · 綠旗認證
Taiwan-US Eco-Schools · 臺美生態學校
🌿
Green Flag Certified
通過綠旗認證 · December 2025
Mingli earned the Taiwan-US Eco-Schools Green Flag in December 2025 — recognition of our whole-school commitment to sustainable food systems, healthy living, and responsible waste management. From farming local rice to composting kitchen scraps, sustainability is woven into our daily school rhythm.

明禮在 2025 年 12 月通過臺美生態學校綠旗認證——從在地米食文化、健康生活到廢棄物管理，永續精神融入每一天的校園節奏。
