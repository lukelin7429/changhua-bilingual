I.

Our Bilingual Programme

雙語教育計畫 · 在田中鎮的土地上，用兩種語言認識世界

Mingli's bilingual programme grows out of a simple belief: English is not a subject to be tested, but a language to be used — in the rice paddies, at the pottery studio, on the high-speed rail platform, and in every real-world encounter that a Tianzhong child might have.

明禮的雙語教育計畫源自一個簡單的信念：英語不是要考的科目，而是要使用的語言——在稻田裡、在陶藝工坊、在高鐵月台，以及在田中孩子可能遇到的每一個真實場景。

The programme is a collaboration between My Culture Connect (MCC) — the non-profit organisation that has been placing foreign English teachers in Changhua's rural schools since 2009 — and the Changhua County International Education and English Resource Centre (CIEETRC). Together, they bring world-class bilingual support to a school of eighty students in a township known not for its internet speeds, but for its rice.

這個計畫是人師教育協會（MCC）——自 2009 年起在彰化縣偏鄉媒合外師的非營利組織——與彰化縣國際教育暨英語教育資源中心（CIEETRC）的合作成果。他們共同為一所八十位學生的小鎮學校，帶來了城市等級的雙語支援。

My Culture Connect · 人師教育協會
Non-profit organisation placing foreign English teachers (FETs) in Changhua's rural schools since 2009. Produces bilingual learning content, manages school partnerships, and runs the Changhua Bilingual Hub.

彰化縣國際教育暨英語教育資源中心
Changhua County's official International Education and English Resource Centre — providing policy support, teacher training, and county-wide coordination for bilingual and international education initiatives.

Classroom English

課室英語 · 雙語教師的日常教學用語

Bilingual teaching happens every day in the classroom — and it works best when teachers have natural, well-phrased English expressions ready for every moment of the school day.

雙語教學每天都在教室裡發生——當老師備有流暢、自然的課室英語表達，雙語環境就能在日常中真實成型。

IV.

Changhua Bilingual Hub

彰化雙語資源網 · 明禮是彰化 100+ 校雙語大家庭的一員

Mingli's bilingual programme is part of a wider network — the Changhua Bilingual Hub, which connects over 100 schools, 80+ foreign teachers, and thousands of bilingual learning videos across Changhua County.

明禮的雙語計畫是更大網絡的一部分——彰化雙語資源網連結了彰化縣 100 多所學校、80 位以上的外師，以及數千支雙語學習影片。
