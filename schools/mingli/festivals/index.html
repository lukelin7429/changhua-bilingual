I.

Festivals at Mingli

節慶英語 · 用每一個節日開一扇通往世界的窗
Every festival at Mingli is a chance to learn something new in English — and to share Taiwan's culture with the world. From moon cakes to Easter bunnies, from lantern-painting to Christmas songs, bilingual celebration is woven into the Mingli school year.
明禮的每個節日都是用英語學新事物的機會——也是向世界分享台灣文化的時刻。從月餅到復活節兔子、從彩繪燈籠到聖誕歌曲，雙語慶典是明禮一整年的日常。
🌕
Autumn · 秋季

Moon Festival

中秋節

Mingli celebrates the Moon Festival with an English listening competition — students tune in to a recording by our teacher and answer questions about the holiday, moon cakes, and family traditions.

English Listening Raffle 聽力競賽 Moon Cake 月餅 Family Gathering 家人團聚
🍎
Autumn · 秋季

Teachers' Day

教師節

On Teachers' Day, Mingli honours its educators with a special English listening competition. Students listen to a recorded message from Teacher Maeve and celebrate the teachers who shape their daily lives.

English Listening Raffle 聽力競賽 Teacher Appreciation 感謝師恩 Student Performances 學生表演
🎄
Winter · 冬季

Christmas

聖誕節

Jingle bells at Mingli — students perform in a Christmas talent show with English self-introductions, Christmas acronym spelling, and festive performances that light up the whole school.

Talent Show 才藝表演 English Self-Intro 英文自我介紹 Christmas Acronym 節日縮寫

Spring · 春季

Lunar New Year

農曆新年

For Tianzhong Township's Lunar New Year celebration, Mingli students put their artistic touch on traditional lanterns — each one hand-painted, each one a colourful expression of a child's imagination and cultural pride.

Lantern Painting 彩繪燈籠 Community Event 鎮公所活動 Tradition 在地傳統
💐
Spring · 春季

Mother's Day

母親節

Students craft handmade pipe-cleaner bouquets for their mothers — guided by school volunteer parents, each child creates a personal gift that says "I love you" in colour, shape, and care.

Handmade Bouquets 手作花束 Parent Volunteers 志工媽媽 Love & Gratitude 愛與感恩
🐰
Spring · 春季

Easter

復活節

Story time brings Easter to Mingli — students enjoy the picture book My Easter Bunny, learning about Western holiday traditions through an illustrated bilingual reading session.

Story Time 繪本時間 My Easter Bunny 繪本 Western Culture 西方文化
