I.

Summer Camp

夏日樂學 · 三年的社區探索與農藝美學
Every summer, Mingli students step beyond the classroom — into local coffee shops, rice paddies, high-speed rail stations, and pottery studios — learning English through real experiences in their own community.
每年夏天，明禮的孩子走出教室——走進在地咖啡館、稻田、高鐵站與陶藝工坊，在真實的社區場景中學習英語。

E 農藝境

Eco-Farming & Aesthetic Exploration

明禮E農藝境：社區創生與美學探索

Five core values — Simple Fun, Trying New Things, Bright Ideas, Staying Healthy, Being Happy — guided students through sushi-making, sweet-twist pastry, eco-enzyme, Tianzhong pottery, weather station exploration, and an HSR station guided tour.

壽司製作 寶大甜麻花 田中陶藝 氣象站 高鐵站導覽
2024
E 食代

Community Food Exploration

明禮E食代社區探索

Students became community explorers — visiting Little Field Coffee beside the rice paddies, ordering food in English, buying tickets at Tianzhong HSR station, interviewing local farmers, and learning Tianzhong pottery at the pottery studio.

點餐對話 高鐵購票 在地農夫採訪 田中窯陶藝 環保堆肥
2023
田中穀倉

Rice Granary English Ambassador

學習擔任田中穀倉英文代言人

Students learned to be English ambassadors for Tianzhong — the "Rice Granary of Taiwan." Exploring the history of the 1937 granary building, Zhuoshui River rice culture, and the Pabao Rice brand, they practised presenting their hometown in English.
