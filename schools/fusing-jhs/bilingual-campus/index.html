I.

English Life on Campus

校園英語生活 · 從早自習到放學鐘聲
English isn't just a subject at Fusing — it's the soundtrack of campus life. Listen for it in the morning announcements, in the classroom, and on the line with students in Japan and Singapore.
在福興，英文不只是一個科目——它是校園生活的背景音。你可以在早晨的廣播、教室的對話、甚至與日本與新加坡學生連線的螢幕裡聽見它。

Two video resources — produced by My Culture Connect (人師教育協會) and shared across Fusing's classrooms — anchor our everyday English: Classroom English by Teacher Sarah Thomas, and Bilingual Announcements by Sarah and Susan. Both run as continuous playlists below.

我們有兩套由人師教育協會製作、跨校共用的影音資源支撐校園英語日常：課室英語（Sarah Thomas 老師）與廣播英語（Sarah 與 Susan）。兩條 playlist 完整嵌入在下方。

Classroom English

課室英語 · Sarah Thomas 老師主講

Ten classroom moments — from morning roll-call to handing out homework — taught in English with light Chinese cues. Designed for in-service teachers, but openly watchable for any student who wants to hear English used the way teachers actually use it.

十大課堂情境——從早晨的點名到分派作業——以英文為主、中文為輔。為在職教師設計，但任何想聽見「老師如何真實使用英文」的學生也歡迎觀看。

Bilingual Announcements

廣播英語 · 校園四處室自我介紹

Thirteen short episodes that introduce the school's four offices — Academic Affairs (5), Student Affairs (3), General Affairs, and Counselling — in both English and Chinese. Built for campus broadcasting, but equally useful as a quick orientation tour of any junior high.

13 集短片，以英中雙語介紹學校四處室——教務處（5 集）、學務處（3 集）、總務處、輔導室。為校園廣播情境設計，但也是任何國中校園導覽的最佳起點。
