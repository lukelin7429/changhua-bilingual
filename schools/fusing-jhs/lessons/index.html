I.

Classroom English

課室英語 · 給老師、給學生
A free, bilingual video series for teachers and students — ten classroom scenarios, taught by Teacher Sarah Thomas, produced by My Culture Connect.
為老師與學生量身打造的免費雙語教學影音——十大課堂情境，Sarah Thomas 老師主講，人師教育協會製作。

At Fusing, we believe English shouldn't only live inside the textbook — it should walk into the classroom every morning, into the announcement booth, and into the conversations students have with the world. Classroom English is one of the bilingual resources we share with every teacher and every student on campus.

在福興，英文不應該只活在課本裡——它應該在每天早晨的教室、廣播室，以及學生與世界的對話中真實出現。課室英語是我們與全校師生共享的雙語資源之一。

Maker & Robotics Curriculum
自造教育 × 機器人課程
As MOE's Technology Education Flagship School (2024), Fusing runs a Maker & Tech Centre and a robotics curriculum that weaves computational thinking into project-based learning across grades.
福興國中為教育部 114 學年度科技教育領航學校，設有自造教育與科技中心，機器人課程貫穿三個年級，將運算思維融入專題式學習。
📱
Digital Learning Platforms
數位學習平台 · Cool English / PaGamO / AILEAD365
Three platforms run alongside the regular curriculum — Cool English (vocabulary & listening drills), PaGamO (gamified quizzes across subjects), and AILEAD365 (AI-assisted feedback) — so practice continues seamlessly between school and home.
三個平台與正規課程並行：Cool English（單字與聽力操練）、PaGamO（遊戲化跨科測驗）、AILEAD365（AI 智能回饋）——讓學習在校園與家庭之間無縫銜接。
🌏
International Online Exchange
國際線上交流 · 日本與新加坡
Live online sessions and in-person visits connect Fusing students with peers in Japan and Singapore — putting English to use the way it was meant to be used: speaking with someone from another culture.
透過線上同步課程與實體出訪，福興學生與日本、新加坡夥伴學校的學生對話——讓英文回到它最該被使用的地方：與不同文化的人交流。
