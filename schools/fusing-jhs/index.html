I.

About Fusing

關於福興 · 紮根鄉土、放眼國際的鄉鎮型國中
A junior high school on the coastal plain of Changhua — where rural roots meet a global classroom, and where every child is invited to "rise with heart, and reach for the world."
座落於彰化縣福興鄉沿海平原的鄉鎮型國中——讓在地的根與世界的窗在校園交會，鼓勵每個孩子「以心啟航、走向世界」。

Fusing Junior High School sits in the heart of Fushing Township, a coastal community in central Changhua. Founded in 1962 as the Fushing Branch of Lukang High School, the school became fully independent in 1964 and was reorganised as a three-year junior high in 1968 under Taiwan's nine-year compulsory education reform.

福興國中座落於彰化縣福興鄉沿海地帶。民國 51 年（1962）以「彰化縣立鹿港中學福興分部」之名創辦，1964 年正式設置為彰化縣立福興初級中學，1968 年配合九年國教改制為國中，沿用至今。

Today, Fusing serves families from across the township's seven elementary feeder schools. We cultivate four interlocking strengths — Technology, Language, Sports, and the Arts — anchored in MOE recognitions including the Technology Education Flagship School (2024) and the Language Education Voyager School.

如今福興國中服務福興鄉七所學區國小的學生與家庭，以「科技、語文、體育、藝術」四大發展軸為主軸——榮獲教育部 114 學年度科技教育領航學校與語文教育啟航學校等多項認證。

"Rising with Heart, Reaching for the World."
「以心啟航・走向世界」——福興國中辦學精神。

III.

Champions on the Mat

體育班與全國冠軍跆拳道隊
Sports Programme · Est. 2008
National Taekwondo Champions
體育班 · 跆拳道全國冠軍隊

Fusing launched a dedicated Sports Programme in 2008, and our taekwondo team has gone on to win multiple national championships — putting a small-town rural junior high on Taiwan's competitive sports map. Sports here aren't a side activity; they're a path of discipline, mental focus, and self-respect. 福興國中自 2008 年設立體育班，跆拳道隊多次榮獲全國冠軍，讓一所沿海鄉鎮型國中站上全台競技舞台。體育不是配角，而是培養紀律、專注與自尊的修煉之路。

Featured Programs

MOE Technology Education Flagship
教育部科技教育領航學校 · 114 學年度
In 2024, Fusing was named a Technology Education Flagship School by Taiwan's Ministry of Education — recognising the school's Maker & Tech Centre, robotics curriculum, and integration of computational thinking across subject areas.
114 學年度（2024）福興國中榮獲教育部頒贈科技教育領航學校認證——肯定本校自造教育與科技中心、機器人課程、以及跨領域的運算思維融入教學。
🏆 MOE Flagship Award · 2024
📡
Digital Learning Platforms
數位學習平台 · 課堂日常
Cool English, PaGamO, and AILEAD365 are woven into the timetable — turning English drills, social-studies quizzes, and AI-assisted feedback into something students can pick up on their phones at home.
Cool English、PaGamO、AILEAD365 平台已融入日常課表——讓英文操練、社會科測驗、AI 智能回饋變成學生回家也能銜接的學習工具。
🌏
International Exchange · Japan & Singapore
國際交流 · 日本／新加坡夥伴學校
Through both online video lessons and in-person school visits, our students exchange ideas with peers in Japan and Singapore — building the confidence to speak across cultures, not just across textbooks.
透過線上連線課程與實體出訪雙軌，福興學生與日本、新加坡的夥伴學校交流——讓孩子在跨文化現場培養開口的勇氣，而不只是課本上的英文。
📖
Reading Achievement & Exam Lift
語文閱讀 × 會考減 C 提 A
Fusing is one of the few rural junior highs to hold both the Reading Achievement Voyager award and the National Exam Lift Voyager — for narrowing the rural-urban academic gap one student at a time.
福興同時獲頒提升學力揚帆學校與教育會考減 C 提 A 績優揚帆學校——是少數能同時拿下兩項殊榮的鄉鎮型國中，致力於縮小城鄉學力落差。
🏆 Twin Voyager Award · MOE
