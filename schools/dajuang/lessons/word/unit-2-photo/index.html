Scene 01

Taiwan Rain Trees

台灣欒樹觀察

What a fun day at school! These children are observing the beautiful Taiwan Rain Trees. They are sitting outside and drawing what they see.

多麼有趣的一天學校生活啊！這些孩子們正在觀察美麗的台灣欒樹。他們坐在戶外，畫下他們所看到的景象。

🐸
Scene 02

Sun Moon Lake Old Street

日月潭老街校外教學

The students went on a field trip to Sun Moon Lake! They explored the old street, looking at all the shops. And they also enjoyed some special frog-shaped sponge cakes.

學生們去日月潭校外教學了！他們探索了老街，逛了所有的商店。他們還享用了一些特別的青蛙造型雞蛋糕。

🍡
Scene 03

Winter Solstice Tangyuan

冬至湯圓製作

To celebrate Winter Solstice, a very special traditional festival, these students are learning to make tangyuan by hand!

為了慶祝冬至這個非常特別的傳統節日，這些學生正在學習親手製作湯圓！

⛵
Scene 04

Sun Moon Lake Boat Cruise

日月潭搭船遊湖

Cruising on the boat at Sun Moon Lake is so much fun! The students and the teachers are enjoying all the beautiful sights around them.

在日月潭搭船遊湖真是太有趣了！學生和老師們正享受著周圍所有美麗的景色。

🎅
Scene 05

Christmas Celebration

聖誕節慶祝

To celebrate Christmas, the students had a very special time at school! First, the teacher taught the students how to make Santa Claus decoration cards with colored paper.

為了慶祝聖誕節，學生們在學校度過了一段非常特別的時光！首先，老師教學生們如何用色紙製作聖誕老公公的裝飾卡片。

🏪
Scene 06

7-Eleven Math Practice

便利商店數學實踐

There's a new 7-Eleven near Dajuang Elementary School! Students walked there to buy things and practice math. They learned how to use math in real life.

大莊國小附近開了一間新的 7-Eleven！學生們走到那裡買東西，也順便練習數學。她們學習了如何在真實生活中運用數學。
