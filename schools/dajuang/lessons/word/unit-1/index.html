Word of the Day · Unit 01

School Life Around Campus

校園日常的英文

Word 01
sweep (v)
掃
Every day, students sweep away fallen leaves on school grounds.
每天學生打掃校園的落葉。
Students in Taiwan sweep the school grounds, keeping them clean and neat.
台灣的學生打掃校園，保持校園的乾淨整齊。
Word 02
Taiwanese (n)
台語
The students learn Taiwanese in class.
同學們在課堂上學習台語。
They learn Taiwanese by watching a video in class.
他們藉由觀看影片來學習台語。
Word 03
spring couplet
春聯
The man writes a spring couplet in black ink.
這個先生用黑墨汁寫春聯。
The spring couplet has wishes for good fortune.
春聯上有吉祥話。
Word 04
bubble (n)
泡泡
The students learn how to make bubbles outside.
同學們學習在室外拉泡泡。
They use different bubble wands to make different-sized bubbles.
他們使用泡泡棒拉出不同大小的泡泡。
Word 05
game (n)
遊戲
The students play a game in class.
同學們在上課時玩一個遊戲。
The game is rooted in Chinese mathematical principles.
這個遊戲的設計源自於中國的數學原理。
Word 06
morning warm-up exercise
健康操
The students do a morning warm-up exercise.
同學們在晨間做健康操。
The teachers join the morning warm-up exercise.
老師跟著小朋友一起做健康操。
Word 07
mud oven
土窯
The students make a mud oven.
同學們製作土窯。
They start a fire in the mud oven.
他們在土窯內起火。
Word 08
smoke (n)
煙
Smoke comes out of the mud oven.
煙從土窯中冒出來。
The smoke comes from the fire in the mud oven.
煙是從土窯內的火所產生的。
Word 09
ocarina performance
陶笛表演
The students go on stage for their ocarina performance.
同學們上台表演陶笛。
They have practiced for a long time for their ocarina performance.
他們為了這場陶笛表演已經練習很久了。
