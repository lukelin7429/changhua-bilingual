Inside the Classroom

課堂教學的英文

Word 01
professional development (n)
專業進修
In Taiwan, all schools have professional development sessions for teachers.
在台灣，所有學校都有教師專業訓練課程。
Teachers learn new skills and strategies during their professional development sessions.
在專業訓練課程中，老師可以學到新的教學技巧與策略。
Word 02
Chinese characters (n)
國字
The students learn Chinese characters in class.
同學們在課堂上學習國字。
They read and write Chinese characters.
他們閱讀並書寫國字。
Word 03
recess (n)
下課時間
After the bell rings for recess, most students go outside to play.
下課鈴聲響起後，大多數學生都在外面玩。
Recess is when kids can play outside, run around, and have fun.
下課時間是孩子們可以在外面玩耍、跑來跑去、玩得開心的時候。
Word 04
wash your hands (v)
洗手
It's important to wash your hands after using the bathroom.
上完廁所後洗手是很重要的。
Make sure to wash your hands with soap and water before eating.
確定在吃東西之前要用香皂和水來洗手。
Word 05
basketball (n)
籃球 · 鬥牛
Students love to play basketball during recess.
學生們喜歡在下課時打籃球。
A pick-up game is an on-the-spot game for basketball.
鬥牛就是現場組隊比賽的籃球。
Word 06
correct (v)
改正
The teachers correct the students' work.
老師改同學的作業。
They use a red pen to correct the students' work.
他們使用紅筆來改學生的作業。
Word 07
math class (n)
數學課
The students are in math class.
學生們正在上數學課。
The teacher teaches math in math class.
老師在數學課教數學。
Word 08
check-in (n)
檢核 · 認證
The students say English sentences for their check-in.
學生們說英語句子進行認證。
They get a stamp at the end of the check-in.
通過認證，他們會得到一個戳印（蓋章）。
Word 09
test (n)
考試
The students take tests in class.
同學們在課堂上考試。
They focus on taking their tests.
他們很專注於他們的考試。
