🏃 Graduation Triathlon · 畢業三鐵

Dajuang's Here · Youth on the Run

大莊來了 · 青春開跑

May 20, 2026 · 115/5/20 · Road Run Edition 路跑篇
Running out our youth with sweat, leaving memories with every step. 用汗水跑出青春、用腳步留下回憶。

English

On May 20, 2026, Dajuang Elementary's sixth graders ran the Road Run Edition of the Graduation Triathlon — one of three challenges they take on in the weeks before they leave the school. This road run is not just about distance; it is the last hot-blooded chapter of their elementary school life.

Step by step, the kids ran out their youth with sweat, and left memories with every footprint on the road. The teacher team ran alongside them, leading the class with energy and making sure every student finished. Volunteer parents stood at every corner cheering — they are the strength that keeps our children moving forward.

One hot-blooded mission down. Two more events to go before graduation.

中文

115 年 5 月 20 日，大莊國小六年級孩子們完成畢業三鐵的「路跑篇」——這是他們離開校園前要挑戰的三項任務之一。《大莊來了·青春開跑》，用汗水跑出青春、用腳步留下回憶，這場路跑，跑的不只是距離，更是國小生活最後的熱血篇章。

畢業前的熱血任務，感謝教師團隊帶學生熱情參賽、順利完賽，及志工家長加油陪伴——是孩子們向前跑動的力量。

一鐵已落幕，距離畢業還有兩鐵要繼續挑戰。
