Purple Whirlwind: Eco-Drumming Band Opens the Changhua Marathon Carnival

大莊紫色旋風 · 用熱情活力為馬拉松嘉年華開場

June 1, 2026 · 115/6/1

English

On June 1, 2026, the children of Dajuang Elementary's Eco-Drumming Band took the stage as shining stars, opening the 2026 Changhua Marathon Carnival press conference with confident smiles, tight rhythm, and boundless energy.

Every drumstick swing, every move on stage was the fruit of long, dedicated practice. The County Commissioner and honored guests gave the children warm encouragement and recognition. Thanks to the Changhua County Government and the Department of Education for giving our students the chance to perform on a bigger stage and show the world what they can do.

Heartfelt thanks to percussion instructors Director Hsieh Hong-Long and Teacher Kuo Yi-Hsin for years of devoted coaching; to Manager Chien-Li, the band's best "agent," who handled every detail with care; to school team members Min-Hsien, Yu-Chieh, and Chi-Wen for their strong support; and to Parents' Association President Li-Min for accompanying the team throughout the event.

After the performance, the drumming kids happily posed for a photo with the County Commissioner and Mr. Cheng Tsung-Cheng, founder of the Tianzhong Marathon.

中文

115 年 6 月 1 日，大莊環保打擊樂的孩子們化身閃亮的主角，用自信笑容、整齊節奏與滿滿活力，為2026 彰化馬拉松嘉年華暖身開幕。

舞台上的每一次揮棒、每一個動作，都是努力練習的成果。感謝縣長及與會貴賓給孩子們滿滿的肯定與鼓勵，感謝縣府與教育處讓孩子有機會站上更大的舞台，勇敢展現自己。

感謝打擊樂謝鴻隆主任、郭奕欣老師長年的指導，最佳經紀人芊秝主任細心提醒每個細節，學校團隊旻仙、羽捷、啟文給力支援，家長會麗敏會長全程陪同。

打擊樂孩子們開心和縣長及田中馬創辦人鄭宗政執行長合影。
