One school year, seven big celebrations

一個學年裡，大莊孩子參與的七個大型節慶活動

At Dajuang, festivals are never just decoration — every one of them is a curriculum unit. Students don't just eat tangyuan on Winter Solstice; they learn the Five Elements. They don't just celebrate Children's Day; they earn 3Q Awards. Cooking, learning, gratitude, safety — all woven into the rhythm of the year.
大莊的節慶從來不只是裝飾——每一個節日都是一個課程單元。冬至不只是吃湯圓，是學「五行」；兒童節不只是慶祝，是「3Q 小超人」表揚；爌窯不只是烤雞，是「防災闖關」。

January · 五屆傳統 · 2026/1/12

Earth Oven & Safety Day

開春爌窯 × 防災闖關
"Others take tests — we roast chickens!"
「別人考試，我們烤雞！」

Now in its 5th year, this signature day combines what Dazhuang Village does best: building earth ovens, roasting chickens, and learning disaster safety. Students stack and seal the dirt ovens, tend the fire, and play disaster-safety puzzle and archery games while the food cooks. Even Vice Executive Wu of the Executive Yuan came in 2026.

第 5 年的「開春爌窯」，孩子們發揮農村優勢，學會堆窯、燒窯、燜窯、開窯。同時設「防災闖關」——積木拼圖、射箭比賽、防災知識搶答。行政院中辦副執行長吳音寧曾蒞臨參與拔河與防災對抗賽。
🥚 雞蛋爌窯 🧩 防災闖關 🏹 射箭 👨‍👩‍👧 親子共參
🎤
January · 首屆 · 2026/1/19

Dajuang's Got Talent

首屆「大莊好聲音」歌唱比賽
"Seeing children shine on stage is the best music in our school."
「看著孩子們在舞台上閃閃發亮，就是校園裡最動聽的聲音！」

The first-ever school singing contest — designed less as a competition and more as a stage where every child gets a moment to stand up and be brave. Professional judges gave warm, constructive feedback. Top performers moved to a second round.

2026 年 1 月舉辦的首屆「大莊好聲音」——重點不在比賽，而在給每個孩子一個「勇敢上台」的舞台。評審老師提供溫馨講評，前段選手進入第二關淘汰賽。
🎵 全校舞台 🌟 累積自信 👨‍🏫 專業講評
🎉
April · 兒童節 · 2025/4/2

3Q Superstars on Children's Day

兒童節 · 3Q 小超人表揚
Beyond IQ — celebrating AQ, EQ, and MQ.
不只是 IQ — 大莊頒發 AQ、EQ、MQ「3Q 小超人」獎。

Most schools celebrate Children's Day with games. Dajuang adds a serious twist — 3Q Superstar Awards recognising students who excel in:

💪 AQ Adversity 逆境 ❤️ EQ Emotional 情緒 🙏 MQ Moral 道德
大部分學校用遊戲慶兒童節，大莊多了一層意涵——「3Q 小超人」表揚。AQ（逆境商數）：在困難中振作；EQ（情緒商數）：理解管理情緒；MQ（道德商數）：良好品德與善良。
🍡
December · 冬至

Five-Element Tangyuan

冬至五行湯圓 · 把防災揉進麵團
Where else does dumpling-making teach about disaster preparedness?
把「防災」揉進湯圓裡——大莊獨有的冬至。

Volunteer moms lead students in making colourful tangyuan — but each colour represents one of the Chinese Five Elements, and the round shape symbolises community togetherness in emergencies:

🍐Metal芭樂 · 金
🥬Wood菠菜 · 木
⚫Water黑米 · 水
🐲Fire火龍果 · 火
🍚Earth白米 · 土
志工媽媽帶領製作「五行湯圓」——芭樂金、菠菜木、黑紫米水、火龍果火、白米土。學生在揉麵團時學習五行，圓滿形狀則象徵災害來臨時的社區凝聚力。
💐
September · 教師節

Thanking the Teachers

教師節感恩
A handmade card. A few words from the heart. A schoolwide thank you.
一張卡片、一句真心話、一場全校感恩。

Each class makes handmade thank-you cards; the Parents' Association presents gift vouchers on behalf of all families. The warmth of the day lingers into the rest of the academic year.

9 月教師節，各班學生親手製作感謝卡並寫下心意，家長會代表家長贈送禮券給老師。整場活動氣氛簡單溫馨，把感謝與尊敬具體化。
✉️ 手作感謝卡 🎁 家長會代表致謝
🎤
November · 親職教育

Cai-Jie Dad Parent Talk

「蔡傑爸」親職講座
"Education that touches lives with lives."
「用生命影響生命的教育。」

The well-known parent speaker "Cai-Jie Dad" visited Dajuang in November to share his own life stories — funny, warm, and deeply touching. The full house of parents went home with new ways of thinking about their role at home.

知名講師「蔡傑爸」蒞校演講，主題「用生命影響生命的教育」。他以幽默又溫暖的方式，分享如何透過以身作則陪伴孩子成長。家長們深受感動，重新思考自身的家庭角色。
👨‍👩‍👦 家長參與 💬 親職交流
👵
December · 敬老活動

Respect for Elders

敬老感恩 · 與華山基金會合作
Wearing the suit of an elder — to truly feel what care means.
穿上「老人裝」體驗——才真正懂得「敬老」是怎麼一回事。

In partnership with the Huashan Foundation, students try simulators that recreate what aging feels like — heavier limbs, blurred vision, weaker grip. The empathy lesson then flows out of school and into how students treat their own grandparents and neighbours.

與華山基金會合作，學生親身體驗「長大後」的感受——四肢沉重、視覺模糊、握力減弱。這份同理心從校園延伸到家庭與社區，讓「敬老」不只是口號。
🤝 華山基金會 💞 生命教育 🏘️ 社區延伸
