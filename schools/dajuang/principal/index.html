II.

Four-Sense Education

四感教育 · 辦學願景藍圖

Principal Lyu's vision centres on four Chinese verbs, each beginning with a different "感" (sense/feeling) family word — move, push, follow, inspire. The four senses run through every Dajuang lesson, every staff meeting, and every conversation with a parent.
呂校長的辦學願景由四個動詞構成「四感教育」——感動、推動、跟進、激勵，貫穿在大莊國小的日常。

SENSE 01
💛
Move
感動親師生熱情
A school is its people. Before any curriculum, the principal asks: "Are the teachers, students, and parents emotionally with us?" If not, the curriculum doesn't matter.
SENSE 02
📚
Push
推動課程多元發展
Disaster prevention. Agri-food. Eco-drumming. Karate. Bilingual science camps. AI smart sports. A small rural school must dare to teach more, not less.
SENSE 03
🌐
Follow
跟進教育趨勢銜接
From UN SDGs to AI jump-rope leagues, from cloud platforms to online matches with Japan and Thailand — rural schools have to lead, not lag.
SENSE 04
⭐
Inspire
激勵學生未來夢想
Every child looks at the rice fields and the Zhuoshui River and should believe: "I can grow as big as the world out there." The principal's last job is to keep that flame.
