I.

About Dajuang

關於大莊 · 一所立於濁水溪畔的特色防災校園
Where rice, fruit, and joy grow children with humble hearts, passionate minds, and optimistic spirits.
稻、果、樂——三種在地農作，三種人格特質，一所濁水溪畔的小學校。

Welcome to Dajuang Elementary School, a small rural school of about 70 students on the north bank of the Zhuoshui River. Founded in 1955, our school has grown alongside the farming community of Dazhuang Village — a place where rice paddies, dragon fruit fields, and guava orchards shape both the landscape and the curriculum.

大莊國小是一所位於彰化縣溪州鄉、濁水溪北岸的偏鄉小學，全校約 70 名學生。1955 年創校至今 70 年，與大庄村的農作社區一起成長——稻田、火龍果園、芭樂果園，既是這裡的風景，也是學校課程的基底。

We are best known across Taiwan for our place-based disaster prevention curriculum: "SEA Explore Rice-Fruit-Joy" — a four-year MOE-recognized program that turns the school's location (river hazards, an artillery-site relic, an earthquake fault) into hands-on safety lessons. Our Eco-Drumming Band, founded in 2003, makes music from buckets, trash cans, and recycled bins — and tours from Changhua to Taipei.

我們最為人所知的是「SEA 遊稻果樂」特色防災課程——把學校所在地的水患、砲陣遺址、彰化斷層全部轉化為孩子的安全教育，連續 4 年獲教育部肯定。2003 年成立的環保打擊樂團，用鐵桶、廚餘桶、塑膠桶演奏出最具創意的音樂。

"Living is preparing; preparing is living."
「生活即防災，防災也是生活的一部分。」
—— 大莊國小防災課程的核心信念
II.

Four-Sense Education

四感教育 · 呂彥億校長的辦學藍圖

Principal Lyu Yan-yi's leadership rests on four verbs — four "senses" that shape every Dajuang lesson, every event, and every conversation with parents.

呂彥億校長的辦學願景濃縮為四個動詞、四種「感」——感動、推動、跟進、激勵，貫穿在大莊國小的每一堂課、每一場活動、每一次與家長的對話。

SENSE 01
💛
Move with Passion
感動親師生熱情
A school is its people. We start with the question: how do we keep teachers, students, and parents excited to walk in the gate each morning?
SENSE 02
📚
Push the Curriculum
推動課程多元發展
Disaster prevention, agri-food, eco-drumming, karate, bilingual science camps — we keep asking: what else could a 70-student school dare to teach?
SENSE 03
🌐
Stay Current
跟進教育趨勢銜接
From AI smart jump ropes to UN SDGs, from EduSports cloud platforms to AI smart skipping leagues — rural schools have to lead, not follow.
SENSE 04
⭐
Inspire Their Dreams
激勵學生未來夢想
Every child should look out at the rice fields and the river and believe: I can grow as big as the world out there.
"Small daily efforts pile up into mountains."
「每天微小的努力都能積沙成塔，孩子由此建立自我價值與成就感。」

IV.

Featured Programs

特色課程與社團
🌾
Rice-Fruit-Joy Culture
稻果樂文化 · 三種人格的養成
Rice = humble. Dragon fruit = passionate. Guava = optimistic. The local farming triangle (20% rice / 10% dragon fruit / 70% guava) shapes the three personalities we hope every child grows into.
大庄社區農作比例：稻米 2 成、火龍果 1 成、芭樂 7 成。三項農作對應三種人格——謙、熱、樂，是大莊學子的學習圖像。
🥁
Eco-Drumming Band
環保打擊樂團 · 自 2003
Since 2003, our band has made music from metal buckets, trash cans, food-waste bins, and plastic barrels. We've played at the Changhua County Government, the Sijhou Farmers' Association, basketball tournaments — even toured Singapore (2011).
2003 年成立，用鐵桶、垃圾桶、廚餘桶、塑膠桶演奏。受邀至彰化縣府、溪州鄉農會、桃園科技公司、新加坡文化交流（2011）。
🎵 Touring since 2003
🥋
Karate at Dajuang
空手道 · 一半學生都在練
38 of our 70 students train in karate — coached free of charge by national-team-level instructors Chen Fu-hsing and Yen Shih-chin. Multi-year national Formosa Cup team champions, with two students winning the Presidential Education Award.
全校 38 / 70 人練空手道——由國手級陳福興、顏士欽教練義務指導，連年全國福爾摩沙盃團體冠軍，培養出彭琪鈞、彭詩荃兩位總統教育獎得主。
🏆 National team champions, multiple years
🌍
SDGs Children's Magazine
SDGs 兒童刊物《小永報》
Our own student-edited bilingual magazine — connecting UN Sustainable Development Goals to the everyday life of a rural elementary school. Because if 10-year-olds can think globally, anyone can.
學生自編的雙語校刊《小永報》——把聯合國永續發展目標（SDGs）與偏鄉小學的日常生活連結起來。
📡
EduSports Smart Alliance
EduSports 智慧體育策略聯盟（2026 起）
In March 2026, Dajuang joined 6 other schools in Taiwan's first AI-powered sports league — using smart jump ropes and a cloud platform. In 2027, our students will compete online with schools in Japan, Thailand, and Malaysia.
2026 年 3 月，與全臺 7 所學校共組「EduSports 智慧體育策略聯盟」，使用 AI 跳繩與雲端平台。2027 年起將與日本、泰國、馬來西亞的學校線上競賽。
🆕 New: rural to global
"From local roots to global reach."
「從在地出發，與國際接軌。」
—— 大莊國小 2026 的辦學主軸
V.

Our Story

校史時間軸 · 從成功國小分校到特色防災校園
1955
Founded as a temporary branch of Chenggong Elementary
民國 44 年 8 月 1 日，創校為「成功國民學校大莊臨時分校」。
1960
Established as an independent school under principal Chen Neng-tong
民國 49 年獨立設校為「大莊國民學校」，首任校長陳能通。
1968
Renamed Dajuang Elementary with the launch of 9-year compulsory education
民國 57 年配合九年國教改稱「大莊國民小學」，沿用至今。
2003
Eco-Drumming Band founded
環保打擊樂團成立，2004 年於花博會、西螺文藝季、縣府跨年晚會演出。
2012
MOE National Reading Promotion Award
榮獲教育部磐石閱讀推手獎。
2017
English Digital Classroom & Books-Bless Reading Room completed
「英語數位教室」（教育部補助 120 萬）+「書福閱讀圖書室」（教育部補助 200 萬）完工。
2018
Principal Lyu Yan-yi takes office (15th principal)
民國 107 年 7 月，呂彥億校長接任第 15 任校長。特教背景、曾任縣府教育處課程督學。
2021
MOE Foundational Disaster Prevention Campus · Distinguished
民國 110 年，獲教育部第 10 屆基礎防災校園建置「績優學校」。
2024
MOE Advanced Outreach Award (4th consecutive year)
民國 113 年，獲教育部進階防災校園推廣案「績優學校」，連續 4 年榮獲教育部肯定。
2026
EduSports Smart Alliance launched · partnered with 6 schools
民國 115 年 3 月，加入「智慧躍動・健康領航」EduSports 智慧體育策略聯盟，2027 年起與日、泰、馬國際對賽。
