I.

Classroom English

課室英語 · 給老師的口袋句庫（影片版）
The phrases teachers say between the lessons — taught on video.
老師在課堂裡每天會用上的英文句子——這次有人示範給你看。

This is a practical video library covering the everyday English a Taiwanese teacher actually uses in the classroom: greetings and roll call, pre-class set-up, instructions and explanations, praise and correction, classroom management, lesson activities, assigning homework, wrapping up, and exam directions. Each video isolates one situation so the phrases stick on first hearing.

這是一個實用的影片教材庫，涵蓋臺灣老師每天會用到的課堂英語：問候點名、課前準備、講解、回饋讚美、糾正、教室管理、課堂活動、分派作業、課堂收尾，到考試指令。每支影片只處理一個情境，第一次聽就能記住。

Why It Matters · 為什麼重要

A teacher who can say it in English
turns the whole classroom bilingual. 會用英文說的老師，整堂課就是雙語的。

The strongest predictor of bilingual fluency in a Taiwanese school is not curriculum — it's whether teachers feel confident saying simple things in English without rehearsal. This series exists to give them exactly that confidence, one classroom phrase at a time.
