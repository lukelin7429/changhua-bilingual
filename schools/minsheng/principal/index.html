The Twelfth Hand at the Gate 第十二任校長，走進百年校史

When Principal Tseng Chun-yao walked through Minsheng's gate in August 2024, he became the twelfth person to take that walk — the twelfth hand on the keys of a school first opened, in 1916, inside the courtyard of the Changhua Confucius Temple. He came to us from Macuo Elementary in Sihhu Township, the school he led before this one.

Eleven principals had walked here before him — from Cheng Mao-lin, who took office four months after Taiwan's retrocession in December 1945, to Hong Rong-yan, his immediate predecessor. The leadership ledger of Minsheng is not a list of names; it is a chain of hands, each handing the school on a little steadier than they found it.

2024 年 8 月，曾俊堯校長走進民生國小校門，成為這所百年小學中的第十二任校長——也就是從 1916 年在彰化孔廟廟埕開學以來，第十二雙握過民生鑰匙的手。他從溪湖鎮媽厝國小調任而來，那是他在民生之前所服務的學校。

在他之前，有十一位校長走過這條路——從 1945 年戰後光復後四個月就到任的首任校長鄭茂淋，到他的前任洪榮炎校長。民生的歷任校長名單不只是一張名單，而是一條手與手相接的鎖鏈，每一雙手都把學校交到下一雙手裡，比接過時，再穩一些。

Five Quiet Commitments 五大重點工作

The five working commitments Principal Tseng has set for Minsheng's next chapter.

曾校長為民生下一個篇章設下的五個工作承諾。

合

Team, Family, Community 活化團隊合作

Bring the school, the parents, and the surrounding community into one steady conversation. A school that talks well with the families around it is a school where every child has more than one safe pair of hands.

把學校、家長與社區之間的對話打通——讓校園、教職、家長都在同一條溝通的渠道上。一所與家庭社區好好對話的學校，每個孩子身邊就不只有一雙安全的手。

深

Deepen the Lessons 深化課程教學

Anchor every subject in the principle of "close to nature, in love with learning." International education, IT, reading, P.E., and character: not five competing programs, but five threads in one curriculum.

以「親近自然 · 學習 · 愛」為核心錨點——讓國際教育、資訊科技、閱讀、體育、品格成為同一套課程裡的五條線，而不是五個彼此競爭的計畫。

境

The Space Around the Lesson 美化環境空間

Shape the campus into a learning environment that is local, ecological, and curricular. A school that the community is in, and a community that the school is in — two sides of the same daily walk.

把校園打造成一個「在地、生態、課程化」的學習場域。讓「學校就在社區裡，社區也在學校裡」這件事，不是口號，而是每天走在校園時自然發生的事。

精

Teachers Who Keep Growing 精緻專業精進

Build a school where teachers attend training, share what they learn, and leave a trail of resources behind them for the next colleague to pick up. Professional growth is not extra-curricular; it is the curriculum the teacher takes home each evening.

讓老師參加進修、彼此分享，並把資源留下來給下一位接手的同事。專業成長不是「課外的事」——而是老師每天晚上帶回家的那份功課。

融

Many Hands, One Child 整合多元資源

Pull together people, materials, environment, and community resources around the child — not around the office that happens to control them. The point isn't an integrated budget; the point is whether the student gets the right help, from anyone in the school, on the day they need it.

把人力、物力、環境與社區資源，圍繞著「這個孩子」拉攏在一起——而不是圍著「剛好管這筆預算的處室」打轉。整合的不是經費，而是「孩子在需要的那一天，學校裡有誰能對的時候出手」。

Come Visit 歡迎蒞校交流

Educators, parents, and friends from Taiwan and abroad are warmly welcome to visit Minsheng. We are especially keen to talk with colleagues working on bilingual education in mid-to-large urban schools, on integrating gifted-class and music-class programs into a regular campus, and on what it means to lead a 110-year school into its next century.

歡迎國內外教育工作者、家長與朋友蒞校交流。我們特別樂於與致力於「都會型大校的雙語教育」、「資優班與音樂班如何融入普通校園日常」、以及「百年校史進入下一個世紀，校長該怎麼帶」的同路人對話。
