The Twelfth Hand at the Gate 第十二任校長，走進百年校史

When Principal Tseng Chun-yao walked through Minsheng's gate in August 2024, he became the twelfth person to take that walk — the twelfth hand on the keys of a school first opened, in 1916, inside the courtyard of the Changhua Confucius Temple. He came to us from Macuo Elementary in Sihhu Township, the school he led before this one.

Eleven principals had walked here before him — from Cheng Mao-lin, who took office four months after Taiwan's retrocession in December 1945, to Hong Rong-yan, his immediate predecessor. The leadership ledger of Minsheng is not a list of names; it is a chain of hands, each handing the school on a little steadier than they found it.

2024 年 8 月，曾俊堯校長走進民生國小校門，成為這所百年小學中的第十二任校長——也就是從 1916 年在彰化孔廟廟埕開學以來，第十二雙握過民生鑰匙的手。他從溪湖鎮媽厝國小調任而來，那是他在民生之前所服務的學校。

在他之前，有十一位校長走過這條路。民生的歷任校長名單不只是一張名單，而是一條手與手相接的鎖鏈，每一雙手都把學校交到下一雙手裡，比接過時，再穩一些。

Four Verbs at the Heart 教育信念 · 四個核心價值

Love makes education move. Living gives it meaning. Leading gives it direction. Learning keeps it alive.

愛是教育的原動力，樂活賦予教育意義，引導給予教育方向，學習讓教育持續活著。

To Love
關愛
Love is education's driving force.

To Live

Education is living; living is education.
教育即生活、生活即教育。
To Lead
引導
Not to instill — to guide.
教育非灌輸，而是引導，引導孩子發光發熱。

點燃學習熱情，淬鍊學習毅力，享受學習樂趣。

Five Quiet Commitments 五大重點工作 · 深耕校園

The five working commitments Principal Tseng has set for Minsheng's next chapter.

曾校長為民生下一個篇章設下的五個工作承諾，啟承彰化縣「基礎扎根 · 創新突破 · 多元展能」三大教育願景。

🤝

Team, Family, Community 活化團隊合作

School, parents, and community as one body: a healthy PTA, devoted parent volunteers, and unobstructed channels of communication. The school is a home, and the home raises every child.

學校、家長、社區緊密結合、共生共榮——家長會組織健全、家長志工挹注、教職員工溝通暢通。學校如家，親師合作，社區協力，共同營造愛與關懷的學習情境，守護莘莘學子。

📚

Deepen the Lessons 深化課程教學

Anchored in Close to nature · Learning · Love, with three threads — environment, joyful learning, character — woven through international education, IT, reading, P.E., and the arts.

以「親近自然 · 學習 · 愛」為核心，透過「守護環境熱愛生命」、「樂在學習多元展能」、「優質品格感恩關懷」三大主軸，扎根基本學力，推動國際教育、資訊科技、閱讀、體育、品格、藝文與多元社團。

🏡

The Space Around the Lesson 美化環境空間

Shape the campus into a learning environment that is local, ecological, and curricular. The school is in the community; the community is in the school.

活化校園空間，以「在地化、生態化、課程化」為主題，以孩子學習為起始點，建構「學校社區化、社區學校化」的學習場域。打造一所用愛與關懷守護孩子的校園環境。

🌱

Teachers Who Keep Growing 精緻專業精進

Teachers keep attending training, sharing what they've learned, and leaving a trail of resources behind them for the next colleague to pick up.

教師團隊積極參與進修，透過教學經驗分享交流，在教學之路上互相學習，建立教學資源分享平台，為孩子持續追求專業上的精進發展。

🧩

Many Hands, One Child 整合多元資源

Pull together people, materials, environment, and community resources around the child. Parents, teachers, and students build the SMART school together.

整合人力、物力、環境、專案計畫、社區資源，拓展資源網絡，聚焦孩子學習需求。親師生共同營造兼具自然之美與人文典範的「SMART」學校。

Come Visit 歡迎蒞校交流

Educators, parents, and friends from Taiwan and abroad are warmly welcome to visit Minsheng. We are especially keen to talk with colleagues working on bilingual education in mid-to-large urban schools, on integrating gifted-class and music-class programs into a regular campus, and on what it means to lead a 110-year school into its next century.

歡迎國內外教育工作者、家長與朋友蒞校交流。我們特別樂於與致力於「都會型大校的雙語教育」、「資優班與音樂班如何融入普通校園日常」、以及「百年校史進入下一個世紀，校長該怎麼帶」的同路人對話。
