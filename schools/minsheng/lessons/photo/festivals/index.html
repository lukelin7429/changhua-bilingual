Story 01 · Western Festival

Halloween

萬聖節

Halloween is a major global holiday. These young pupils enjoyed the Halloween theme by listening to scary stories about famous monsters. They also had lots of fun dressing up in costumes and participating in classroom activities.

萬聖節是一個全球性的重大節日。這些年輕的學生透過聆聽關於著名怪物的恐怖故事，享受了萬聖節的主題。他們還穿上了各種服裝，參加了教室裡的活動，玩得非常開心。

Story 02 · Voice & Body

Chinese Storytelling Competition

中文說故事比賽

Students work very hard to perform well in the Chinese storytelling competition. Through the use of vocal power and body language, the students practice their theatrical skills before a small audience. It's an exercise in confidence and pride.

這堂課專門用於中文說故事活動。透過聲音表達力和肢體語言，學生們在小型觀眾面前練習他們的戲劇技巧。這是一項培養自信和自豪感的練習。

Story 03 · Teacher's Day

Respect

尊敬 · 教師節

Students showed respect by joining the Teacher's Day ceremony. It is important to show respect to teachers on Teacher's Day. Pulling out the "wisdom pens" is a way to show respect for teachers.

學生們通過參加教師節典禮來表達尊敬。在教師節向老師表達敬意是非常重要的。拔出「智慧筆」是表達對老師尊重的一種方式。

Story 04 · Wisdom Pen Ritual

Wisdom

智慧 · 智慧筆儀式

The students pulled wisdom pens to gain wisdom. In the past, people pulled hair from a cow to gain wisdom. Today, students hope for wisdom by participating in the ceremony.

學生們拔智慧筆來獲取智慧。過去，人們會拔牛毛來獲得智慧。今天，學生們透過參與這個儀式來祈求智慧。
