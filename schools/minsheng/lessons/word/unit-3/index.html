Word 01
Easter egg (n)
復活節彩蛋
Easter eggs are often dyed in bright colors to represent new life.
復活節彩蛋通常被染成鮮豔的顏色，代表著新的生命。
Some students enjoy decorating Easter eggs with complex designs or patterns.
有些學生喜歡用複雜的設計或圖案裝飾復活節彩蛋。
Word 02
spring couplet (n)
春聯
The man writes a spring couplet in black ink.
這個先生用黑墨汁寫春聯。
The spring couplet has wishes for good fortune.
春聯上有吉祥話。
Word 03
rhythm band (n)
節奏樂隊
The students are participating in rhythm band practice, playing instruments like pianos and xylophones.
學生們正在參加節奏樂隊練習，演奏鋼琴和木琴等樂器。
During rhythm band practice, they learn to synchronize and maintain rhythm, enhancing their musical talent.
在節奏樂團練習中，他們學習同步和保持節奏，提高他們的音樂天賦。
Word 04
campfire (n)
營火
During winter camp, the students learn how to perform a fire dance before lighting the campfire.
冬令營期間，學生在點燃營火前學習如何表演火舞。
They take turns reciting a pledge and have fun around the campfire.
他們輪流宣讀誓言，在營火旁玩樂。
