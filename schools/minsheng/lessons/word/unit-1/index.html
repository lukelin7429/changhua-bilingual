Word of the Day · Unit 01

Sports & Movement

運動 · 動起來
⚽ 4 videos · 4 個單字 · ≈4 min total

Word 01
soccer (n)
足球
When you play soccer, you mainly use your feet to move the ball.
當你踢足球時，你主要是用雙腳來移動球。
Soccer players can dribble the ball, pass the ball to their teammates, or kick the ball toward the goal.
足球員可以運球、傳球或踢球攻門。
Word 02
basketball (n)
籃球
Basketball was invented in the US in 1891.
籃球於 1891 年在美國發明。
Now basketball is played by people all around the world!
現在全世界的人都會打籃球。
Word 03
frisbee (n)
飛盤
A frisbee is a disc that can glide in the air.
飛盤是可以在空中滑行的圓盤。
You can use a frisbee to play catch with your friends!
你可跟朋友玩接飛盤。
Word 04
warm up (n)
暖身
The students do warm-up exercises.
學生們進行熱身練習。
They do stretches for their warm-up.
他們做伸展運動來熱身。
