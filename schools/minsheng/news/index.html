English Readers' Theatre — County Winner

英語讀者劇場比賽榮獲優勝

Minsheng's English-Club team improved on last year's third place by taking second at this year's Changhua County Readers' Theatre Contest — a county-wide stage that the school itself helps run from the English Resource Center hosted on campus.

民生英語俱樂部代表隊在彰化縣英語讀者劇場比賽榮獲優勝（第 2 名），相較去年第 3 名再進一步——這個縣級比賽，正是由設於民生的英語資源中心承辦。

Taiwanese Speech Contest — Award Winners

臺灣台語演說比賽優勝

May 11, 2026 Confucius · 在地文化

Confucius Temple
Analects Recitation Exam

孔子廟論語經典會考

The Changhua Confucius Temple — the same building where Minsheng was founded in 1916 — hosts its annual Analects Recitation Exam. Minsheng students walk the few blocks over to take part. A 110-year loop comes around once more.

彰化孔子廟——也就是民生 1916 年創校時的代用校舍——舉辦每年一度的論語經典會考，民生學生步行幾條街即可參加。110 年的迴圈，又走回了同一個院子。

Oct 31, 2024 Halloween · 萬聖節

Halloween Celebration
Brings Joy to Minsheng

萬聖節慶祝活動 · 為民生帶來歡樂 · 校史回顧

Music-class students greeted everyone with cheerful tunes as parents and pupils arrived. Children dressed as witches, superheroes, Pikachu, and Spider-Man filled the activity center. Foreign English teacher Mr. Jov taught Halloween words and led a sing-along; the school also taught students to make pumpkin baskets. Principal Tseng Chun-yao dressed as a superhero for the day. "The activities not only encourage creativity but also help students understand other cultures," he said.

音樂班學生以歡快樂音歡迎到校的孩子與家長，活動中心擠滿了女巫、超級英雄、皮卡丘、蜘蛛人。外籍英語老師 Jov 教萬聖節單字、帶大家唱萬聖節歌曲，學校也教孩子做南瓜籃。曾俊堯校長以超級英雄裝扮現身，他說：「這個活動不僅鼓勵創意，也幫助學生理解其他文化。」
