I.

About Yude

關於育德 · 一所逆勢生長的南境小校
A six-class rural school in Outer Bamboo Village — small in number, steady in growth, deep in roots.
坐落於二林鎮外竹里的六班小校——人數不多，逆勢成長，根扎得深。

Yude Elementary School sits in Outer Bamboo Village (外竹里), in the southern reach of Erlin Township — Taiwan's largest buckwheat-growing region. The school began as a branch of Zhongzheng Elementary in 1959, and became independent in August 1970. After more than half a century, eleven principals, and countless harvests, we remain a school that knows each child by name.

育德國小位於二林鎮外竹里——全台最大蕎麥產區的南端。1959 年從 中正國小外竹分班教室起步，1970 年 8 月正式獨立設校。半世紀、十一任校長、無數蕎麥收成之後，我們仍然是一所「每個孩子都叫得出名字」的小學校。

Our six classrooms hold around 60 children. Our teacher-to-student ratio is 1:4.8. And in a county where most rural schools shrink each year, Yude has grown 23.5 % in student numbers — a quiet vote of confidence from the families of Outer Bamboo and beyond.

全校 6 班、約 60 位孩子，師生比 1:4.8。在彰化縣多數偏鄉小校少子化壓力下，育德國小的學生數逆勢成長 23.5%——這是外竹里與鄰近家庭，對學校最安靜的肯定。

II.

Four School-Based Curricula

四大校本課程 · 育德辦學的四根柱子

Four signature curricula carry every Yude child through six years — one for the arts, one for the page, one for the village, one for the table.

我們以四大校本課程陪伴每一位育德的孩子走過六年——一個給藝術、一個給閱讀、一個給社區、一個給餐桌。

Soaring Arts
藝翔天開
A full-spectrum arts curriculum — ocarina, ukulele, keyboard, saxophone, drawing, calligraphy, dance — anchored by our saxophone ensemble and the biennial Soaring Arts Exhibition at the Erlin Township Library.

Yude Reading Time
育德愛閱

走讀外竹

III.

Soaring Arts

藝翔天開 · 育德的招牌課程
Signature Curriculum · 校本課程
A small school with a big sound.
一所迷你校，發出不迷你的聲音。

Few rural schools of six classes can field a saxophone ensemble. We can. Our students learn ocarina, ukulele, keyboard, saxophone, drawing, calligraphy, and dance — and every other year, their work fills the walls of the Erlin Township Library for the Soaring Arts Exhibition. 很少有 6 班規模的偏鄉小校能組起薩克斯風樂團——育德可以。孩子們從陶笛、烏克麗麗、鍵盤樂器、薩克斯風、繪畫、書法到舞蹈都學，每兩年一次的「藝翔天開」師生美展，更把孩子們的作品掛上二林鎮立圖書館的牆。

IV.

Programs & Moments

特色課程與校園時刻
📚
Reading on Wheels
行動圖書車 · 育德愛閱現場
When the Fo Guang Shan mobile library pulls into our courtyard, the schoolyard becomes a reading room. Children sit on stools beside the green truck, choosing books while spring leaves drift overhead — a small ritual, repeated year after year.
佛光山行動圖書車開進育德校園，操場就成了閱讀室。孩子們在綠色卡車旁坐成一排，挑書、看書、聊書——一個小小的儀式，年復一年地重複。
🥟
Winter Dumpling Festival
冬饗 · 育德餃者
Every winter, students, parents, and community-college partners gather in our hall to wrap dumplings together — part of the "Community College × 12-Year Basic Education" project. Children learn that food is also a way of belonging.
每年冬天，學生、家長與「社區大學連結十二年國教計畫」夥伴齊聚校內，一起包餃子——孩子們在這個冬日活動裡，學到食物也是一種「歸屬」的方式。
🤝 Community-College Partnership
🖼️
Soaring Arts Exhibition
藝翔天開美展 · 二林鎮立圖書館
Every other year, the Erlin Township Library hands its walls to Yude. Our students' paintings — bold characters, mythic deer, dragon girls, harvest grapes — fill the public space and make the village look again at what its children can do.
每兩年一次，二林鎮立圖書館把整面牆讓給育德——孩子們筆下的大字、傳說中的鹿、龍少女、葡萄豐收……佔滿公共空間，讓整個社區重新看見「我們的孩子能做什麼」。
🎨 5th Biennial Exhibition
🎺
Music Across the Day
音樂課 × 才藝課程
Keyboard, drum, saxophone — and traditional ensemble work too. With only 60 children, every student gets enough time on the instrument and enough attention from the teacher to actually progress.
鍵盤、鼓、薩克斯風，加上傳統樂團——因為全校只有 60 個孩子，每個學生都有足夠的時間摸到樂器、足夠的師資注意力，才真的能進步。
Meet Our Resident Foreign Teacher

Teacher Amanda

Amanda Kalinowski · 柯晴曦 · 育德國小駐校外師
"Drama is how I teach English — because both ask children to step forward, listen, and speak as themselves."

Amanda joined Yude in September 2024, bringing a theatre-trained ear and a culture-first classroom. A graduate of East Stroudsburg University in Pennsylvania with a Bachelor's in Drama, and certified in both TEFL and TESOL, she teaches English through story, performance, and the small daily conversations that make English feel like a real way to be in the world.

Amanda 老師 2024 年 9 月加入育德——她有戲劇背景的耳朵，也有以文化為核心的課堂。畢業於美國賓州 East Stroudsburg University 戲劇學系，並持有 TEFL + TESOL 英語教學認證。她用故事、表演、與每日的小對話來教英文——讓英語不只是課本，而是孩子真正可以「在世界裡活」的方式。

Her classroom rests on four small commitments: build positive relationships, keep the door open, hold student safety and trust above all, and let children lead. Her signature methods — giant puzzle games, class mascots, drama performances — turn a 60-student school into a stage where every voice can be heard.

她的課堂建立在四個簡單的承諾上：建立正向師生關係、開放政策、學生安全與信任、學生主動性。她最招牌的教法——巨型拼圖遊戲、班級吉祥物制度、戲劇表演——讓 60 個孩子的迷你校，變成每個聲音都被聽見的舞台。

🎓 ESU Drama, BA 📜 TEFL · TESOL 🇺🇸 Pennsylvania, USA 📅 At Yude since Sept 2024

Our Story

Zhongzheng Elementary opens a branch classroom in Outer Bamboo
民國 48 年，中正國民學校於外竹里成立「外竹分班教室」——育德的起點。

Upgraded to a full branch class
民國 50 年，升格為「外竹分班」。
1962
Upgraded again — to a branch campus
民國 51 年，升格為「外竹分校」。
1970
Independent school founded — Yude Elementary
民國 59 年 8 月，外竹分校正式獨立設校，定名「育德國民小學」（創校元年）。首任校長：謝登。

Principal Hong Dou begins the longest tenure (10.5 years)
民國 75 年，洪斗校長（第五任）就任，任期長達 10 年半，是 11 任中最長者。
