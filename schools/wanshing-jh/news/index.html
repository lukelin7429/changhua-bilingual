National Skills · 全國技能競賽

National Gold — Painting & Decoration第 52 屆全國技能競賽 · 漆作裝潢 全國第一

2025 school year · 114 學年度

A Wanshing alumna from the Class of 2025 went straight from our school gate into the Interior Spatial Design programme at National Erlin Industrial & Commercial Vocational High School. Three months of mentored training later, she stood at the 52nd National Skills Championships in Painting & Decoration — and brought home first place, nationally. The pipeline from Wanshing's after-school workshop, through Erlin Vocational's master classroom, to the national workbench is no longer a hopeful experiment. It is repeatable.

本校 2025 屆畢業校友，從萬興校門直接進入國立二林高級工商職業學校室內空間設計科。經過三個月的師徒訓練，於第 52 屆全國技能競賽漆作裝潢項目奪下全國第一。從萬興課後工坊、二林工商教練場、到全國工作台——這條路線在萬興已經不是希望，而是可被複製的常態。

Source: School honor roll · Wanshing announcements · UDN news

National Honor · 全國榮譽

National Outstanding Vocational Arts Student113 學年度全國技藝教育績優人員

2024-2025 school year · 113 學年度

A current Wanshing ninth-grader was named a National Outstanding Vocational Arts Student for the 113 school year — one of the highest individual recognitions a vocational arts student can receive while still in junior high. In the same year, she also placed 4th in the county Computer Graphics Design contest, in the Design Cluster of the technical-arts competition.

本校 901 同學獲頒113 學年度全國技藝教育績優人員——這是國中階段技藝教育學生可獲得的最高個人榮譽之一。同一學年，她也在彰化縣技藝教育競賽設計職群電腦繪圖組奪下第 4 名。

Source: 萬興國中榮譽榜 · whjh.chc.edu.tw/posts

Languages · 縣賽語文

County 3rd in Mandarin Recitation彰化縣 114 年度語文競賽 · 國語朗讀組

2025-2026 school year · 114 學年度

A Wanshing eighth-grader carried the school to 3rd place in Mandarin Recitation at the county language preliminaries, and then 5th place in the finals. In a school of 119 students, the language stage and the workbench stage are equally part of who we are.

本校 802 同學，於彰化縣 114 年度語文競賽國語朗讀組初賽榮獲第 3 名、複賽第 5 名——在這所 119 位學生的學校，朗讀的舞台與工作台的舞台，同樣是我們是誰的一部分。

Performance · 校級代表隊

The Wanshing Taiwanese Drum Troupe萬興太鼓隊 · 校園文化舞台

Standing programme since 2016 · 2016 年成立至今

Founded around 2016, the Wanshing Taiwanese Drum Troupe is the school's most visible cultural ensemble. Each drum carries the school name on its face. The troupe performs at township ceremonies, county-level events, and the school's own open days — giving students whose strengths do not show up on a test paper a real stage to stand on.

2016 年成立至今，萬興太鼓隊是校內最易被認出的文化團隊。每一面鼓的鼓面都印著「萬興」——團隊在二林鎮、縣級活動、校園開放日固定演出。那些不會在考卷上被看到的孩子，這裡有一個屬於他們的舞台。

Source: School FB · @WANSHING1969

Community · 社區數位

Erlin Digital Opportunity Centre — on Campus二林數位機會中心 · 駐校 + 行動巡迴

Ongoing · 駐校多年

The Erlin Community Digital Opportunity Centre (DOC) is hosted on the Wanshing campus, guided by National Formosa University and the Changhua County Education Network Centre. Free workshops run for community elders, disadvantaged students, and parents who would otherwise have no access. Through Mobile DOC, the same workshops travel back on rotation to Wanshing Elementary and Hsinsheng Elementary.

「二林數位機會中心」駐校於萬興國中，由國立虎尾科技大學與彰化縣教育網路中心輔導，提供免費數位課程予社區長輩、弱勢學童、與無法到校的家長。透過行動 DOC，課程巡迴回到萬興國小與新生國小——讓二林鎮北部的數位落差，每年都被縮小一點。

Source: 教育部偏鄉數位培力推動計畫 · itaiwan.moe.gov.tw
