I.

About Wanshing

關於萬興國中 · 二林鎮北部的九里小校
A small junior high in the rice-field belt north of Erlin Town — where 119 students hold up a digital outreach centre, a national-gold vocational pipeline, and a working drum troupe at the same time.
立於二林鎮北部稻作腹地的偏鄉小校——119 位學生，同時撐起一座社區數位機會中心、一條全國金牌的技藝出口、一支真實上場的太鼓隊。

Welcome to Wanshing Junior High School, founded in 1969 in Wanshing Village, northern Erlin. Our catchment is nine villages in the upper half of the township — including Wanshing, Zhenxing, Yongxing, Zhaojia, and Hualun — and our main feeder elementary schools are Wanshing Elementary and Hsinsheng Elementary.

萬興國中創校於 1969 年，立於二林鎮北部的萬興里。學區涵蓋鎮北九個里——萬興、振興、永興、趙甲、華崙等——主要生源國小為萬興國小與新生國小。

We are not in the township centre. We are out in the rice and peanut fields where Taiwan Highway 17 turns east into smaller country roads. The school is officially classified as a remote rural school — and that is the truth of where our students arrive from each morning, by school bus, by scooter behind a parent, by their own bicycles through the paddies.

我們不在鎮中心。我們在台 17 線轉向產業道路的稻穀與花生田之間。教育部正式將本校列為「偏遠地區學校」——這就是我們的孩子每天清晨抵達校門的真實路徑：校車、爸媽機車後座、自己騎腳踏車穿過田埂。

What we are is a school that decided not to chase the same metrics as a city campus. Instead, we serve three audiences at the same campus gate — our 119 students, the community elders learning digital tools in our Digital Opportunity Centre, and the two feeder elementary schools we send mobile DOC workshops back to. That is unusual for a junior high. That is who Wanshing is.

我們選擇了不和都市校競同一份成績單。我們選擇了在同一座校門裡服務三個族群——校內 119 位學生、來上 DOC 數位課程的社區長輩、以及我們透過行動 DOC 巡迴回到的兩所生源國小。對一所國中而言，這並不尋常。這就是萬興。

"Brilliance Wanshing · Vital SMART."
「卓越萬興 · 活力 SMART」——萬興國中的課程願景，也是這所學校對每一位畢業生的承諾。
II.

Three Anchors

三大發展主軸 · 從社區、從雙手、從文化扎根

Wanshing's identity is built on three commitments that all start at the school gate and reach outward. None of them is borrowed from a city template.

萬興國中的身分認同建立在三條承諾上——三條都從校門口開始，都向外延伸到社區、產業、文化。沒有一條是抄自都市校的模板。

ANCHOR 01
💻
Community Digital Hub
社區數位樞紐 · 數位機會中心
The Erlin Community Digital Opportunity Centre (DOC) is based on our campus — guided by National Formosa University and the Changhua County Education Network Centre, funded jointly by central and county governments. Through our Mobile DOC, we carry the same workshops back to Wanshing Elementary and Hsinsheng Elementary, so rural elders, disadvantaged students, and parents with no transport can still touch a keyboard and a tablet on the same week.
ANCHOR 02
🔧
Skilled Hands
技藝出口 · 與二林工商接軌
Our ninth-graders who learn better through making — through computer graphics, painting and decoration, interior design — get a real path forward to National Erlin Industrial & Commercial Vocational High School. The pipeline is not theoretical. A Wanshing graduate from the Class of 2025 stood on the national workbench and brought home gold.
ANCHOR 03
🥁
Drums, Drills, and Drama
太鼓・運動・舞台——身體與文化並進
The same kid who hits a drum head in afternoon practice will run a county track meet in spring and stand on a recitation stage in autumn. The school's Taiwanese drum troupe, founded in 2016 alongside the school baseball team, has become one of the most recognisable images of Wanshing — and a counter-narrative to the idea that rural schools have to be quiet.
"Science · Multi-arts · Active · Real-world · Technology."
「S・M・A・R・T」——萬興國中課程願景的五個字母，五條我們答應自己每年都做的事。
III.

National Gold

全國第一 · 漆作裝潢金牌的萬興出口
52nd National Skills Championships · 全國技能競賽

A Wanshing graduate took home the gold.

一位萬興校友，把全國技能競賽漆作裝潢的金牌帶回了二林。

Most schools tell the story of their best students with a list of universities. Wanshing tells it with a workbench. Our Class-of-2025 alumna entered National Erlin Industrial & Commercial Vocational High School straight from our gate, joined their Interior Spatial Design programme, and three months later stood at the 52nd National Skills Championships in Painting & Decoration. She came home with first place — nationally. The pipeline from after-school workshop, through Wanshing's design and graphic-arts curriculum, to Erlin Vocational's master classrooms, to the national workbench — is no longer hopeful. It is repeatable. 多數學校用「考上哪所大學」說自己的好學生。萬興用「站上哪張工作台」說。我們 2025 年的畢業校友從萬興國中校門直接進入國立二林高級工商職業學校室內空間設計科，三個月後站上第 52 屆全國技能競賽漆作裝潢職場，奪下全國第一。從我們課後工坊、設計與電腦繪圖課程，到二林工商的師徒訓練，到全國工作台——這條路線在萬興已經不是希望，而是可被複製的常態。

🥇 1st Place · National · 全國第一
IV.

The SMART Vision

活力 SMART · 五個字母，五條課程承諾

學校的正式課程願景叫做「卓越萬興・活力 SMART」。五個字母，是我們對每一位萬興學生的課程承諾——課本之外、也課本之上。

V.

Featured Programs

特色課程與校園 · 三大主軸的具體實踐
💻
Erlin Digital Opportunity Centre
二林數位機會中心 · 駐校 + 行動巡迴
The Erlin DOC is based on the Wanshing campus, guided by National Formosa University and the Changhua County Education Network Centre. Free workshops run for community elders, disadvantaged students, and parents who would otherwise have no access to digital tools. Through our Mobile DOC, the same workshops travel back to Wanshing Elementary and Hsinsheng Elementary on rotation — so the gap between "digital-rich" and "digital-poor" within rural Erlin narrows year by year.
「二林數位機會中心」駐校於萬興，由國立虎尾科技大學與彰化縣教育網路中心輔導，向中央與縣府爭取經費。免費課程開給社區長輩、弱勢學童、無法到校的家長。透過行動 DOC，同一批課程巡迴回到萬興國小、新生國小——讓「二林鎮北部數位落差」這件事，每年都被縮小一點。
💻 教育部偏鄉數位培力推動計畫駐點
🥁
Taiwanese Drum Troupe
太鼓隊 · 鄉村小校最響亮的一支
Founded around 2016, the Wanshing Taiwanese Drum Troupe has become the school's most recognisable image. Each drum carries the school name on its face — and the troupe performs at township ceremonies, county-level events, and on the school's own open days. For students who do not see themselves on an academic stage, the drum platform is theirs.
2016 年成立的萬興太鼓隊，已經成為這所學校最容易被認出的形象。每一面鼓的鼓面都印著「萬興」二字——孩子在校園、在鄉里、在縣級活動上演出。那些不會在學科舞台上被看到的孩子，這裡有一個屬於他們的舞台。
🥁 校級代表隊・對外演出常態
🎓
Bai-Sha · Co-Prosperity Partnership
白沙×共好 · 與彰師大師培中心合作
In the 109 school year (2020-21), Wanshing partnered with the Teacher Education Centre of National Changhua University of Education on the "Bai-Sha · Co-Prosperity" programme. Pre-service teachers, in-service mentor teachers, and Wanshing's seventh, eighth, and ninth graders worked together on cross-disciplinary curriculum design — bringing university-grade pedagogy into a 6-class rural campus.
109 學年度（2020-21），萬興與國立彰化師範大學師資培育中心合作「白沙×共好」計畫——師資生、現職指導教師、與萬興國一二三的孩子共同進行跨領域課程設計。把大學等級的教學專業，直接帶進一所 6 班的偏鄉校園。
🤝 國立彰化師範大學師資培育中心合作校
📚
Wanshing Community Reading Pavilion
萬興書苑社區共讀站
The on-campus Wanshing Community Reading Pavilion (萬興書苑) is a public reading space — not just for students, but for the rural families across the nine villages. It pairs naturally with the DOC: one space teaches the keyboard, the next teaches the page. Together they form Wanshing's quiet community-education footprint.
校內設有「萬興書苑社區共讀站」——這是一個對外開放的閱讀空間，不只服務學生，也服務九個里的鄉親家庭。和 DOC 是天然搭檔——一個空間教鍵盤、一個空間教書頁。兩個一起構成萬興安靜的社區教育足跡。
📖 社區共讀站・對外開放
"Small school, broad doors — opening to the village, the workbench, and the drum."
小校大門——向九個里、向工作台、向太鼓鼓面，同時開啟。
