II.

A Vision for a Small Rural School

小校大願景 · 從一所偏鄉國中可以看見什麼

The first thing Principal Wu's office looks out at is not the city — it is the rice fields, the peanut rows, the canal water at the end of the Choshui irrigation network. A principal's job in this part of Changhua is unusual: he is at once the head of a small school, the host of a community digital opportunity centre that serves elders and disadvantaged students, and the gateway through which nine villages' children pass on their way to vocational high school, senior high, and beyond.

從吳校長辦公室望出去——不是城市，是稻田、花生田、濁水溪水利末端的圳溝水。在彰化縣這個地段擔任校長，職責並不單純：他既是一所小校的校務領導人、也是一座服務鄉里長輩與弱勢學童的數位機會中心的主辦者、更是九個里的孩子升學前的關鍵節點。

Under his stewardship, the campus continues three commitments that were already in place when he took the principal's chair, and adds a fourth that is uniquely his: holding the school steady. In a rural school system that often experiences principal turnover every few years, the operating value of "the principal stays" is incalculable. It is what allows the Erlin DOC to keep planning a multi-year curriculum. It is what allows the Taiwanese drum troupe and the school baseball team — both founded in 2016 under his predecessor — to keep being possible.

在吳校長任內，萬興繼續實踐三條他接任前就在進行的承諾，並加上一條他獨有的：把學校穩定地撐住。在台灣偏鄉學校體系裡，「校長 3-4 年一換」是常態——而「校長留得住」這件事的營運價值無法用數字衡量。這是二林 DOC 可以規劃多年期課程的原因；這是 2016 年由前任校長奠基的太鼓隊、青少棒隊，可以繼續存在的原因。

He inherited a school with a clear identity — and he keeps that identity working in the daily routine. The Erlin Digital Opportunity Centre runs on campus. The Bai-Sha · Co-Prosperity partnership with the Teacher Education Centre at National Changhua University of Education brings university-grade pedagogy in. The vocational arts pipeline to National Erlin Industrial & Commercial Vocational High School has just produced a national-gold-medal alumna in painting and decoration. Each of these is a quiet vote of confidence in the work happening here.

他接手了一所身分清晰的學校，並讓這份身分在每日校務中持續運作：二林數位機會中心駐校如常；與彰化師大師培中心的「白沙×共好」合作把大學級教學專業帶進校園；通往國立二林工商的技藝教育出口，剛剛在 2025 年產出了一位全國技能競賽金牌校友。每一件，都是對「萬興正在做的事」的安靜投票。

III.

SMART, Unpacked

SMART 五個字母 · 一條一條打開

The Wanshing curriculum vision uses an acronym that is also a value statement. Each letter names a commitment the school makes to every child who walks through the front gate — not as a slogan on a banner, but as a checklist for the school year.

萬興的課程願景是一個首字母縮寫，也是一份價值清單。五個字母，五條我們對每一位走進校門的孩子做出的承諾——不是橫幅口號，而是每學年的檢核清單。

S
01
Science
科學與探究

From the chemistry classroom to the school's STEM equipment grants, Wanshing positions science not as a single subject but as a habit of asking questions. Rural classrooms get the same lab time as the city campus next door — that is the equity floor we refuse to drop below.

M
02
Multi-Arts
多元智慧與技藝

A child who learns through hands, through rhythm, through movement deserves a stage. Wanshing's Taiwanese drum troupe, vocational arts pipeline, and fine-arts entries in county competitions make multi-arts a practical promise, not a Sunday hobby.

A
03
Active Learning
主動學習

"Active" is not extra-curricular — it is how the curriculum itself is delivered. Through the Bai-Sha · Co-Prosperity programme with NCUE, in-class teaching consciously moves towards inquiry, peer dialogue, and student-led projects across all three grade levels.

R
04
Real-World
跨領域實踐

A Wanshing student's nine villages, two feeder elementary schools, one community digital centre, and the rice fields outside the gate are the curriculum. We do not pretend students are anywhere else. We teach the real Erlin they live inside — and let it be their first laboratory.

T
05
Technology
科技與創新

The same campus that hosts the Erlin Digital Opportunity Centre for community elders is, by definition, a campus that takes technology seriously for its students. The same building, the same Wi-Fi, the same teachers — pass-through every day from senior visitors to seventh graders.

Curriculum Vision · 課程願景

Brilliance Wanshing · Vital SMART

卓越萬興 · 活力 SMART

Five letters, five promises, one rural campus — held in place by a principal whose job is, above all, to make sure the school keeps showing up for nine villages of children.

五個字母，五條承諾，一所偏鄉校——由一位校長持續守護：他最重要的工作，是讓這所學校，繼續為九個里的孩子，每天準時在這裡。
