Lunch & Food

午餐與食農的英文

Word 01
school lunch (n)
營養午餐
Every day, the cafeteria prepares delicious school lunch for all students.
每天，學校餐廳為所有學生準備美味的營養午餐。
School lunch in Taiwan usually includes rice, soup, vegetables, and meat.
台灣的營養午餐通常包含米飯、湯、蔬菜和肉。
Word 02
serve (v)
打菜
Students take turns to serve food to their classmates at lunch.
學生輪流在午餐時間為同學打菜。
She learned how to serve rice properly using a large ladle.
她學會了如何用大湯勺正確地打飯。
Word 03
utensils (n)
餐具
Please wash your utensils carefully after you finish eating lunch.
吃完午飯後，請仔細清洗你的餐具。
Our school provides utensils such as spoons, forks, and chopsticks.
我們學校提供湯匙、叉子和筷子等餐具。
Word 04
rocket stove (n)
火箭爐
We used a rocket stove to cook rice during the outdoor farm activity.
我們在戶外食農活動中使用火箭爐煮飯。
A rocket stove burns wood very efficiently to produce a strong, clean flame.
火箭爐非常有效率地燃燒木材，產生強力而乾淨的火焰。
Word 05
pizza (n)
披薩
The students made pizza with tomato sauce and cheese in cooking class.
學生在烹飪課上用番茄醬和起司做披薩。
Pizza is a popular food that originally comes from Italy.
披薩是一種源自義大利的流行食物。
