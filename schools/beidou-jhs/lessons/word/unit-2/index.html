Word 01
sweep (v)
打掃・掃地
Every day, students sweep the classroom floor after lunch.
每天，學生在午飯後打掃教室地板。
It is important to sweep away the fallen leaves in the school garden.
打掃學校花園裡的落葉是很重要的事。
Word 02
carry (v)
搬運・攜帶
Students carry their lunch trays carefully back to their seats.
學生們小心翼翼地把午餐托盤端回座位。
The teacher asked us to carry the boxes to the library.
老師叫我們把箱子搬到圖書館。
Word 03
mop (n)
拖把
She used a mop to clean the muddy hallway floor after it rained.
下雨後，她用拖把清理走廊上泥濘的地板。
The janitor uses a mop to keep the school hallways clean every morning.
工友每天早上用拖把保持學校走廊的乾淨。
Word 04
carport (n)
車棚
Students park their bicycles in the carport near the school entrance.
學生把腳踏車停在學校入口附近的車棚。
The school built a new carport to keep bicycles safe from the rain.
學校建了一個新車棚，讓腳踏車不被雨淋。
Word 05
wash your hands (v)
洗手
Always wash your hands with soap and water before you eat lunch.
在吃午飯前，一定要用肥皂和水洗手。
We should wash our hands often to prevent spreading germs.
我們應該常常洗手，以防止傳播病菌。
Word 06
take a nap (v)
午睡
After lunch, students take a nap for about twenty minutes.
午飯後，學生午睡大約二十分鐘。
Taking a nap in the afternoon helps you feel energized for afternoon classes.
午睡幫助你為下午的課程恢復精力。
Word 07
climbing trees (v)
爬樹
Some students enjoy climbing trees in the school garden during recess.
一些學生在下課時喜歡在學校花園爬樹。
Climbing trees helps children develop strength, balance, and confidence.
爬樹幫助孩子培養力量、平衡感和自信心。
