Word 01
woodball (n)
木球
Our school has a woodball team that practices every afternoon.
我們學校有一支木球隊，每天下午都練習。
Woodball is a traditional sport that is very popular in Taiwan.
木球是台灣非常流行的傳統運動。
Word 02
batting practice (n)
打擊練習
The baseball team does batting practice every morning before school.
棒球隊每天早上上學前都要做打擊練習。
Good batting practice helps players hit the ball farther and faster.
良好的打擊練習幫助球員把球打得更遠更快。
Word 03
shoot (v)
投籃
He shoots the basketball from outside the three-point line.
他從三分線外投籃。
To score points, you need to shoot the ball into the hoop.
要得分，你需要把球投進籃框。
Word 04
volleyball (n)
排球
Our school volleyball team won the district championship last year.
我們學校的排球隊去年贏得了地區冠軍。
Volleyball is a team sport that requires great communication and teamwork.
排球是一項需要良好溝通和團隊合作的運動。
Word 05
fitness club (n)
體能健身社
She joined the fitness club to stay healthy and build strength.
她加入了體能健身社，讓自己保持健康並增強體力。
The fitness club meets three times a week in the school gymnasium.
體能健身社每週三次在學校體育館集合。
