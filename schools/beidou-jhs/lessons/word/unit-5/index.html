Word 01
observatory (n)
天文台
Our school has its own observatory where students can look at stars at night.
我們學校有自己的天文台，學生可以在晚上觀星。
On a clear night, the observatory gives a wonderful view of the moon and planets.
在晴朗的夜晚，天文台可以看到美麗的月亮和行星。
Word 02
telescope (n)
望遠鏡
We used a telescope to look at the moon and the planets in the night sky.
我們用望遠鏡觀看夜空中的月亮和行星。
A telescope makes far-away objects appear much closer and clearer.
望遠鏡讓遙遠的物體看起來更近更清楚。
Word 03
earthquake drill (n)
地震演習
The school holds an earthquake drill once every semester to keep students safe.
學校每學期舉行一次地震演習，讓學生保持安全。
During an earthquake drill, students practice hiding under their desks quickly.
地震演習時，學生練習迅速躲在桌子下面。
Word 04
firefighter (n)
消防員
A firefighter visited our school to teach us about fire safety.
一位消防員來我們學校教我們消防安全知識。
Firefighters work very hard to protect our community from danger.
消防員非常努力地保護我們的社區免受危險。
Word 05
woodshop (n)
木工坊
Students learn to use saws and hammers safely in the school woodshop.
學生在學校木工坊學習安全使用鋸子和鐵鎚。
The woodshop club made beautiful wooden chairs for the school library.
木工坊社團為學校圖書館製作了漂亮的木椅。
Word 06
pop music club (n)
熱音社
The pop music club performs exciting shows at the school festival every year.
熱音社每年在學校嘉年華上表演精彩的演出。
She plays guitar in the pop music club and loves performing on stage.
她在熱音社彈吉他，喜歡在舞台上表演。
