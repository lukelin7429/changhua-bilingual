Word of the Day · Unit 04

Reading & Study

閱讀與學習的英文

Word 01
test (n)
考試
We have a math test on Friday, so I need to study hard this week.
我們週五有數學考試，所以我這週需要努力讀書。
The teacher said the test will cover chapters three and four.
老師說考試會涵蓋第三和第四章。
Word 02
test paper (n)
試卷
The teacher handed back the test papers with scores written at the top.
老師把寫了分數的試卷發回給學生。
Please write your name clearly on the top of the test paper before you begin.
開始作答前，請在試卷頂部清楚地寫上你的名字。
Word 03
newspaper (n)
報紙
My grandfather reads the newspaper every morning over breakfast.
我的爺爺每天早上吃早餐時讀報紙。
The school library has both Chinese and English newspapers for students to read.
學校圖書館有中文和英文報紙供學生閱讀。
Word 04
story listening (n)
聽故事
Story listening is a wonderful way to improve reading comprehension.
聽故事是提高閱讀理解能力的好方法。
The teacher reads aloud while students practice story listening in class.
老師大聲朗讀，同學在課堂上練習聽故事。
Word 05
book (n)
書
I borrowed a book about science from the school library last week.
我上週從學校圖書館借了一本關於科學的書。
Reading a book for thirty minutes every day will improve your English.
每天讀書三十分鐘會改善你的英語能力。
Word 06
professional development (n)
專業進修
Teachers attend professional development workshops to learn new teaching skills.
老師參加專業進修工作坊來學習新的教學技能。
Our school encourages all staff to join professional development activities each year.
我們學校每年鼓勵所有員工參加專業進修活動。
