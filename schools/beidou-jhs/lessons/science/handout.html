Handout 1 — GIGO Robotic Arm

機械手臂 · Beidou JH · Philippines Exchange Prep

📺 Watch before you start: GIGO Robotic Arm — assembly, joints, and operation. 開始前先看：機械手臂組裝、關節與操作示範。
🔬 Science Concepts  科學概念

What is a Robotic Arm?

A programmable arm that uses servo motors and joints to grip, rotate, and move objects with precision — mimicking a human arm.

透過伺服馬達與關節，進行精確夾取與移動的可程式化裝置。

How Does It Work?

The controller sends signals to each servo motor. Every motor controls one joint. Together they let the arm pick, place, and rotate objects precisely.

控制器發訊號給各伺服馬達，每個馬達控制一個關節，協同運作完成精準動作。

Handout 2 — Air Paper Rocket

空氣紙火箭 · Beidou JH · Philippines Exchange Prep

📺 Watch before you start: Paper Rockets and Launcher (The Sci Guys) — construction, launch, and angle testing. 開始前先看：紙火箭製作、發射與角度測試。
🔬 Science Concepts  科學概念

How Does an Air Rocket Fly?

Air pressure from the launcher propels the rocket forward. Gravity then pulls it back down. The fins stabilise its flight path.

發射器產生的氣壓推動火箭向前，重力再將其拉回，尾翼穩定飛行路徑。

The Best Launch Angle

At 45°, the rocket achieves maximum range on flat ground. Below 45° it lands quickly; above 45° it goes high but not far.

在平地上 45° 達最遠距離。低於 45° 很快落地；高於 45° 飛高卻飛不遠。
