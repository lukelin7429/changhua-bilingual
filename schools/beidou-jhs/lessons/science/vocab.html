🤖 Topic 1 — GIGO Robotic Arm
機械手臂 · Words 01–06
Word 01
robotic arm (n)
機械手臂
Our group assembled a robotic arm that can pick up small objects.
我們小組組裝了一隻可以夾取小物品的機械手臂。
A robotic arm uses motors and joints to move like a human arm.
機械手臂使用馬達和關節，像人類的手臂一樣移動。
Word 02
assembly (n)
組裝
The assembly takes about 40 minutes. Follow each step carefully.
組裝大約需要 40 分鐘，請仔細按照每個步驟進行。
After assembly, check that all joints move freely.
組裝完成後，確認所有關節都能自由活動。
Word 03
joint (n)
關節
Each joint in the robotic arm allows it to bend in a different direction.
機械手臂的每個關節讓它可以朝不同方向彎曲。
This joint seems to be stuck. Can you show me how to fix it?
這個關節好像卡住了，你能告訴我怎麼修嗎？
Word 04
gripper (n)
夾爪
Use the gripper to pick up the object and place it in the box.
用夾爪夾起物品，放進盒子裡。
The gripper opens and closes by pressing the controller button.
按下控制器按鈕，夾爪就會開合。
Word 05
operate (v)
操作
Let me operate the robotic arm. Can you hold the object in place?
讓我來操作機械手臂，你能把物品固定住嗎？
To operate the arm safely, always follow the teacher's instructions.
安全操作手臂，請務必遵照老師的指示。
Word 06
rotate (v)
旋轉
Rotate the base 90 degrees to the right, then lower the arm.
將底座向右旋轉 90 度，然後放低手臂。
The arm can rotate 360 degrees at the base joint.
手臂可以在底部關節旋轉 360 度。
🚀 Topic 2 — Air Paper Rocket
空氣紙火箭 · Words 07–12
Word 07
air rocket (n)
空氣火箭
We built an air rocket from paper and launched it across the field.
我們用紙做了一支空氣火箭，發射它飛過操場。
An air rocket uses air pressure instead of fuel to fly.
空氣火箭用氣壓而非燃料來飛行。
Word 08
launch (v)
發射
Ready? 5, 4, 3, 2, 1 — launch! The rocket flew 8 meters!
準備好了嗎？5、4、3、2、1——發射！火箭飛了8公尺！
We launched the rocket three times and recorded each distance.
我們發射了三次火箭並記錄每次的距離。
Word 09
air pressure (n)
氣壓
More air pressure in the launcher = a stronger push and longer flight.
發射器中的氣壓越大，推力越強，飛行距離越遠。
We squeezed harder to increase the air pressure for our third launch.
我們用力擠壓，增加第三次發射的氣壓。
Word 10
gravity (n)
重力
Gravity is the force that pulls every object — including rockets — back to Earth.
重力是將所有物體（包括火箭）拉回地球的力量。
Without gravity, the rocket would fly in a straight line forever.
如果沒有重力，火箭就會永遠沿直線飛行。
Word 11
launch angle (n)
發射角度
We tested three launch angles: 30°, 45°, and 60°.
我們測試了三種發射角度：30°、45° 和 60°。
A launch angle of 45° gives the maximum flight distance on flat ground.
在平地上，45° 的發射角度可達最遠的飛行距離。
Word 12
trajectory (n)
飛行軌跡
Watch the trajectory — the rocket curves upward and then falls back down.
觀察飛行軌跡——火箭向上弧起，然後落回地面。
A higher angle creates a steeper trajectory but a shorter landing distance.
角度越大，軌跡越陡，但落地距離反而越短。

Sentence Patterns
句型練習 · 10 個科學英語必備句型
1
How do you [verb] the [noun]?
詢問操作方式
How do you assemble the robotic arm? How do you launch the rocket?
2
Can you show me how to [verb] it?
請對方示範
Can you show me how to operate the controller? Can you show me how to measure the distance?
3
Let me try to [verb] it.
表達想嘗試
Let me try to rotate the arm. Let me try to adjust the angle.
4
My group is [verb+ing] the [noun].
描述小組正在做的事
My group is assembling the robotic arm. My group is testing different launch angles.
5
Be careful when you [verb].
給出安全提醒
Be careful when you use the gripper. Be careful when you launch the rocket.
6
What happens when you [verb] the [noun]?
詢問實驗結果
What happens when you increase the air pressure? What happens when you change the angle to 60°?
7
I think [noun] affects [noun] because …
表達科學推論
I think the angle affects flight distance because … I think the motor speed affects arm precision because …
8
Our result shows that [finding].
報告實驗結果
Our result shows that 45° gives the longest distance. Our result shows that the arm can lift small objects.
9
In Taiwan, we also … Do you do this in the Philippines?
與國際夥伴交流文化
In Taiwan, we use GIGO kits for robotics. Do you have science clubs at your school?
10
That's interesting! In my school, we [fact].
分享與回應
That's interesting! In my school, we test three different angles. That's interesting! In Taiwan, we build the arm as a team.
