Word 01
robotic arm (n)
機械手臂
Our group assembled a robotic arm that can pick up small objects.
我們小組組裝了一隻可以夾取小物品的機械手臂。
A robotic arm uses motors and joints to move like a human arm.
機械手臂使用馬達和關節，像人類的手臂一樣移動。
Word 02
assembly (n)
組裝
The assembly takes about 40 minutes. Follow each step carefully.
組裝大約需要 40 分鐘，請仔細按照每個步驟進行。
After assembly, check that all joints move freely.
組裝完成後，確認所有關節都能自由活動。
Word 03
joint (n)
關節
Each joint in the robotic arm allows it to bend in a different direction.
機械手臂的每個關節讓它可以朝不同方向彎曲。
This joint seems to be stuck. Can you show me how to fix it?
這個關節好像卡住了，你能告訴我怎麼修嗎？
Word 04
gripper (n)
夾爪
Use the gripper to pick up the object and place it in the box.
用夾爪夾起物品，放進盒子裡。
The gripper opens and closes by pressing the controller button.
按下控制器按鈕，夾爪就會開合。
Word 05
operate (v)
操作
Let me operate the robotic arm. Can you hold the object in place?
讓我來操作機械手臂，你能把物品固定住嗎？
To operate the arm safely, always follow the teacher's instructions.
安全操作手臂，請務必遵照老師的指示。
Word 06
rotate (v)
旋轉
Rotate the base 90 degrees to the right, then lower the arm.
將底座向右旋轉 90 度，然後放低手臂。
The arm can rotate 360 degrees at the base joint.
手臂可以在底部關節旋轉 360 度。

🔬 Science Concepts  科學概念

What is a Robotic Arm?

A programmable arm that uses servo motors and joints to grip, rotate, and move objects with precision — mimicking a human arm.

透過伺服馬達與關節，進行精確夾取與移動的可程式化裝置。

How Does It Work?

The controller sends signals to each servo motor. Every motor controls one joint. Together they let the arm pick, place, and rotate objects precisely.

控制器發訊號給各伺服馬達，每個馬達控制一個關節，協同運作完成精準動作。

70–90 minOperation操作控制Describe movements: "I'm rotating the arm 90 degrees." "The gripper is open."
