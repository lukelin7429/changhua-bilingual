Leading Beidou JHS into a new era

帶領北斗國中邁向新的里程碑

Principal Wu Bing-lien holds a Bachelor's degree in Industrial Technology Education from National Kaohsiung Normal University, a Master's degree in Physics from National Changhua University of Education (NCUE), and is currently pursuing a Doctorate in Financial Technology at NCUE. He completed the National Academy for Educational Research principal training programme and previously served as a curriculum supervisor at Changhua County Government and held key administrative roles at Hemei Experimental School — including Director of Student Affairs and Director of General Affairs. He pioneered the establishment of Taiwan's first AIoT Smart Networking Education Centre and drove campus aesthetic development and special-character school initiatives. Under his leadership, Beidou JHS continues to deepen the GTRS education philosophy — Gratitude, Technology, Reading, and Sports — as the north star guiding every programme and classroom.

吳炳連校長擁有國立高雄師範大學工業科技教育學系學士、國立彰化師範大學物理學系碩士學位，目前攻讀彰師大金融科技博士。曾完成國家教育研究院校長儲訓課程、擔任彰化縣政府課程督學，並在和美實驗學校歷任訓導主任、總務主任等要職，推動校園美學、特色學校建設，創立全國第一間 AIoT 智慧聯網教育中心。

II.

Educational Philosophy

辦學理念 · SMART × 三生 × 3C
Principal Wu's vision combines three interlocking frameworks — the SMART approach, 三生 (Three Lives) learning, and 3C competencies — into a unified educational culture built on innovation, humanity, and global citizenship. 吳校長的辦學理念以三個相輔相成的框架為核心：SMART 目標導向、三生（生活・生產・生命）學習觀，以及 3C 能力（溝通、創造、批判思考），共同形塑北中的教育文化。
SMART
🎯
SMART Education
目標導向的智慧教育
Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound — every programme at Beidou JHS is designed with clear goals, concrete outcomes, and a meaningful timeline that students and teachers can both see and believe in.
三生
🌱
Three Lives Learning
生活・生產・生命
Education at Beidou integrates three dimensions of living: 生活 (daily life skills), 生產 (productive capacity — from farming to technology), and 生命 (the meaning and depth of a well-lived life). Learning is never abstract; it is always anchored in real experience.
3C
💡
3C Competencies
溝通・創造・批判思考
Communication — speaking up, listening carefully. Creativity — building the AIoT centre, designing the bilingual farm tour, making music from the observatory. Critical Thinking — questioning assumptions, reading data, deciding what matters. These are the skills that outlast every exam.
AIoT
🤖
Taiwan's First AIoT Centre
全國第一間 AIoT 智慧聯網教育中心
Before arriving at Beidou JHS, Principal Wu pioneered the establishment of Taiwan's first AIoT Smart Networking Education Centre at Hemei Experimental School — a landmark achievement that demonstrates his commitment to bringing tomorrow's technology into today's classroom.
"Innovation · Humanity · Full Vision · Unity"
「創新教育 · 多元發展 · 全人視野 · 團結合作」
—— 吳炳連校長辦學四大核心價值
III.

GTRS Vision

四大辦學理念 · 感恩、科技、閱讀、運動

The GTRS philosophy is not a motto — it is a daily practice. From the solar-powered campus to the astronomy observatory, from food-agriculture experiments in the smart farm to reading programmes that have won national recognition, GTRS shapes what learning looks like at Beidou.

GTRS 不是口號，而是每天的具體實踐：從太陽能校園到天文台，從智慧農場到閱讀磐石獎，感恩、科技、閱讀、運動貫穿北中所有的課程設計。

G · Gratitude
🙏
感恩
Gratitude starts with the land
Students who understand where their food comes from — who have knelt in the soil, planted seeds, and waited for the harvest — develop a natural sense of gratitude that no classroom lecture can produce.
T · Technology
🔬
科技
Technology that you can touch
An observatory with a 13-cm telescope. 2,083 solar panels. An air-pollution monitoring box. Technology at Beidou is never abstract — it is always something you can observe, measure, and understand.
R · Reading
📖
閱讀
Reading that earns national recognition
MOE Reading Cornerstone Award winner. Reading at Beidou JHS is woven into every subject — history in the Zhengzhong Hall, science in the observatory, culture in the farm. Books are a starting point, not a destination.
S · Sports
🏃
運動
Sports as character building
From woodball on the field to team sports in the gym, physical activity at Beidou builds more than fitness — it builds the discipline, resilience, and cooperative spirit that carries students into adulthood.
"A school's job is not to fill students with knowledge — it is to light a fire."
「學校的責任不是把知識塞進學生腦袋，而是點燃他們對學習的熱情。」
—— 北斗國中辦學精神
IV.

GTRS in Action

GTRS 的具體成果 · 用獎項見證理念的落地
The GTRS philosophy produces measurable outcomes — awards, programmes, and students who carry these four values beyond the school gate. GTRS 的理念產生了可見的成果——獎項、課程，以及帶著這四種信念走向社會的學生。
G — Gratitude · 感恩成果
第一屆「國家食農教育傑出貢獻獎」團體組優等 · 台美生態學校銀牌認證 · 智慧農場與雨水集水系統
T — Technology · 科技成果
2,083 片太陽能板（彰化縣最大） · 2020 能源教育標竿金獎 · 圓頂天文台（2020 年啟用） · 空汙觀測盒
R — Reading · 閱讀成果
教育部閱讀磐石獎 · 2014 國際網界博覽會白金獎（以北斗中正堂為研究主題） · 校內閱讀推廣計畫
S — Sports · 運動成果
木球、田徑、團體球類運動 · 2023 會考 3 人滿級分（均衡發展的最佳證明）
"Gratitude · Technology · Reading · Sports — four words that take twenty years to teach."
「感恩、科技、閱讀、運動——四個字，需要二十年才能真正教會。」
—— 北斗國中辦學哲學
