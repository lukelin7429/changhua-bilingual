Teacher Aileen Sim
Foreign English Teacher · 北斗國中外籍英語老師
Teacher Aileen Sim comes from the Philippines, bringing extensive experience as both a university lecturer and an ESL teacher. At Beidou JHS, she designs bilingual lessons that connect English directly to the school's signature programs — food-agriculture, astronomy, sustainability, and campus life.
Aileen Sim 老師來自菲律賓，擁有大學教授與 ESL 英語教學的豐富經驗。在北斗國中，她設計的雙語課程將英語直接連結到食農、天文、永續與校園生活——讓英語成為探索真實世界的語言。

III.

Bilingual in Action

北中雙語教育的真實場景
At Beidou JHS, English is not a subject confined to a classroom — it is the language students use to describe the solar panels on the roof, the stars in the observatory, and the vegetables growing in the smart farm. 在北斗國中，英語不只是一門科目，它是學生描述屋頂太陽能板、天文台星空、智慧農場裡蔬菜的語言。
🌾
School Farm — English on the Ground
食農教育英語課 · 從土地學英文
Students learn English vocabulary directly in the smart farm — soil, harvest, irrigation, organic, compost. The words are not in a textbook; they are in the ground beneath their feet.
🔭
Observatory — Stars in Two Languages
天文台科普英語 · 用英文仰望宇宙
Telescope, refractor, constellation, lunar cycle — the observatory is a natural bilingual environment. When you are looking at the same moon in both languages, science and language learning become one.
📡
Air-Pollution Monitoring Box
空汙觀測盒英語課 · 科技與環境的雙語融合
Students build and operate air-quality sensors, then document their findings in English. Particulate matter, sensor, calibration — the vocabulary of modern environmental science, learned by doing.
