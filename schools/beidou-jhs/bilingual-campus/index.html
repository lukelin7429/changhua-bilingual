Teacher Aileen's Hub

Aileen 老師英語教學資源中心
Teacher Aileen Sim
Foreign English Teacher · 北斗國中外籍英語老師
Teacher Aileen Sim comes from the Philippines, bringing extensive experience as both a university lecturer and an ESL teacher. At Beidou JHS, she designs bilingual lessons that connect English directly to the school's signature programs — food-agriculture, astronomy, sustainability, and campus life.
Aileen Sim 老師來自菲律賓，擁有大學教授與 ESL 英語教學的豐富經驗。在北斗國中，她設計的雙語課程將英語直接連結到食農、天文、永續與校園生活——讓英語成為探索真實世界的語言。
