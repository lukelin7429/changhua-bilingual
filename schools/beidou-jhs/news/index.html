June 2023
Three Students Score Perfect 45 — First Time in 20 Years
睽違 20 年！北斗國中 3 名學生獲國中教育會考滿級分
For the first time in two decades, three students from Beidou JHS — Tseng Yu-cheng (資優班), Yang Cheng-hung (普通班), and Chang Yuan-chun (資優班) — achieved a perfect score of 45 on the national junior high assessment, with all three receiving a Grade 5 in the writing section. The students credited consistent effort, reduced screen time, and targeted practice on challenging subjects.

睽違 20 年，曾宥誠、楊丞閎、張蒝畯三名學生在國中教育會考中獲得 45 分滿級分，寫作測驗均為 5 級分。三人共同表示，減少使用 3C 產品、持之以恆地刷題、徹底理解每一道不會的題目，是他們成功的關鍵。
