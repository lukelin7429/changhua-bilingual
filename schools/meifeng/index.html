I.

About Mei Feng

關於美豐 · 一所溫暖的偏鄉生態小學
A small school at the southwest tip of Changhua — where every child is known by name, and every classroom opens onto the field.
位於彰化縣最西南端的偏鄉小校——每一位孩子都被叫得出名字，每一間教室都通向田野。

Mei Feng Elementary School sits in Sanfeng Village, Dacheng Township — at the very southwest corner of Changhua County, on the agricultural plain between the Zhuoshui River and the Taiwan Strait. Founded in April 1952 as a branch of Sigang Public School, the school became independent in 1964 and serves the two villages of Fengmei and Sanfeng — the two villages whose names give us our own: Mei + Feng.

美豐國小位於彰化縣大城鄉三豐村，地處彰化縣最西南角，在濁水溪與台灣海峽之間的農業平原上。1952 年 4 月創校為「西港國民學校美豐分校」，1964 年獨立設校，學區涵蓋「豐美村」與「三豐村」兩村——「美豐」校名即取自兩村之名。

Designated by the Ministry of Education as a remote-area school in 1984, Mei Feng today serves 32 children across 6 classes — one class per grade, with 13 teachers who know every child personally. Small in number, big in care.

美豐自 1984 年起經教育部核定為偏遠地區學校，目前全校 6 班共 32 位學生（一至六年級各一班），13 位教師——人數雖少，照顧卻深。

III.

The F.A.M.I.L.Y. Curriculum

F.A.M.I.L.Y. 校本課程 · 美豐六字頭
School-Based Curriculum · 校本課程
A Curriculum Spelled F.A.M.I.L.Y.
把整個學校當成一個家：六個字母，六條學習軸線

At Mei Feng, our school-based curriculum is spelled F.A.M.I.L.Y. — six letters that name the six dimensions of how a Mei Feng child grows up. From farming in our school plots to literature read aloud at morning circle, every letter is a thread we weave together throughout the school year. 美豐國小的校本課程以「F.A.M.I.L.Y.」六個字母為骨架——每個字母對應一條孩子成長的軸線：從校園農作到晨光朗讀，從禮儀生活到國際視野，整個學年裡彼此交織。

IV.

Three Lead-School Certifications

彰化縣領航學校三項認證

雖然規模很小，美豐國小已榮獲彰化縣三項領航學校認證——在藝術、學力、科技三條軸線上，被認可為帶頭領航的學校。
