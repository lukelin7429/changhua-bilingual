Disaster Preparedness · 防災教育

Earthquake Country 地震國的孩子怎麼學防災

Taiwan sits on the edge of two tectonic plates and four typhoons a year. Disaster preparedness is not a subject — it's part of growing up here. 台灣坐落在兩大板塊交界，每年迎接四場以上颱風。防災不是一門課，而是生活的一部分。

Three Drills Every Year

What We Practise on Campus

校園每年三大演練
🌋

Earthquake Drill 地震演習

Following the Ministry of Education's 9-21 protocol — drop, cover, and hold on. Twice a year we practise the full evacuation route from classroom to assembly area; once a year we join the nationwide 9-21 drill at 09:21 sharp.

🔥

Fire Drill 消防演練

With the Dacun Fire Brigade, students learn to stop, drop, and roll, evacuate calmly with a cloth over the nose, and identify the school's fire extinguishers. Older grades practise basic first aid.

🌀

Typhoon Readiness 颱風應變

From late summer through autumn, our staff monitor Central Weather Administration alerts and coordinate early-release decisions with Changhua County. Students learn what a sea/land alert means and how their school stays open or closes safely.

At Cundong

Health, Safety & Disaster — One Curriculum

"Preparation is the easiest way to keep a community safe."

At Cundong, disaster preparedness sits inside a broader Health Promotion programme. We teach it the way we teach everything else here — calmly, in plain language, with both Mandarin and English vocabulary, and always close to the campus and the community.

Our official school feature statement names 校園防災宣導 (campus disaster awareness) alongside 傳染病防治 (communicable-disease control), 緊急救護 (emergency first aid), and 慢性病防治 (chronic-disease prevention) as one connected programme — "delivered in light, accessible, multi-format ways so that students and staff alike can take the knowledge home and use it in daily life."

Disaster Awareness 校園防災宣導
Communicable Disease Control 傳染病防治
Emergency First Aid 緊急救護
Chronic Disease Prevention 慢性病防治

校方原文：「推動健康促進議題、傳染病防治、緊急救護、慢性病防治...等相關衛教宣導，從愉悅輕鬆多元化方式推動多項宣導衛教，讓師生共同吸取知識和技能應用在生活上。」

我們把防災教育放在「健康促進」的整體框架裡——用平易近人、輕鬆多元的方式教，並同時學中英文相關語彙，讓孩子能把知識帶回家、用在生活裡。
