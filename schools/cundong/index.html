About Our School

From a Hillside Branch School to a Green-Flag Campus

從村上分校到綠旗小校，六十五年的山麓教育
Our campus mural — a hand-painted forest of all four seasons 校園彩繪牆 · 四季森林手繪

Founded 1961 · Serving three villages at the foot of the Bagua Plateau

In 1957, fourteen local elders petitioned to bring schooling closer to children in Fuxing, Pinghe, and Huangcuo — three villages spread along the western edge of the Bagua Plateau. Two years later, Tsunshang Elementary opened a branch school here. In August 1961, that branch became its own campus: Cundong Elementary School.

Sixty-five years on, Cundong is a small school with a wide view. Our classrooms sit on hilltop terrain along Shanjiao Road (Provincial Highway 137); from many windows, students can see all the way across the Dacun grape country and into Yuanlin City.

Today our 11 classes follow three commitments first set out by the school: character, reading, and ecology. In School Year 114 (2025), our long-running environmental work earned the highest honor in Taiwan's Eco-Schools program — a U.S.–Taiwan Green Flag certification.

民國 46 年（1957），何前、石金彩等十四位地方人士在大村鄉鄉民代表會提案設校，方便平和、福興、黃厝三村學童就近就讀。民國 48 年（1959）8 月成立「村上國校村東分校」，民國 50 年（1961）8 月奉准獨立，正式更名為村東國民學校。

校址位於福興村山腳路（縣道 137 號），地處八卦台地西緣山麓高處，視野可俯瞰大村葡萄產區與員林市區。全校 11 班，校本三大教育理念為「品格、閱讀、生態」。民國 114 學年度（2025），長期耕耘的環境教育榮獲臺美生態學校認證系統最高榮譽「綠旗（Green Flag）」。

U.S.–Taiwan Eco Green Flag 臺美生態學校綠旗

Three years of work, three rising honors. In 112 we earned bronze; in 113, silver; in 114, the Green Flag — the program's highest tier. Our student "Eco Action Team" declaration is simple: take only what you need, sort what you no longer use, protect the plants and animals around us.

三年深耕、三級晉升：112 學年度銅、113 學年度銀、114 學年度綠旗——臺美生態學校認證系統最高榮譽。學生「生態行動團隊宣言」很簡單：拿得剛好吃得對、資源回收要分類、保護植物與動物棲地。

🌐

A-mae-zing English 菲律賓外師 Mae 駐校

Our English program is named after our Foreign English Teacher, Ms. Maelyn (Mae) De Torres Villano from the Philippines. "A-Mae-zing" is more than wordplay — it's how our students think about English class: a place where learning happens by doing, by playing, and by laughing together. Mae's own teaching site, with two full years of Cundong lesson archives, is linked below.

英語課的名字「A-mae-zing English」其實就是外師 Maelyn (Mae) 老師的名字諧音——Mae 老師來自菲律賓，是村東駐校外籍英語老師。她的個人教學網站收藏了 113 與 114 兩個學年度的課堂、公開課、讀書會與歌唱比賽全紀錄。

🌏

Pen Pals With Thailand 5A 班國際交流

Since May 2026, our 5A class has been writing — in English — to a partner class of 10-year-olds in Thailand. They introduce themselves, share their hobbies and pets, and leave each other comments in three languages. International education, without the airfare.

自 2026 年 5 月起，村東 5A 班與泰國夥伴班透過 Padlet 線上交流牆持續用英文互相介紹自己、分享興趣與寵物，並用中、英、泰三語互相留言。不出國的國際教育，正在我們的教室發生。

International Education

Pen Pals in Two Languages

5A 班與泰國夥伴班 · 用英文跨海對話

One Padlet wall, two classrooms, one school year of conversation

Hosted by Cundong's 5A English teacher Ms. Jessie and run alongside Ms Mae's bilingual lessons, the project pairs Cundong fifth-graders with a class of 10-year-olds at a partner school in Thailand. Students post in English, comment in English, and slip in Mandarin and Thai when emoji isn't enough.

Since the wall went live on 7 May 2026, posts have grown to 32 and counting — self-introductions, hobby quizzes, pet photos, and an ongoing debate about who plays the better Roblox.

由村東 5A 班英語老師 Ms. Jessie 主持，搭配 Mae 老師的雙語教學節奏，5A 班與一所泰國小學的同齡夥伴班配對。學生用英文發文、用英文留言，偶爾用中文與泰文補位。2026 年 5 月 7 日上線以來貼文累積至 32 則並持續增加。
