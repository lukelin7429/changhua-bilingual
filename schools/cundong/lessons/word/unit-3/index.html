jump rope (n.)

The students are practicing jump rope during recess.
學生們在下課時間練習跳繩。
Jump rope is good exercise for your legs and your heart.

Word 03
Taekwondo (n.)
跆拳道
The students are learning Taekwondo in PE.
學生們在體育課學習跆拳道。
Taekwondo teaches both self-defense and discipline.
跆拳道訓練自我防衛與紀律。
Word 04

The students are learning to play badminton.
學生們正在學習打羽毛球。

The students are working on their soccer skills on the field.
學生們正在球場上練習足球技巧。

足球是學校最受歡迎的團體運動之一。
