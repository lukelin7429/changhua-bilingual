Word 01
reading
閱讀
Look at the students — they're all reading quietly.
看看這些學生——他們都安靜地閱讀。
Reading every day is one of Cundong's three school commitments.
每天閱讀是村東國小三大教育理念之一。
Word 02
reading activity (n.)
閱讀活動
The students do a reading activity in class.
學生在課堂上進行閱讀活動。
They work in groups to do a reading activity.
他們分組進行閱讀活動。
Word 03
library rules (n.)
圖書館規則
The students learn library rules.
學生學習圖書館規則。
The students need to follow the library rules when they go to the library.
學生去圖書館時必須遵守圖書館的規定。
Word 04
mobile library (n.)
行動圖書館
The mobile library is a truck that's been converted into a small library.
行動圖書館是由卡車改裝的小圖書館。
The mobile library can bring lots of books to students at different schools!
行動圖書館可以將書帶給不同學校的學生閱讀。
Word 05
fountain (n.)
噴水池
The school has a fountain that makes the campus look nice.
學校有一個噴泉，讓校園看起來很漂亮。
The fountain is not only pretty, but also gives the students a place to relax.
噴泉不僅漂亮，而且給學生們提供了一個放鬆的地方。
Word 06
wash your hands
洗手
Wash your hands before lunch and after PE.
午餐前和體育課後要洗手。
Washing your hands is the easiest way to stay healthy.
洗手是保持健康最簡單的方法。
Word 07
first grader
一年級生
The school organized activities to help first graders and their parents get to know the school better.
學校舉辦活動，幫助一年級新生與家長認識校園。
Every first grader gets a welcome book on their first day.
每位一年級新生在入學第一天都會收到一本歡迎手冊。
Word 08
learn English (v.)
學習英語
Listening to stories is a fun and effective way to learn English.
聽故事是一種有趣且有效的英語學習方法。
Interactive games can effectively engage students in learning English.
互動遊戲可以有效地吸引學生學習英語。
Word 09
color (n.)
顏色
Some pieces of art aren't finished until the artist has added color.
有些藝術作品要等到加上顏色才算完成。
The students learn the names of colors in English.
學生們用英文學習顏色的名稱。
Word 10
newspaper (n.)
報紙
These students are reading newspapers.
學生們正在閱讀報紙。
Reading newspapers helps students learn about the world.
閱讀報紙幫助學生認識世界。
