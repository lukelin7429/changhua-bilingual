Sports & Movement 運動與律動

Cundong is the home of three signature teams — unicycling, roller skating, and drone flying — all of which have placed in national championships. These six words come straight off our PE field and after-school practice. 村東以獨輪車、滑輪、無人機三大運動隊聞名，皆有全國錦標賽得獎紀錄。這六個字就在我們的操場與課後訓練拍攝。

Theme 2 of 4 · 7 words

Culture & Crafts 文化與手作

Some words can't be translated — they need to be seen. Sugar painting, glove puppetry, the Chinese yo-yo — these are crafts you can only learn by holding them in your hands. We filmed each one on campus, so the English word and the Taiwanese tradition arrive together. 糖畫、布袋戲、扯鈴——有些字翻譯不來，必須親手摸過。這些手作與文化字彙，每一個都是在校園裡實作中拍攝。

Theme 3 of 4 · 9 words

Ecology & Nature 生態與自然

Cundong is a U.S.–Taiwan Eco Green Flag school. That isn't a poster — it's a daily practice: planting, watering, sweeping, sorting, climbing the campus trees. These nine words come from work students do with their own hands. 村東是臺美生態學校綠旗校。生態不是口號，是每天動手做：種植、澆水、掃地、分類、爬校樹。這九個字都從學生親手做的事情裡誕生。

Earth Day(n.)地球日 moringa(n.)辣木 plant(n.)植物 set plant(n.)植栽 water(v.)澆水 fertilizer(n.)肥料 garden trowel(n.)小鏟子 sweep(v.)掃地 climbing trees爬樹

Theme 4 of 4 · 16 words

School Life 校園生活

This is the biggest theme — the everyday school grammar: the library rules, the mobile library, the fountain in the courtyard, the act of reading itself. From "first grader" to "professional development", these are the words that hold our school day together. 這是字數最多的主題——校園生活的日常文法：圖書館規則、行動圖書館、中庭噴水池、閱讀本身。從「一年級生」到「專業進修」，這些字串起村東每一天的節奏。
