Welcome to Cundong 歡迎來到村東國小

For more than six decades, Cundong Elementary has educated children from three villages — Fuxing, Pinghe, and Huangcuo — at the western foot of the Bagua Plateau. Today, we are a small school of 11 classes with a global outlook: a U.S.–Taiwan Eco Green Flag campus, home of A-mae-zing English, and partners in an international pen-pal exchange with a school in Thailand.

What we hold on to has not changed since 1961. We teach our children to live with character, to fall in love with reading, and to take care of the world around them. Everything else — the drones, the dancing, the bilingual classes — grows from these three roots.

村東國小成立超過一甲子，長期服務八卦山麓下福興、平和、黃厝三村學童。今日，全校 11 個班級雖小，但放眼世界：是臺美生態學校綠旗認證校、駐校外師 Mae 老師的 A-mae-zing English 旗艦校、5A 班正與泰國夥伴班透過 Padlet 進行國際交流。

自 1961 年獨立創校至今，我們守住三件事：教孩子有品格、愛閱讀、護土地。無人機隊、舞蹈社、雙語課——每一項精彩，都從這三個根長出來。
