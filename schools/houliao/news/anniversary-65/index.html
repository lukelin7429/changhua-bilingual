Happy 65th Birthday, Houliao: Anniversary Celebration & Carnival後寮國小 65 週年校慶暨園遊會精彩回顧

November 25, 2025 · 114/11/25

Thank you, everyone, for gathering to witness Houliao Elementary's 65th birthday. The children's lively performances and the bustling market filled the whole campus with happiness.

感謝大家齊聚一堂，見證後寮國小 65 歲生日快樂。孩子們的活力演出與熱鬧市集，讓全校洋溢著滿滿的幸福感。

Sixty-five years of a small school, and a whole village to celebrate it.一所小校的 65 年，與整個村莊一起慶祝。

Our sincere thanks to the Fangyuan Township Mayor, the Farmers' Association General Director, our distinguished alumnus and councillor, the village chief, the district supervisor, principals from neighbouring schools, and friends from every corner — who came in person to send the school their warmest wishes. Special thanks, too, to the reporters of Changhua TV and TOP-NEWS for recording this precious moment through their professional lenses.

誠摯感謝芳苑鄉長、農會總幹事、優質校友議員、村長、駐區督學、友校校長及各界好友親臨現場，為學校送上最暖心的祝福！特別謝謝彰視新聞與大頭條新聞記者採訪報導，透過專業鏡頭記錄下這珍貴的一刻。
