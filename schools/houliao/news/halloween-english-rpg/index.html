No Ice Cream? Cast a Spell! Houliao's Halloween English Adventure不給冰就唸咒 · 後寮國小萬聖節鬼怪英語大冒險

Halloween 2025 · 114/10

Houliao's Halloween RPG adventure was open for business! The campus transformed into a world of magic, and the children became little wizards — solving English riddles, hunting down ghosts and chanting spells to break the seals.

後寮國小萬聖節 RPG 大冒險開啟！校園化身魔法世界，孩子們化身小小魔法師，解英語謎題、尋找鬼怪與唸出咒語解除封印。

The children spoke English with the world — one spell at a time.孩子們用英語與世界對話，一句咒語接著一句。

Thanks to Huamei Co., Ltd. for sponsoring lactic-acid ice cream, learning English became sweet and fun. Amid the laughter and surprises, the children spoke English with the world and opened the door to their most unforgettable Halloween magic journey. The day's fun even caught the eye of Changhua TV news.

感謝樺美股份有限公司贊助乳酸冰淇淋，讓學習英語也能甜蜜又有趣。在笑聲與驚喜中，孩子們用英語與世界對話，開啟最難忘的萬聖節魔法旅程——當天的精彩也吸引了彰視新聞前來採訪報導。
