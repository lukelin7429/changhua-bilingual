Stories from Houliao 後寮的故事 · 86 個孩子，一座小校的近期榮譽

Sports, reading milestones, township carnivals — what 86 children can do when every one of them is given the field. 運動、閱讀里程碑、鄉運嘉年華——當 86 個孩子每一個都被認真對待，能做的事比想像更多。

Sport · 校隊運動
April 27, 2026 FEATURED

Houliao Boys Take the Trophy at the Dayuan Junior-Chamber Soccer Invitational 參加大園青商盃五人制足球邀請賽獲獎

Grade-three boys from Houliao came back from Taoyuan with a podium finish at the 2026 Dayuan Junior-Chamber Five-a-Side Soccer Invitational. The school's public note thanked Principal Yan-zhi's promotion, Coach Chou's training, and Teacher Chun-chieh's guidance — the trio behind a soccer programme that has now travelled outside Changhua County.

後寮國小三甲學生參加 2026 年「大園青商盃五人制足球邀請賽」（桃園大園）獲頒優勝。校方公告中特別感謝彥智校長的推動，周教練、俊傑老師的指導——這支讓偏鄉孩子有機會走出彰化縣的足球隊，背後是長期默默支撐的鐵三角。

Mid-Term Honour Roll & Reading Passport Achievers 114 學年度第二學期期中評量成績優異暨閱讀護照達標

The school's spring semester honour roll runs from Grade 1 through Grade 6 — with separate recognition lists for students who crossed the 25-, 50-, and 75-book Reading Passport milestones. In a school of 86, this list is almost the whole student body.

114 學年度第二學期期中評量公布全校成績優異名單，並同時表揚「閱讀護照」達 25 本、50 本、75 本三個里程碑的孩子。對一所 86 人的小校來說，這份榜單幾乎覆蓋了全校。

Sport · 校隊運動
April 18, 2026

Silver at the County Education Cup — Croquet, Three-Player Division 教育盃槌球錦標賽榮獲佳績

At the Changhua County Education Cup Croquet Championships, Houliao's three-player team brought home a second-place finish. Croquet here is a school sport, not a hobby — the team meets after school on the same field where the soccer team trains.

彰化縣教育盃槌球錦標賽，後寮國小三人制隊伍榮獲第二名。槌球在後寮是正式的校隊運動，與足球隊共用校內運動場域，每週固定練習。
Source · 來源：學校官網公告 hles.chc.edu.tw/posts
Sport · 校隊運動
April 14, 2026

Inline-Skate Speed Racers Bring Home Medals 教育盃競速直排輪溜冰錦標賽獲佳績

The Houliao inline-skate speed-racing team competed at the Changhua County Education Cup and returned with medals. Three sports, one ask: show up at practice, train hard, race for Houliao. Soccer, croquet, and inline-skating each carry that same line.

後寮國小競速直排輪校隊參加彰化縣教育盃競速直排輪溜冰錦標賽，獲頒佳績。足球、槌球、直排輪——這三項運動對每個孩子的要求都一樣：到場、認真練、為後寮出賽。

Community · 社區共學
Spring 2026

At the Fangyuan Township Carnival, Houliao Stands Tall 芳苑鄉運動嘉年華 · 後寮的孩子站上更大的舞台

Every spring the ten-plus schools of Fangyuan Township meet for the township-wide Sports Carnival. Houliao students come home with certificates, ribbons, and the quiet pride of having represented their small village on a bigger stage.

每年春天，芳苑鄉十多所學校一同參與「運動嘉年華」。後寮的孩子帶著獎狀與勳章回來，靜靜地站在校門口拍照——他們知道，自己代表的是後寮這個小村莊。
