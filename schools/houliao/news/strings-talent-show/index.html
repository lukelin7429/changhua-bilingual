Strings of Light: Playing a Movement of Self-Belief琴弦撥動流光 · 演繹自信樂章

April 19, 2026 · 115/4/19

The air inside the Zhutang Elementary activity centre flowed with melody. On the stage of the Changhua County club-talent showcase, Houliao's children performed "Strings Sounding, Song of Flowing Light" as the grand finale, letting their brightest confidence shine.

今日的竹塘國小活動中心，空氣中流淌著動人的旋律。在彰化縣才藝社團展演的舞台上，後寮孩子們以「琴弦奏鳴 流光之曲」作為壓軸，展現孩子最耀眼的自信光芒。

Every child is a one-of-a-kind note.每個孩子都是獨一無二的音符。

Education is never a solo effort — it is a gentle gathering of many hands. Thanks to Parents' Association Chair Chen Geng-min and his wife, who arrived with cool drinks for the sweating students and teachers; to every parent who came to style hair, prepare costumes and lend a hand; and to Director Yu-wen, leader Yu-ren and teacher Wei-cheng, who guarded the whole performance to its close.

教育從非孤軍奮戰，而是一場溫柔的眾志成城。感謝家長會陳庚民會長賢伉儷特地帶著沁涼飲品蒞臨，為揮汗練習的師生注入動力；感謝每一位到場、為孩子梳妝打扮與贊助物資的家長；感謝郁雯主任率領育仁組長與偉誠老師全程守護，讓演出圓滿順遂。

The music drifts away on the wind, but the memory of working together stays — carved, like flowing light, into the rings of each child's growing years. We believe every child is a one-of-a-kind note; at Houliao, we invite them to compose their own golden childhood.

琴聲會隨風散去，但這份共同努力的記憶，將如流光般，永遠鐫刻在孩子成長的年輪裡。我們相信每個孩子都是獨一無二的音符，邀請孩子在後寮譜寫屬於自己的金色童年。
