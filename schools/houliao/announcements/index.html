What each office actually does — in English and 中文 每個處室在做什麼 · 雙語版本

A bilingual school-announcement series, organised by the four school offices: Academic Affairs, Student Affairs, General Affairs, and Counselling. Each episode introduces what one office does, in both English and 中文 — so the school's daily public-address time turns into real-world bilingual exposure for every child on campus. 雙語校園廣播系列，依教務、學務、總務、輔導四個處室分組；每一集都中英雙語介紹該處室在做的事——把學校每天的廣播時間，變成校內真實的雙語浸潤。

A note about Houliao's offices · 後寮的處室小說明： Houliao is a small village school that runs a streamlined structure — "Educational Affairs" (教導處) combines what bigger schools split between Academic Affairs and Student Affairs. The series still maps cleanly onto the work each office does day-to-day.

Why this matters at Houliao 在後寮，為什麼這份廣播庫特別重要

Daily public-address time exists in every Taiwanese school, including Houliao. Plugging in a short bilingual episode during morning announcements gives every Houliao child a regular, low-pressure encounter with English — no extra prep period, no extra paperwork, no extra teacher hours. 每一所台灣國小都有校內廣播時間，後寮也不例外。在每天的廣播時段中嵌入一支短短的雙語廣播，等於讓校內每一位孩子每天都能規律、無壓地接觸英文——不需要額外備課、不需要額外公文、不需要額外師資工時。
