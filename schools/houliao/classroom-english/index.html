Why this matters at Houliao 在後寮，為什麼這份影片庫特別重要

Houliao is a small rural village school where English teachers carry a heavy mix of grades and subjects, and outside English exposure is hard to come by. A pre-made video library means a teacher can sit a class down before the bell and let the same sentences a Taipei classroom hears walk into a Fangyuan classroom for free. 後寮國小是一所偏鄉小校，英文老師往往跨年級、跨科目，校外的英語接觸機會有限。一個預先做好的影片庫，意義是——讓台北課堂聽到的同一組句子，也免費走進芳苑鄉的課堂裡。
