English that lives in the school day 活在校園日常裡的英文

A bilingual campus is not one English lesson a week — it is English woven into the ordinary day. Houliao plugs into two free video libraries made by My Culture Connect 人師教育協會 and shared across all 89 Changhua Bilingual Hub schools: one for the classroom, one for the school's daily announcements. No new budget, no extra teacher hours — just press play. 雙語校園不是一週一節英文課，而是把英文織進平常的一天。後寮接上人師教育協會製作、全 89 校共用的兩套免費影片庫：一套給課堂，一套給每天的校園廣播——不需要新預算、不需要額外師資工時，按下播放就能用。

Why this matters at Houliao 在後寮，為什麼這兩套影片庫特別重要

Houliao is a small rural village school where teachers carry a heavy mix of grades and subjects, and outside English exposure is hard to come by. These two libraries let the same sentences a city classroom hears walk into a Fangyuan classroom for free — and turn daily announcement time into a regular, low-pressure encounter with English for every child, with no extra prep, paperwork, or teacher hours. 後寮國小是一所偏鄉小校，老師往往跨年級、跨科目，校外的英語接觸機會有限。這兩套影片庫讓城市課堂聽到的同一組句子免費走進芳苑鄉的課堂，也把每天的廣播時間，變成校內每一位孩子規律、無壓的英語接觸——不需要額外備課、公文或師資工時。
