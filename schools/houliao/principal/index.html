II.

Four Values, One School

學校四項核心價值 · 公開揭示的辦學願景

These four short phrases are what Houliao puts on its own front page and what the principal returns to every morning assembly: gratitude, vitality, optimism, and the room for each child to find their own path. 這四項是後寮國小公開揭示的學校願景，也是校長在每一次升旗、頒獎、運動會上重複向孩子說的話：感恩、活力、樂觀、屬於自己的路。

🌾
Value 01
Gratitude & Contentment
感恩惜福

"To know how blessed we are — and then to turn around and help someone else."

「做一個知福惜福的人，懂得獲得後再去幫助別人；做一個能夠被人感恩的人。」

Value 02
Vitality & Innovation
活力創新

"Encourage children to invent — and to keep their learning alive."

「鼓勵在各方面的創新，確保高度學習的活力。」

🌅
Value 03
Optimism & Drive
樂觀進取

"Optimism is a lantern — it lights the road of hope. Children who learn to face life with optimism find joy on the way."

「樂觀如明燈，照亮希望的前程……樂觀進取，才能面對喜悅的人生。」

🌱
Value 04
Many Paths Forward
多元發展

"Grow with each child's gift in mind — let every Houliao student find what they are good at, and stand in that light."

「因應學童的個性與天分來發展，協助學童發揮潛能、尋得專長。」

III.

What the Principal Stands Behind

校長在背後推動的事

In a small village school, leadership shows up in the everyday — showing up at the field, the assembly, and the prize-giving. Below is what we have learned about the principal's quiet hand on Houliao's recent year. 一所偏鄉小校，校長的領導力在日常顯現——出現在球場邊、升旗台前、頒獎時刻。下方是過去這一年，校長默默推動的事。

Soccer, croquet, inline-skating — sports as confidence 用球場讓孩子建立自信

Three sports teams — five-a-side soccer, croquet, and inline-skate speed-racing — have all won county-level honours under the principal's tenure. The 2026 公告 thanking "Principal Lin's promotion, Coach Chou's training, and Teacher Chun-chieh's guidance" for the soccer victory is the public face of what is, in practice, a daily commitment to keeping rural children on the field. 校內三支校隊——五人制足球、槌球、競速直排輪——都在校長任內走到全縣榮譽榜。校方 2026 年公告中那句「林彥志校長的推動，周教練、俊傑老師的指導」是公開的致謝，背後則是日復一日讓偏鄉孩子持續站上球場的承諾。

Reading Passport — the 25 / 50 / 75-book milestones 閱讀護照 · 25 / 50 / 75 本的里程碑

The school's Reading Passport programme recognises every child who reads 25, 50, or 75 books in a semester. Each milestone gets its own roll-call at assembly — one of the principal's clearest signals that, even in a small village school, every page read is publicly celebrated. 校內「閱讀護照」計畫，每學期表揚 25 本、50 本、75 本三個里程碑達成者。每一級在升旗台上都有自己的點名時刻——這是校長對全校孩子最清楚的訊號：在偏鄉小校，每一本讀完的書，都會被公開看見。

Built-in safeguards for a small rural school 為偏鄉小校建構的基本守護

Houliao publishes campus policies on gender-equity education, smoke-free school, mobile-device management, and gender-incident prevention. In a school where everyone knows everyone, these aren't paperwork — they're the quiet scaffolding the principal keeps in place so the day-to-day stays safe and predictable. 後寮的校園政策頁列有性別平等教育、無菸校園、行動載具管理、校園性別事件防治——這些不是表單，而是校長為「彼此都認得」的小校維持安全、可預期日常的底層支架。

"Small enough that every teacher knows every child — and every child has a chance to lead."
「小到每位老師都認得每一個孩子，每個孩子也都有屬於自己的舞台。」
—— 後寮國小辦學底色
