Houliao Elementary · 後寮國小

Clubs & Activities 社團活動

A small village school where every child finds a stage 在這所偏鄉小校，每個孩子都找得到自己的舞台

Music· 音樂 Art· 藝術 Sport· 運動 English· 英語 Care· 課後照顧

A small village school, a surprising number of stages.

後寮為孩子準備了音樂、藝術、運動與英語社團——因為在這麼小的學校，每個孩子都輪得到發光的機會。從練琴到揮毫，從綠茵場到紅色跑道，孩子在課本之外，找到屬於自己的那件事。

🎵

Music音樂社團

Harmonica · 口琴

Harmonica Club口琴社團

From quiet practice in the hall to the bright stage of the county student music contest, our harmonica players learn that a small instrument can fill a whole room. The club performs at the school anniversary sports day and competes as both an ensemble and a quartet.

從練習室裡反覆吹奏，到彰化縣學生音樂比賽的舞台，口琴社的孩子學會：小小的樂器，也能填滿整個禮堂。社團在校慶運動會登台演出，並以合奏與四重奏的形式參賽。

Strings · 弦樂

Violin · Beginner Class小提琴初階班

In the school library, beginners take up the violin for the very first time — learning to hold the bow, read a line of music, and play together. For many of our coastal-village children, this is their first encounter with a string instrument.

在學校圖書館裡，初階班的孩子第一次拿起小提琴——學著握弓、看譜、一起合奏。對許多沿海農村的孩子來說，這是他們第一次接觸弦樂器。

Strings · 弦樂

Violin · Advanced Class小提琴進階班

Students who have found their footing move on to the advanced class, playing longer pieces and tackling trickier passages alongside the school piano. Step by step, a village school grows its own little string section.

站穩腳步的孩子升上進階班，挑戰更長的曲目與更難的樂段，並與鋼琴搭配練習。一步一步，偏鄉小校也養出了自己的小小弦樂團。

Strings · 弦樂

Ukulele Club烏克麗麗社團

Four strings, big smiles. The ukulele club gives our younger children a friendly first instrument — easy to hold, easy to love — and the quiet confidence of strumming their very first song together.

四條弦，滿滿的笑容。烏克麗麗社給年紀小的孩子一把最親切的入門樂器——好拿、好上手——也給他們一起刷出第一首歌的成就感。

Art & Making藝術與手作

Calligraphy · 書法

Calligraphy Club書法社團

Brush, ink, and a quiet kind of focus. In the calligraphy club, students practise the strokes of Chinese characters, steady their hand, and discover the patience that good writing asks of them.

毛筆、墨汁，和一種安靜的專注。書法社的孩子練習一筆一畫，穩住手腕，也在一橫一豎之間，體會寫好字所需要的耐心。

Craft · 手作

Collage Club拼貼社團

With scissors, paper, and a pile of odds and ends, the collage club turns everyday materials into bright new pictures. It is hands-on art that rewards imagination over neatness.

一把剪刀、各色紙張，加上一堆零碎素材，拼貼社把日常的材料變成繽紛的畫。這是一門動手做的藝術——比起工整，更看重想像力。

Visual Art · 美術

Art Club美術社團

At their desks, sleeves rolled up, our young artists draw, paint, and colour their way through the year. The art club is where a small school makes a big, bright mess — and hangs the results proudly on the wall.

在座位上捲起袖子，小小藝術家們畫畫、上色，度過一整年。美術社是小校盡情揮灑色彩的地方——也是把作品驕傲地掛上牆的地方。

Sport & Movement運動與體能

Soccer · 足球

Five-a-Side Soccer足球社團

On the green field beside the school, our five-a-side soccer team trains hard and competes countywide. In their bright jerseys, these children carry the name of a tiny coastal school onto a much bigger pitch.

在校園旁的綠茵場上，五人制足球隊認真練球，征戰全縣賽事。穿上鮮明的球衣，這群孩子把一所沿海小校的名字，帶上了更大的舞台。

Skating · 直排輪

Inline Skating直排輪社團

Helmets on, eight wheels rolling. On the school court our skaters practise balance and speed, then race in the Changhua County speed-skating championship. It is one of the three sports — with soccer and croquet — that define life at Houliao.

戴上安全帽，八個輪子轉動起來。在校內球場，直排輪的孩子練平衡、練速度，再參加彰化縣競速直排輪錦標賽。這是定義後寮校園生活的三大運動之一（另兩項是足球與槌球）。

Jump Rope · 跳繩

Jump Rope Club跳繩社團

Simple rope, endless tricks. The jump-rope club builds stamina, rhythm, and teamwork out on the school track — proof that you don't need fancy equipment to get a whole body moving.

一條簡單的繩子，變化無窮。跳繩社在跑道上練體能、練節奏、練默契——證明了不需要昂貴的器材，也能讓全身動起來。

Whole School · 全校活動

Morning Run晨跑活動

Before the first lesson, the whole school gathers on the red track to run together as the sun comes up. Morning run isn't a competition — it's how Houliao starts the day, every child side by side.

第一節課之前，全校在紅色跑道上集合，迎著朝陽一起晨跑。晨跑不是比賽——而是後寮一天的開始，每個孩子並肩同行。

English英語社團

Lower Grades · 低年級

Lower-Grade English Club低年級英語社團

For our youngest learners, English begins with movement, games, and laughter rather than worksheets. The lower-grade English club turns the corridor into a playground where children pick up their first English through play.

對最年幼的學習者來說，英語是從動作、遊戲與笑聲開始的，而不是從學習單。低年級英語社把走廊變成遊樂場，孩子在玩中學會他們的第一句英語。

Part of our bilingual journey · 雙語的起點： these early, playful sessions are where Houliao's English-rich campus begins.
Middle & Upper Grades · 中高年級

Saturday English Class週六中高年級英語勤學班

On Saturday mornings, our middle- and upper-grade students choose to come back to school for extra English — small-group lessons, hands-on activities, and time with a teacher who knows each of them by name. In a rural school where English resources are scarce, this is how Houliao closes the gap.

週六上午，中高年級的孩子選擇回到學校加強英語——小組教學、實作活動，以及一位記得每個孩子名字的老師。在英語資源相對缺乏的偏鄉，後寮就是這樣，一點一滴把落差補起來。

🧒

After-School Care課後照顧

Daily Support · 日常照顧

After-School Care Class課後照顧班

When the bell rings, learning doesn't stop. The after-school care class gives our children a calm, supervised place to finish homework, read, and play — until their families come home from the fields and the factory.

放學鐘響，學習並沒有停止。課後照顧班給孩子一個安靜、有人陪伴的地方，讓他們寫完功課、閱讀、玩耍，直到家人從田裡與工廠返家。

Being small means being seen 小校的意義，是每個孩子都被看見

From the violin to the soccer field, from the calligraphy brush to the morning track — at Houliao, there is room for every child to find the thing that is theirs.

從小提琴到足球場，從一支毛筆到清晨的跑道——在後寮，每個孩子都有空間，找到屬於自己的那件事。
