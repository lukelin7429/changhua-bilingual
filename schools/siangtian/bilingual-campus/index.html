ENGLISH LIFE · 01
A Small Door, Always Open
校園英語的三條路

English is not a subject. It is a daily door.

英文不是一個學科，是一道日常的門。

For a country school, the question is not how many vocabulary words a child can list. It is whether English shows up at all in the rhythm of the week. Siangtian's answer is three small but steady doors — a Weekly English sentence, a teacher-facing video library, and a campus-broadcast library — all built in partnership with My Culture Connect. 對偏鄉小校來說，問題不是「孩子能背幾個單字」，而是「英文有沒有出現在這一週」。香田的答案是三道小而穩定的門：每週英語、給老師用的影片庫、給校園廣播用的影片庫——全部由人師教育協會協力打造。

週

Weekly English 每週一句的英語門

One sentence, one week, one whole school. The simplest possible curriculum — and the most reliable.

每週一句、用一整週、全校通用——最簡單，也最容易堅持下去的英文。

師

For Teachers 課室英語影片庫

A video library of teaching language — greet, manage, praise, correct — for every teacher in the building.

給全校老師使用的「課室英語」影片庫——問候、管理、讚美、糾正，全部都在裡面。

廣

For Campus Broadcast 校園廣播英語

Bilingual announcements — so the school's daily voice itself is one of the places children meet English.

校園廣播英語——讓學校每天的聲音，也是孩子遇見英文的地方。

ENGLISH LIFE · 02
This Week at Siangtian
每週英語 ─ 本週一句
This Week · 本週

"It's harvest day. Let's go to the rice paddy!"

今天是收割日，我們去稻田吧！

A Weekly English sentence is posted on the school site each week, paired with audio. It is short on purpose. The idea is not to memorise — it is to use it: at line-up, on the way to lunch, when a parent picks up at the gate.

「每週英語」每週在學校網站更新一句，並附音檔。句子短是刻意的——重點不是「背」，而是真的去「用」：晨間集會、走去吃午餐、家長來校門口接送的那幾分鐘。
