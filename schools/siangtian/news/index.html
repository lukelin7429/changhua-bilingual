NEWS · 01
From the School & the Paddy
校園動態 ─ 從教室、從稻田、從村子

A school's news is rarely sensational. It is just the year, written in small events — the spring planting, a visiting teacher, the autumn harvest. We collect those moments here, in English and in Chinese.

學校的新聞通常不轟動。那只是「一年」用小事寫成的版本——春耕、外師來訪、秋收。我們在這頁把這些片段集結起來，用英文、用中文，讓稻田之外的世界也能讀到。

Food & Farm · 食農教育 Autumn 2025

Koshihikari Harvest Day at the school paddy
校內水稻生態田 ─ 秋收

Our autumn season of Koshihikari rice came home. With the local rice mill on hand, our children walked into the paddy with banners and sickles. By afternoon, the harvest was loaded into the Kubota and on its way to the mill.

秋季的越光米回家了。在地碾米廠的師傅在現場，孩子帶著手作橫幅與鐮刀走進稻田。下午，稻穀已經上了 Kubota，送往碾米廠。

Food & Farm · 食農教育 Spring 2026

Spring planting, with the ducklings
春耕 ─ 秧苗與小鴨一起入田

While students set the seedlings in rows, ducklings paddle around like quiet supervisors, eating the snails that would otherwise nibble the new roots. The cycle is not just taught — it is in the water around the children's ankles.

孩子在田裡一行一行插秧，小鴨在行列間游動，像一群安靜的小監工，把會啃秧的福壽螺吃掉。生態循環不是用講的——它就在孩子腳邊的水裡。

Community · 校園社群 Year-end

A class portrait in the rice paddy
在水田裡，拍一張全校照

At year-end, the whole school stands together in the paddy — one frame, one season, every child. The field is not next to school. The field is the school.

年末，全校站在稻田裡拍一張合照——一個畫面、一個季節、每一個孩子。農田不在校園旁，農田就是校園。
