Bilingual Announcements — Meet Every School Office in English 校園廣播英語：用英文認識每一個處室

Thirteen short broadcasts — the four offices that look after every Yuhua child, told in everyday school English. 十三集校園廣播：四個照顧育華孩子的處室，用最日常的校園英語介紹給全校學生聽。

For All Yuhua Students · 全校學生適用

Same broadcasts, every school in our network 同一份廣播，網絡內每所學校都聽得到

These short bilingual broadcasts walk every student through what happens inside each school office — Academic Affairs, Student Affairs, General Affairs, and Counselling — in friendly, age-appropriate English. Presented by Sarah Thomas and Susan Rose, the series is designed for morning announcements, English class warm-ups, or any quiet moment when a child is curious about how the school works. 這套校園廣播帶全校學生用簡單英文認識四個處室——教務處、學務處、總務處、輔導室——做什麼、由誰負責。由 Sarah Thomas 與 Susan Rose 主講，適合做為早自習廣播、英文課暖身、或孩子想了解學校怎麼運作的隨選教材。
