English for the Classroom — for Every Yuhua Teacher 給育華每一位老師：實戰課室英語

Ten classroom situations, every phrase you actually use in front of children — from greetings to wrap-ups, in plain English. 十大課堂情境，老師站在孩子面前真正會用到的每一句話——從問候、講解、回饋到收尾，由淺入深。

For In-Service Teachers · 在職教師專用

A teacher-friendly playlist, made for everyday use 給在職老師的日常實用版本

This series is built for teachers who want to use more English in class — without feeling thrown into the deep end. Presenter Sarah Thomas walks through ten everyday classroom situations: greeting and roll-call, getting ready for the lesson, explaining and demonstrating, warm-ups, feedback and praise, classroom management, classroom activities, assigning homework, lesson wrap-ups, and tests. 本系列專為「想多用英文上課，又不想被丟下水」的老師設計。由 Sarah Thomas 主講，逐一示範十個課堂情境：問候點名、課前準備、講解與示範、課前活動、回饋讚美與糾正、教室管理、課堂活動、分派作業、課堂收尾、測驗。
