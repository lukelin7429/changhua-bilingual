Part Three · 參

How We Get There — Three Strategies

經營策略
一

Reading the Needs — Strategic Thinking

分析需求 — 戰略思維
  • S-O Strategy — Connect external resources, organize volunteer support, and co-design curriculum through dialogue. S-O 戰略：鏈結資源、組織志工協助、共同對話及推展課程。
  • S-O-T Strategy — Audit teaching talent, apply for special projects, and build a campus with warmth. S-O-T 戰略：盤點師資、申請專案、營造有溫度的校園。
  • W-T Strategy — Use every occasion to advocate for the school and strengthen teacher–parent relationships. W-T 戰略：運用各式場合、行銷學校並提升親師親職關係。
二

Action Plan — Three Commitments

行動方案
  • Raise the quality of learning — host professional learning communities and run internal curriculum reviews. 提升學習品保：辦理社群及課程校內評鑑。
  • Strengthen core competencies — plan workshops, deepen evaluation of outcomes, and bring the competency-based curriculum to life. 強固本質學能：規劃工作坊、深化成效評估執行、落實素養課程。
  • Activate local resources — build a volunteer team, draw on parents' expertise, and integrate community resources. 活化在地資源：籌組志工團隊、借重家長專長、整合資源推動。
三

Curriculum in Practice — An Ecological Model

課程實踐 — 生態模式的溝通詮釋活動
  • Hold together the four common elements of every lesson — teacher, student, subject content, and environment — and run them as a single ecology. Through this curriculum-in-practice ecological model, our competency-based courses cultivate five capacities in every child: 掌握課程共同要素：教師、學生、科目內容與環境。運用「課程實踐生態模式」於素養課程，打造學生五大力。
  • Learning power · 學習力 · Reading power · 閱讀力 · Caring power · 關懷力 · Action power · 行動力 · Character power · 品格力
Part Four · 肆

Looking Ahead

展望未來
“As the principal goes, so goes the school.” 「有什麼樣的校長，就有什麼樣的學校。」

My hope is to be a principal who is honest and grounded, professional in leadership, and unafraid to open up new and creative ways of running a school — so that every child, watered by love and laughter, can shine in their own way and grow into all they are capable of.

期許自己成為一位真誠踏實、專業領導、開創多元與創新經營的校長，讓每位孩子在愛與歡笑的滋潤下，人人適性揚才、發揮所長。
