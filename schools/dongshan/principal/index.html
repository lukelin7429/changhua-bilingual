A school at the foot of the mountain has two compasses — the hills behind us, and the children walking toward us. Our job at Dongshan is to make sure those two never lose sight of each other: a baseball that still hears Beiguan drums, an English broadcast that still calls home in Taiwanese.

山腳的學校有兩個方位——身後的山，與走來上學的孩子。我們在東山的任務，是讓這兩件事永遠不要彼此走散：讓棒球場上聽得到北管樂聲，讓英語廣播裡仍有台語的故鄉。
