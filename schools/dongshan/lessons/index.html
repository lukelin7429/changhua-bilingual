02 · Foreign Teacher in Residence

Jennifer Chen · Resident Foreign Teacher

駐校外師 · Jennifer Chen 老師
Foreign Teacher · 外師

Meet Jennifer

Jennifer Chen joins Dongshan as our resident foreign English teacher, working alongside our local English staff. She brings the immersive English moments the timetable can't always hold — recess chats, hallway questions, baseball-field small talk.

Jennifer Chen 老師駐校支援東山國小英語教學，與本地英文老師合作。她帶進的是課表上排不進的英語時刻——下課、走廊、球場邊的英文對話。

03 · Partner Recognition

Thank You, Books for Taiwan

感謝 Books for Taiwan 贈書

Dongshan publicly thanks the Books for Taiwan project for its donation of English books — every shelf in our reading corner is a gift from a partner who believed Changhua kids deserved an English library.

東山國小公開感謝 Books for Taiwan 計畫的英文書捐贈——閱讀角每一本書，都是相信彰化孩子值得擁有英文圖書館的夥伴給的禮物。
