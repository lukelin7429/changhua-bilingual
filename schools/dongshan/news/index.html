School Evaluation · Excellent

School Year 114 Evaluation: Excellent

114 學年度校務評鑑「優等」

Dongshan received the top "Excellent" rating in the Changhua County school evaluation for the 114 academic year — across leadership, curriculum, student affairs, and resource management.

本校榮獲彰化縣 114 學年度校務評鑑「優等」——涵蓋校長領導、課程教學、學務輔導、行政資源四大面向。

PaGamO · Distinction

PaGamO Reading Literacy Base · Top Distinction

PaGamO 閱讀素養基地學校 · 特優

Dongshan is one of the few Changhua elementary schools to earn the top "Distinction" grade as a PaGamO Reading Literacy Base School — recognition of how reading is woven into the timetable, not added on top of it.

本校為彰化縣少數獲得「特優」評等的 PaGamO 閱讀素養基地學校——表彰閱讀作為每日課程的一部分，而非附加活動。
