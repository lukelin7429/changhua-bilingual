Bilingual Campus · 雙語校園

English on Campus 課室英語 · 英文廣播

Two free video libraries every teacher and student can use — anytime, on any screen. 兩套跨校共用的雙語影片庫，隨時隨地免費使用

Shared across the Changhua Bilingual Hub 與彰化各雙語學校共用的免費影片資源

These two video libraries were produced by My Culture Connect and are freely shared with every school in the Changhua Bilingual Hub. Classroom English covers the everyday phrases a teacher uses in class; English Announcements introduces each school office in bilingual format — both are designed to make English a natural part of Hosin's campus environment. 課室英語收錄課堂常用語；英文廣播則以雙語介紹學校各行政處室。兩套素材完全免費，作為合興校園雙語環境的日常支持。

Section 01 · 課室英語

Classroom English 課堂上每天都用得到的英文

A growing collection of short video clips covering the phrases teachers use every day — giving instructions, managing the class, encouraging students, and more. Watch a clip before class or project it on screen during the lesson. 這套短影片涵蓋老師課堂每天都會用到的英文指令與互動語句——管理班級、鼓勵學生、給出指示等，可課前預習或直接在課堂投影使用。

Section 02 · 英文廣播

English Announcements 各處室雙語廣播介紹

Short bilingual videos introducing each school office — the Academic Affairs Office, Student Affairs, General Affairs, Library, and more. Great for morning announcements, parent visits, or getting students familiar with the campus in English. 以雙語短片介紹學校各行政處室，包含教務處、學務處、總務處、圖書館等。適合晨間廣播、家長參訪，或讓學生在英文情境中認識校園各功能空間。
