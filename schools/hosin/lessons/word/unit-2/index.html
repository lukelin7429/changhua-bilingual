Word 01
modelling clay (n.)
造型黏土
In art class, we use modelling clay to sculpt animals and buildings.
在美術課，我們用造型黏土捏動物和建築物。
Modelling clay helps students express their creativity with their hands.
造型黏土幫助學生用雙手表達創造力。
Word 02
leave (v.)
離開
Please remember to leave school on time after your club activities.
請記得社團活動結束後準時離開學校。
Don't leave without saying goodbye to your teacher.
離開前別忘了跟老師道別。
Word 03
badminton (n.)
羽球
Badminton is one of the most popular sports at Hosin Elementary.
羽球是合興國小最受歡迎的運動之一。
The badminton team practices on Tuesday and Thursday afternoons.
羽球隊在每週二和週四下午練習。
Word 04
Mother's Day (n.)
母親節
Mother's Day is celebrated on the second Sunday of May every year.
每年五月的第二個週日是母親節。
Students made handmade cards and flowers for their mothers on Mother's Day.
學生在母親節製作手工卡片和花朵送給媽媽。
