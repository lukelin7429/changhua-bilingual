Word 01
mural (n.)
壁畫
The students painted a colorful mural on the school wall.
學生在學校牆上畫了一幅色彩繽紛的壁畫。
The mural tells the story of our village and its history.
這幅壁畫訴說著我們村莊的故事與歷史。
Word 02
jump rope (n.)
跳繩
We practice jump rope every morning before class starts.
我們每天早上上課前練習跳繩。
The teacher asked everyone to bring a jump rope for PE class.
老師要每位同學帶跳繩來上體育課。
Word 03
professional development (n.)
專業進修
Teachers attend professional development workshops to learn new skills.
老師們參加專業進修工作坊學習新技能。
Professional development helps teachers become better educators.
專業進修幫助老師成為更好的教育工作者。
Word 04
make tea (v.)
泡茶
Our school teaches students how to make tea as part of cultural education.
我們學校把泡茶課納入文化教育的一部分。
Making tea is an art that takes patience and practice.
泡茶是一門需要耐心和練習的藝術。
Word 05
workbook (n.)
習作本
Please open your workbook to page ten.
請翻開你的習作本第十頁。
Students complete exercises in their workbook after every lesson.
學生在每堂課後在習作本上完成練習題。
Word 06
sit-up (n.)
仰臥起坐
We do thirty sit-ups every morning to keep our core muscles strong.
我們每天早上做三十個仰臥起坐，保持核心肌群強健。
The sit-up test is part of our annual physical fitness assessment.
仰臥起坐測驗是我們每年體適能評量的一部分。
Word 07
umbrella (n.)
雨傘
Always bring an umbrella when it rains in Changhua.
在彰化，下雨天一定要帶傘。
She forgot her umbrella and got wet walking home from school.
她忘了帶傘，走路回家時被淋濕了。
Word 08
plant (n.)
植物
Our school has an ecological pond with many native plants.
我們學校有一個生態池，裡面有許多原生植物。
Students learn the names of local plants in science class.
學生在自然課學習本地植物的名稱。
Word 09
sit and reach test (n.)
坐姿體前彎測量
The sit and reach test measures how flexible your back and legs are.
坐姿體前彎測量測試你的背部和腿部柔軟度。
Students stretch every week to improve their sit and reach test score.
學生每週做伸展運動，以提升坐姿體前彎測量的成績。
