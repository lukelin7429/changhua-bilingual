II.

Weekly English

每週一句 · 大城國中自製英文短句計畫
English by Dacheng · 大城自製

One phrase. One week. The whole school.

一句話 · 一週時間 · 整個校園

Dacheng's Weekly English Phrase is a school-wide initiative: every week, one English sentence is selected, broadcast through morning announcements, displayed in classrooms, and embedded into the school's daily rhythm. The project lives on the school's English Google Site — an open, ever-growing archive of the phrases that have travelled through Dacheng's corridors, one week at a time. 大城國中「每週一句」是一個全校型計畫——每週挑選一句英文，透過晨間廣播、教室張貼、與課堂活動，把它嵌進校園的日常節奏。所有累積的句子，都放在學校英文 Google Site 上，是大城走過的英語小宇宙。這是「英語不只在課堂裡」這句話的具體版。

III.

School Development Plan

113-118 校務發展計畫 · 校長親自撰寫的六年藍圖
113-118 · 六學年度 · Six School Years

The plan Principal Chao stays for.

校長為它留下來的那份計畫

Principal Chao wrote and presented the 113-118 School Development Plan at the campus meeting of August 29, 2024, and the school council approved it on January 17, 2025. It is the first publicly published document Dacheng has produced under a principal who has committed to staying through to retirement — and it reads like one: concrete, six-years-deep, and tied to specific budget sources for each line item. 本校 113-118 校務發展計畫由校長親自撰寫，於 113 年 8 月 29 日校務會議提報、114 年 1 月 17 日校務會議決議通過。這是大城國中第一份「校長承諾任期內」的公開計畫——具體、跨六學年、每一條都對應到經費來源。我們把它做成一個雙語的閱讀版，讓家長與外部夥伴也能看見大城國中接下來六年要做什麼。
