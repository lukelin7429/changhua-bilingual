I.

About Dacheng

關於大城國中 · 大城鄉唯一一所國中
A small junior high at the windward edge of Changhua — where every child who lives in Dacheng Township eventually comes through these gates.
立於彰化最西南端、面對台灣海峽的偏鄉國中——大城鄉每一位孩子的升學歸宿，都從這裡的校門開始。

Welcome to Dacheng Junior High School, founded in 1964 as the only junior high in Dacheng Township at the southwestern tip of Changhua County. Our students arrive from the three elementary schools that share the township — Dacheng, Sigang, and Meifeng — and spend three years with us before fanning out to senior highs across Changhua and central Taiwan.

大城國中於 1964 年正式設校，是大城鄉唯一一所國中，學生來自鄉內三所國小——大城國小、西港國小、美豐國小。三年國中時光後，他們會升學到彰化各高中職、邁向更廣的世界。

We sit where the wind from the Taiwan Strait meets the end of the Choshui River — what the school's own description calls "a place at the head of the wind and the tail of the water." The geography is honest about who we are: a coastal-rural school that does not pretend to be a city-centre prep academy. What we are is a school that gives every child more than one path forward — academic, vocational, and humanistic, all at the same school gate.

我們所在的位置，是台灣海峽季風的最前沿、濁水溪水的最末端——學校自我描述為「風頭水尾的地方」。地理坦白地說出我們是誰：一所沿海偏鄉學校，不假裝是市中心明星校。但我們做到了一件事——讓每個孩子都有不只一條路可以走，學科、技藝、人文同時在這座校門裡。

"Coastal roots, skilled hands, global minds."
沿海扎根 · 手藝立身 · 放眼世界——大城國中對每一位畢業生的期勉。
II.

Three Anchors

三大發展主軸 · 從地理、雙手、視野三條軸線扎根

Rather than chase a single shining metric, Dacheng anchors itself on three parallel commitments. Each one matches a real fact about our place and our students.

我們不追逐單一亮眼指標，而是以三條相互平行的承諾為錨——每一條都對應「我們在哪裡」與「我們的孩子是誰」這兩個真實的問題。

ANCHOR 01
🌊
Coastal Roots
沿海扎根 · 在地連結
Our place teaches before we do. The Choshui River outlet, the Strait wind, the rice paddies and watermelon fields — these are not background scenery. They are the curriculum's first chapter. The school's Living Water Pavilion library (2020) makes that explicit: an ocean-themed reading space where books and geography meet at the door.
ANCHOR 02
🔧
Skilled Hands
手藝立身 · 技藝教育
Since 2017, Dacheng has run its own vocational electrical-arts program — a rarity among rural junior highs in Taiwan. Ninth-graders who learn better with their hands get a real path: indoor wiring, electronic applications, painting and decoration. They are not "the rest" — they are competitors at the county and national skills championships, and they win.
ANCHOR 03
🌐
Global Minds
放眼世界 · 國際視野
Principal Chao Shih-Ying's guiding principle calls explicitly for "weaving local and international elements into the curriculum." The bilingual campus you see on this site is one of the first concrete steps in that direction — built with My Culture Connect (人師教育協會) for every Dacheng student to grow up with two languages in the air, not just one on paper.
"Education has nothing to do with category — only with fit."
「有教無類，因材施教。」
—— 趙士瑩校長辦學理念第一條
III.

Skilled Hands Spotlight

水電技藝班 · 大城國中最稀有的一支
Self-Run Vocational Program · 自辦水電技藝班

A path beyond the entrance exam.

學測之外，還有一條真實的路。

Most junior highs in Taiwan funnel every student toward the same senior-high entrance exam. Dacheng made a different choice. Since 2017, we have run our own vocational electrical-arts program in-house — indoor wiring, electronic applications, painting and decoration — for ninth-graders whose hands are smarter than the standard textbook gives them credit for. They learn with industry-standard tools, sit county and national skills competitions, and bring home placements. It is the most concrete answer Dacheng has to the question: "What if my child does not want to take the same road as everyone else?" 台灣絕大多數的國中，把所有學生都導向同一條「會考升學」的路。大城選擇不同。自 2017 年起，我們自辦水電技藝班——室內配線、電子應用、漆作裝潢——給那些「手比書本聰明」的九年級孩子。他們用業界規格的工具學習，下場參加縣賽與全國技能競賽，並名列前茅。對於「如果我的孩子不想跟大家走同一條路」這個問題，水電技藝班是大城最具體的回答。

IV.

Featured Programs

特色課程與校園 · 三大主軸的具體實踐
📚
The Living Water Pavilion
活水軒 · 海洋主題閱讀空間
In 2020, Dacheng transformed its library into the Living Water Pavilion — an ocean-themed reading room that makes the school's coastal geography part of every quiet afternoon. A donated English-book collection along one wall puts imported reading in the hands of rural students at no cost.
2020 年，大城國中將圖書室改建為「活水軒」——一個海洋主題閱讀空間，把學校沿海的地理感放進每個閱讀的午後。一面捐贈書牆讓沿海偏鄉的孩子，也能讀到美國同齡孩子讀的英文書。
📖 教育部閱讀推手 · 津琳老師個人獎
☀️
Solar Campus & Green School
太陽能光電校園 · 四葉綠色學校
A full solar photovoltaic system covers our roofs, completed in 2016. The campus is a four-leaf certified Green School with the Ministry of Education — built on a partnership with the Tzu Chi Foundation for resource recycling. Sustainability is not a single class. It is the roof above every other class.
2016 年完工的太陽能光電系統覆蓋校園屋頂，是學校永續教育的具體存在。我們是教育部綠色學校網的四葉認證學校（深 2 葉 + 淺 2 葉），長期與慈濟基金會合作資源回收，並推動「環保酵素搶救地球」與「城中環保義工」等專案。
🌱 教育部綠色學校 4 葉認證
🏆
Educational Priority Zone · Grade A
教育優先區評鑑 A 等
In October 2025, Dacheng was awarded the top tier — Grade A — in the Ministry of Education's Educational Priority Zone evaluation. The evaluation looks at whether a rural school is doing right by students whose families do not have the resources of city schools. For Dacheng, that meant being recognised for what we already do every day.
2025 年 10 月，大城國中獲評為教育部教育優先區評鑑最高等級 A 等。這個評鑑檢視的，是一所偏鄉學校是否真正為「沒有都市資源支撐」的孩子做了該做的事。大城被肯定的，是日常已經在做的工作。
🅰️ 2025-10 教育優先區評鑑 A 等
🗣️
Languages — Mandarin, Taiwanese, English
語文競賽 · 國語 / 台語 / 英文同時進場
Coastal does not mean monolingual. Dacheng students compete county-wide in Taiwanese recitation (a recent county silver), Mandarin reading and writing, and English programs through resources like the Ministry's Cool English platform. The same school where you'd hear hammers in the afternoon, you'd hear three languages in the morning.
沿海，不等於單語。大城學生在縣賽台語朗讀（近期榮獲縣賽第二名）、國語讀寫、英文教材（教育部 Cool English）三個語言場域同時表現。早上聽得到三種語言、下午聽得到鎚子敲打——這是大城校園真實的聲響地圖。
🥈 115 年度語文競賽 · 台語朗讀第二名
"Though the school is small, every organ functions — and reaches out to the township that surrounds it."
「麻雀雖小，五臟俱全」——大城國中的自我描述，也是這所學校在大城鄉中心、緊鄰鄉公所、農會、大城國小的真實位置。

Our Story

校史時間軸 · 從二林初中分部到自辦技藝校
1960
Founded as a branch of Erlin Junior High
民國 49 年 12 月 27 日，奉縣府令成立「二林初中大城分部」，由戴丁順先生負責籌備。
1961
First class admitted — opens at Dacheng Elementary
民國 50 年 7 月招收第一屆新生兩班，9 月 18 日借大城國小校舍開課。
1964
Officially established as Dacheng Junior High
民國 53 年 2 月 1 日正式設校，由張芸亭先生擔任第一任校長。7 月第一屆畢業生 59 人。
2012
First-phase new campus completed
一期校舍竣工、二期工程動工——校園進入大整建週期。
2014
50th anniversary & new campus opening
民國 103 年 1 月 4 日舉行創校 50 週年校慶暨新校舍啟用典禮。
2016
Solar photovoltaic system completed
校園太陽能光電系統完工——為大城走向「永續校園」奠基。
2017
Self-run vocational electrical-arts program opened
自辦水電技藝班開設——成為彰化偏鄉國中少數有自辦技藝班的學校。
2019
Covered athletic court completed
風雨球場完工，所有球類活動有了不受沿海風雨干擾的主場。
2020
Library reborn as the Living Water Pavilion
圖書室改建為「活水軒」——海洋主題閱讀空間，是大城閱讀教育的新地標。
2021
Principal Chao Shih-Ying takes office
民國 110 年 8 月 1 日，第 12 任校長趙士瑩先生上任（國立暨南國際大學教政系博士）。
2025
Educational Priority Zone Grade A · Reading Master national award
10 月教育優先區評鑑 A 等；津琳老師獲教育部閱讀推手個人獎。
