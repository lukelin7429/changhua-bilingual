Achievement · 校務評鑑

Educational Priority Zone — Grade A教育優先區評鑑 A 等

October 17, 2025 · 2025 年 10 月 17 日

The Ministry of Education's Educational Priority Zone evaluation looks at whether a rural school is doing right by students whose families do not have the resources of city schools. Dacheng was awarded the top tier — Grade A.

教育部教育優先區評鑑檢視的，是一所偏鄉學校是否真正為「沒有都市資源支撐」的孩子做了該做的事。大城國中被評為最高等級 A 等——是日常已經在做的工作被外部肯定。

National Skills · 全國技能

4th in Central Taiwan, Painting & Decoration王芃瑋同學 · 全國技能競賽青少年組漆作裝潢中區第四

2025–2026 school year · 114 學年度

A Dacheng ninth-grader from the school's self-run vocational electrical-arts program placed 4th in the Painting & Decoration division at the National Skills Championships (Junior Group, Central Region) — proof that a rural junior high can put a student on a national workbench, against the best in central Taiwan.

大城國中三年級王芃瑋同學，來自學校自辦的水電技藝班，於全國技能競賽青少年組漆作裝潢項目中區奪下第四名——一所沿海偏鄉國中，把自己的學生送上全國工作台，跟中部最強的對手同場競技。

Source: School honor roll · 大城國中榮譽榜

Teacher Honor · 教師榮譽

Ministry of Education Reading Master津琳老師 · 教育部閱讀推手個人獎

2025 school year · 114 學年度

The MOE Reading Master award goes each year to teachers who have measurably moved the needle on how much, and how deeply, students read. Teacher Chin-Lin (津琳老師) earned the individual award — recognition for the quiet years of work behind Dacheng's Living Water Pavilion.

教育部閱讀推手個人獎，每年頒給確實讓學生「讀更多、讀得更深」的老師。大城國中津琳老師獲頒個人獎——對「活水軒」海洋主題閱讀空間背後，那些安靜累積的歲月，最具體的肯定。

Source: School announcements · 大城國中公告系統

Languages · 縣賽語文

Silver in Taiwanese Recitation115 年度語文競賽 · 台語朗讀第二名

2025-2026 school year · 115 年度

A seventh-grader (Class 201) carried Dacheng to a 2nd-place finish in Taiwanese recitation at the county language competition. In a school that often gets framed as "small and rural," a county silver in mother-tongue language is the kind of medal that matters most — it says this kid sounds like home, on a stage.

大城國中七年級（201 班）一位同學，在縣賽語文競賽台語朗讀組榮獲第二名。對一所常被框架為「偏鄉小校」的學校來說，母語朗讀的縣賽銀牌特別有意義——它告訴你：這個孩子站到舞台上，聲音依然有家鄉的味道。

County Skills · 縣賽技藝

Indoor Wiring 4th, Electronic Applications 6th縣賽技藝競賽 · 室內配線第四、電子應用第六

2025-2026 school year · 114 學年度

Two ninth-graders from the vocational arts program placed in the county technical-arts competition: 4th in Indoor Wiring and 6th in Electronic Applications. The pipeline from the after-school workshop to the county competition floor is no longer a hopeful experiment — it is repeatable.

兩位三年級的水電技藝班學生，在縣賽技藝競賽中入榜：室內配線組第四名、電子應用組第六名。從放學後的工坊，到縣賽競技場——這條路線在大城國中已經不是一次性的實驗，而是可被複製的常態。

Adaptive Sports · 適應體育

Three Wins on the County Track縣賽適應體育田徑 · 大城三位學生獲獎

2025-2026 school year · 114 學年度

At the county adaptive-sports athletics meet, three Dacheng students — one from each grade — brought home placings in softball throw and the 60-metre dash. Across the seventh, eighth, and ninth grades, the same school showed up to win.

在縣賽適應體育田徑賽中，大城國中三位學生——七、八、九年級各一位——在壘球擲遠與 60 公尺短跑項目奪牌。同一所學校，跨越三個年級，都站上獎台。
