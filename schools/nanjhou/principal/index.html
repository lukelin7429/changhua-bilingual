Yang Xun-Fei楊巽斐 校長

A scientist-educator with three previous principalships in Changhua's smallest schools — Tuku, Lianxing, Xinmin — and a Ph.D. in Science Education from National Changhua University of Education. She returned to a small rural school not because it is small, but because "a small school is where every child is seen."
楊校長是科學教育出身的偏鄉小校經營者——博士學位來自彰化師大科教所，前三任校長職都在彰化縣的小校（土庫、聯興、新民），2022 年 8 月接任南州。她選擇繼續走小校這條路，不是因為「小」，而是因為「在小學校，每一個孩子都會被看見」。

II.

Education Philosophy

教育理念 · 科學的心、教育的眼、自然的腳
🧭 A Three-Point Compass · 三足羅盤
"Curious eyes, careful hands, kind hearts."
「好奇的眼，謹慎的手，溫柔的心。」
—— 楊巽斐校長 · A scientist-educator's compass for a small forest school

At Nan-Jhou, a small school under a hundred-year banyan, the principal's compass has three legs.
在這所百年老榕守護下的小校裡，校長的羅盤有三隻腳。

🔬
Curious Eyes
好奇的眼 · 科學家的眼
A science PhD's habit: ask why before what to do. Children copy what the school takes seriously — and at Nan-Jhou, we take questions seriously.
科教博士的習慣——先問「為什麼」，再決定「怎麼做」。孩子模仿的是「學校認真對待的事」，南州認真對待的是提問。
🌱
Careful Hands
謹慎的手 · 教育者的手
A plant takes years to root. So does a child. Three previous principalships in tiny Changhua schools taught us that the slow way is the only way.
一棵植物要花好幾年扎根；一個孩子也是。在三所偏鄉小校累積的校長經驗告訴我們：慢，是唯一的快。
🦜
Kind Hearts
溫柔的心 · 自然的心
As a standing supervisor of the Changhua Wild Bird Society, the principal teaches by example: be gentle with what's smaller than you. The banyan, the birds, the seventy-six children.
身為彰化縣野鳥學會常務監事，校長以身作則：對比自己小的，要輕——老榕、鳥群、與全校七十六個孩子，都是這條原則的對象。

IV.

Beyond the Office

校長辦公室以外 · 學會、評審、講師三線並進

A small-school principal serves a wider Changhua too. Principal Yang's after-hours work spans wild birds, science fairs, and after-school teacher training — each one feeding back into what Nan-Jhou's classrooms can become.
小校校長的時間，往往也屬於整個彰化。楊校長的「下班後角色」分別在野鳥、科展、課後師資三條線上——每一條都會回饋到南州的教室。
