I.

Bilingual Campus

校園英語 · 怎麼在南州過一天雙語生活

A small school, two languages, three rhythms — Wisdom, Cheerful, Joy.

一所小學校，兩種語言，三個節奏——Wisdom（智慧）、Cheerful（喜悅）、Joy（安心）。

At Nanjhou, bilingual is not a subject. It is the time of day. Morning English at sunrise, classroom English between bells, bilingual announcements at noon, English Club after school, and a HootHub calendar that runs in the background all day. 在南州，雙語不是「一門課」，而是「一天的時辰」。清晨的晨間美語、上下課之間的課室英語、午間的雙語廣播、放學後的英語社團，加上一份從早到晚都在跑的 HootHub 行事曆。

III.

A Day in Two Languages

雙語的一天 · 三個時段
🌅
Morning English & English Club
晨間美語 + 英語社團 · 開場與收尾
The day opens with Anabelle's Morning English — short, joyful, one-word-you-will-use-today. After school, the English Club meets in the HootHub corner of the library, where games, picture books, and small drama exercises run the room.
一天從 Anabelle 的「晨間美語」開場——簡短、開心、收斂在一個「你今天就會用到的字」。放學後，「英語社團」在圖書室的 HootHub 角落聚會，用遊戲、繪本與短劇練習收尾。
📣
Bilingual Announcements
雙語廣播 · 在校園裡走動的英文
Every term, our teachers and student helpers rehearse bilingual announcements — short, factual, two-language daily updates from each department. We reuse the shared MCC Bilingual Announcements playlist (Sarah Thomas & Susan Rose) as a model and pattern.
每學期，老師與學生助理會輪流錄製「雙語廣播」——四個處室的日常公告，用兩種語言、最短句長、最高頻率。我們直接沿用人師（MCC）共用 playlist《Bilingual Announcements》（Sarah Thomas & Susan Rose 主講）為腳本範本。
🧭
Classroom English for Teachers
課室英語 · 給老師的內部研習
Bilingual learning is not just the FET's job. All Nanjhou teachers — homeroom, science, art — use the shared Classroom English playlist by Sarah Thomas as a daily PD reference. Greetings, transitions, instructions, praise, correction, classroom management: ten situations, one common script.
「雙語」不是外師一個人的事。南州所有老師——導師、自然、藝術——都把人師共用 playlist《Classroom English》（Sarah Thomas 主講）當作日常進修的範本。問候、過場、講解、讚美、糾正、班級經營，十大情境，一份共通腳本。
IV.

Classroom English

課室英語 · 給在職教師的十大情境英文

A complete classroom-English library by Sarah Thomas, produced by My Culture Connect for partner schools across Changhua. Ten situations every teacher needs — from greetings to giving feedback to managing a classroom. 由 Sarah Thomas 老師主講、人師教育協會製作，給彰化雙語合作校全體在職教師使用的「課室英語」完整影音庫。十大情境，從問候到糾正到班級經營。
