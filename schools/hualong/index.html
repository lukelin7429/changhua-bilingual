About Hualong

A small school in clear-water country

秀水鄉的一所小學校
Faculty
School-evaluation honors, 2024 113 學年度校務評鑑優等
Our campus on Huaxiu Road 花秀路上的華龍校園

Where every child meets the world beyond the classroom.

Founded in 1966 as a branch of Xiushui Elementary and made independent in 1971, Hualong Elementary serves the rural village of Huaxiu Road in Xiushui Township, Changhua County. The township's old name, Choushuei (literally "stinking water"), was renamed Xiushui ("clear water") during the Qing Jiaqing era — a hopeful turn we still carry.

Today Hualong is small in size but rich in stories: a Beiguan ensemble built by fifth-graders, a principal who plays guitar at Mother's Day, and a school-wide commitment to walking out of the classroom and learning from real soil, real factories, real people.

華龍國小於民國 55 年（1966）成立秀水國小華龍分校，民國 60 年（1971）獨立設校，地處秀水鄉花秀路。秀水鄉古名「臭水」，清嘉慶年間改名「秀水」，意指與濁水溪交會的清水——一個帶著希望的命名。

學校規模不大，卻有自己的厚度：五年級孩子自發組成的北管樂團、母親節彈吉他獻唱的校長、走出教室向真實土地與產業學習的戶外課程。我們是 114 學年度通過教育部認證的「永續發展與戶外教育領導學校」。

三條河流，一所學校

Reading lights the inner life. Outdoor learning extends it. Beiguan music carries it home through the strings of our grandparents' songs.

Our official Facebook page is named Hualong's Love-Reading Broadcasting Station — and that is no accident. Reading is the quiet engine of everything else here.

學校 FB 粉專就叫「華龍愛閱點播站」——閱讀是這所學校所有事情的安靜引擎。

帆

Outdoor Learning 戶外教育揚帆學校

Hualong is officially certified by Taiwan's Ministry of Education as a "Sailing-Flag School" for outdoor and sustainable learning — a national badge for schools that carry students past the classroom walls.

通過教育部「永續發展與戶外教育領導學校」（揚帆學校）認證——把孩子帶離教室的學校，才能掛這面旗。

雅

Beiguan Strings 北管雅樂

Beiguan is a centuries-old Taiwanese folk music tradition — a UNESCO-recognized intangible cultural heritage. At Hualong, fifth-graders carry it forward in their own student-led ensemble.

北管是台灣百年雅樂傳統，國家無形文化資產。在華龍，五年級孩子自發成團，把這個傳統繼續往下傳。

School Signature

The fifth-grade Beiguan band

五年級的小小絲竹團

A two-act story of how children build a music tradition with their own hands.

A two-act story

From practice room to stage in two lessons. 兩堂課從練習室走上舞台

Every Tuesday lunchtime, the Beiguan club's silk-and-bamboo strings come alive in the music room. This spring, a group of fifth-graders walked in on the first week of school and told their teacher: "We want to play at the Children's Day class show." Two lessons later, they had assembled a small ensemble. They were quietly practicing on their own.

每週二中午是北管社團孩子們絲弦樂器分組課的時間。這個春天，五年級的同學在剛開學就跟老師討論演出內容、樂器分配，短短兩次上課就組成了一個小小絲竹團，能演奏出北管絃譜〈春串〉——他們私底下，肯定有自行加練。
