A new chapter for an old little school 一所老學校的新章節

The 7th principal of a school that began as a branch and grew into its own.

In August 2025, Hong Wei-zhen took office as the 7th principal of Hualong Elementary — a school that opened in 1966 as a branch of nearby Xiushui Elementary, gained its independence in 1971, and has been quietly serving the families of Huaxiu Road ever since.

Hualong is a small school. We have no famous alumni, no flagship campus, no glossy marketing. What we do have is a school song you can sing on a bicycle ride home, a music room where fifth-graders teach themselves Beiguan in the cracks of the lunch hour, a Mother's Day where the principal shows up with his guitar, and — as of March 2026 — a county champion's table-tennis trophy on the office shelf.

Principal Hong's first year is about one quiet promise: that a small school can still raise children with full hearts, strong bodies, and curious minds — and that the way you do it is by walking the talk yourself.

洪維振校長於 2025 年 8 月（民國 114 學年度）就任華龍國小第七任校長。華龍國小於 1966 年由秀水國小華龍分校起家，1971 年獨立設校，五十餘年來靜靜守護著秀水鄉花秀路的孩子們。

我們是一所小學校，沒有顯赫的校友、沒有亮麗的招牌、沒有華麗的行銷話術。我們有的，是一首騎腳踏車回家路上會哼的校歌、一間五年級孩子在午休空檔自己組起北管樂團的音樂教室、一個會抱著吉他出席母親節活動的校長——還有，自 2026 年 3 月起，校長辦公桌上多了一座縣級桌球冠軍獎盃。

洪校長到任的第一年，給華龍一個安靜的承諾：小學校也能養出心、身、智都飽滿的孩子——而做到這件事的方法，是自己先走在前頭。

Exercise isn't a slogan. It's a way of life.
運動不只是口號，而是生活態度。
— Principal Hong Wei-zhen, after winning the Changhua County 114th Education Cup Table Tennis Championship, March 18, 2026

Three commitments to your children 給孩子的三個承諾

Health, Music, and the World Outside.

健

A healthy school 健康華龍

The principal walks his talk on the table-tennis court — and you can see the school follow. Daily exercise habits, regular sport, and a quiet but consistent message: a strong body holds a clear mind.

校長在桌球桌上身教，孩子在校園裡跟上。規律運動、培養動態生活——強健的身體裝得下清明的腦袋。

樂

A musical school 音樂華龍

From the principal's guitar at Mother's Day to the fifth-graders' student-led Beiguan ensemble, music at Hualong isn't a class — it's a quiet daily soundtrack. The kind that follows you home.

從母親節的校長吉他，到五年級孩子自發組成的北管樂團——音樂在華龍不是一堂課，是日常的安靜配樂，會跟著孩子回家。

遠

A school that walks out 看見世界的華龍

As a Ministry-of-Education-certified Sailing-Flag School for outdoor learning, we believe textbooks finish the sentence that real fields, real factories, and real conversations begin.

通過教育部「揚帆學校」認證——我們相信課本只是把真實土地、真實產業、真實對話的話題說完。要把句子說完整，得走出去。
