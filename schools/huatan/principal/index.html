An Outsider Who Came Home 外來的校長，回到自己的小鎮

Principal Chan Hsueh-mei was appointed to Huatan Elementary in August 2024, transferring from Wenkai Elementary — another well-respected school in Changhua County, where she had served as principal for several years. She arrived at a meaningful moment: just months before the school completed its NT$150 million renovation, marking 111 years of continuous education in Huatan Township.

She is, in a sense, "an outsider who came home" — new to this particular school, but deeply familiar with Changhua's elementary-school landscape, its rural-and-suburban rhythms, and its quiet expectation that a principal is also a daily presence among the children.

詹雪梅校長於 2024 年 8 月接任花壇國小校長，自彰化縣文開國小校長一職調任而來。她接任時，正逢花壇國小邁入 111 年校慶，1.5 億元校舍翻新工程即將落成的關鍵時刻。

她可以說是「外來的、又像在地」的校長——新進這所學校，但對彰化縣城鄉小學的節奏、文化、與「校長要每天出現在孩子身邊」這件事，都已熟悉。

What Visitors Notice 第一次來的訪客，看見的事

In April 2026, citizen journalist Chou Hou-hsien arrived at the school gate at 7 a.m. to cover Huatan Elementary's annual New Student Registration Day. The reporter expected the standard scene — a registration desk, name tags, an opening speech. What he later wrote about, in his published report, was the principal herself, carrying chairs and setting up the welcome stations alongside the teachers.

Three Things She Cares About 校長辦學的三個重點

Drawn from her interviews and from how Huatan's calendar of activities has taken shape over the past school year.

歸納自校長受訪內容、以及這一學年花壇國小的活動安排。

課

A Stage for Every Child 每個孩子都有舞台

From orchestra and taiko to basketball, soccer, hot-dance, and the school's own Star Cup talent show, Huatan offers more than 20 student clubs. The principal speaks about this often: a public school's job is not only to teach — it is to find the one thing each child can shine at.

從管弦樂團、太鼓，到籃球、足球、熱舞、校內「星光盃」才藝展演——花壇國小有 20 多個社團。校長常談到：公立小學的工作不只是教書，更是幫每個孩子找到他能發光的那一件事。

護

Holding the First-Graders 把一年級的孩子接好

The transition from kindergarten into elementary school is, for a six-year-old, the biggest jump of their young life. Principal Chan has emphasized that Huatan's first-grade teachers are chosen for patience as much as for pedagogy — and that the first weeks of school matter more than any single curriculum unit.

從幼兒園進入小學，對一個六歲的孩子來說，是他人生第一次最大的跨越。詹校長強調，花壇國小一年級的師資，挑選時看重耐心，與教學專業同等重要——而開學的頭幾週，比任何一個課程單元都更值得用心。

在

Rooted in Huatan 扎根在花壇

Huatan's identity comes from very specific things — the jasmine fields, the brick kilns, the Mazu pilgrimage that walks past the school each spring. Under Principal Chan, these are not heritage decorations. They are class trips, partnerships, and the reason the school steps out into the rain to welcome a goddess that the village has welcomed for a century.

花壇的特色，來自具體的事物：茉莉花田、紅磚窯、每年春天經過校門的大甲媽祖。在詹校長帶領下，這些不是用來裝飾「歷史感」的元素，而是真實的課外踏查、社區合作，與在大雨中走出校門迎接媽祖的理由——一件這個村莊已經做了一百年的事。

Come Visit 歡迎蒞校交流

Educators, parents, and friends from Taiwan and abroad are warmly welcome to visit Huatan Elementary. We are particularly interested in conversations about bilingual education, community-rooted curriculum, and how a 113-year-old public school stays young.

歡迎國內外教育工作者、家長與友人蒞校交流。我們特別期待和您聊聊雙語教育、社區共學課程，以及一所創校 113 年的公立小學，如何持續保有年輕的活力。
