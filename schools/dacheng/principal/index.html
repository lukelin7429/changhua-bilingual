IV.

Six Pillars Charter

六大校訓 · 校務藍圖

Principal Chen's charter rests on six interlocking values. Each is a verb in disguise: protect, read, enjoy, sustain, connect, build.
陳校長的校務藍圖由六個互鎖的價值構成，每一項都是動詞——保護、閱讀、樂於學、永續、連結、建造。

PILLAR 01
🛡️
Safety
安全
A campus where every child knows how to protect themselves and others — built on the W.H.E.A.T. disaster curriculum that earned us the MOE Seed School in 2024.
PILLAR 02
📚
Reading
愛閱
Reading rituals from grade one — building both vocabulary and the habit of finishing what you start. Books open doors that geography cannot close.
PILLAR 03
🎭
Joyful Learning
樂學
Theatre, taiko, north-pipe music, fine arts — learning that is hands-on, expressive, and rooted in joy. County drama 1st place in 2025.
PILLAR 04
🌱
Sustainability
永續
Aligned with the UN SDGs. Caring for land, water, and air is folded into the curriculum — not an extra-curricular bolt-on.
PILLAR 05
🌐
International
國際
A resident foreign teacher, SDGs visits from the UN Association Orange County Chapter, and a new English-and-SDGs partnership with Edward Huang from March 2026.
PILLAR 06
💻
Technology
科技
Recognised as a Changhua County Outstanding Digital Learning School (2024). A four-stage GIGO maker curriculum from spatial reasoning to coding launches in 115 學年度.
"Six pillars, one promise — let every Dacheng child grow to their fullest potential."
「六大支柱，一個承諾——讓每一位大城孩子都能大展長才、成就未來。」
V.

Five Story Arcs

五大敘事弧 · 辦學的五條主線

Six pillars describe what a Dacheng education delivers. The five arcs describe how — each pairing a rooted local strength with a future-facing ambition.
六大支柱說的是「教什麼」，五大敘事弧說的是「怎麼教」——每一條都把「在地的根」與「未來的志」綁在一起。

ARC 01
Reading Roots × Academic Lift
閱讀扎根 × 學力提升
A child who reads daily by grade two carries that habit into Bachelor's-level work. We start with picture books in kindergarten and end with newspaper editorials in grade six — closing the rural-urban literacy gap one quiet morning at a time.
每天閱讀的孩子，會帶著這個習慣走到大學。從幼兒園的繪本到六年級的社論——一個安靜的早讀，一點一點把城鄉識字差距縮小。
ARC 02
Many Clubs × Every Spark
多元社團 × 天賦發光
North-pipe music, theatre, taiko, choir, table tennis, diabolo, fine arts — seven clubs, one for every kind of child. Drama 1st place at county, taiko 1st at the national Heritage Cup.
北管、戲劇、太鼓、合唱、桌球、扯鈴、美術創作——七大社團，每個孩子都能找到屬於自己的舞台。縣賽戲劇第一、全國太鼓第一。
ARC 03
Local Ties × Sustainable Learning
在地連結 × 永續學習
From wheat-planting fields to the community Sea-Flower Festival to the rural fishing villages along the Zhuoshui delta — the village is the syllabus. SDGs aren't theory; they're our backyard.
從小麥種植田、社區花海生活節，到濁水溪出海口的漁村——村落本身就是課綱。SDGs 在我們這裡，不是理論，是家門口。
ARC 04
Everyday Life × World-Facing
生活出發 × 接軌世界
A resident foreign teacher. SDGs visits from the UN Association Orange County Chapter. And from March 2026, Mr. Edward Huang's weekly Monday English & SDGs class. Global classrooms grow from local conversations.
外師駐校、聯合國協會橙縣分會 SDGs 交流、2026 年 3 月起 Edward Huang 老師每週一英語與 SDGs 課程——國際對話，從每天的日常生活開始。
ARC 05
Tech Exploration × Competitive Edge
科技探索 × 競爭給力
A four-stage GIGO maker curriculum launching 115 學年度: spatial reasoning & assembly → gear & mechanics → renewable energy → coding. Each stage hands children a more complex problem and a sharper toolkit.
115 學年度全新推出的智高積木四階段課程：空間數量組裝 → 齒輪力學 → 再生能源 → 程式設計。每一階段都讓孩子面對更複雜的問題、握更利的工具。
"Where you start in Taiwan should not decide where you can go in the world."
「在台灣的起點，不該決定你在世界的終點。」
—— 陳校長談大城孩子的未來
