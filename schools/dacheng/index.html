I.

About Dacheng

關於大城 · 一所百年的鄉中心校
A school known as "the Windy Head and Water End" — at the southwest corner of Changhua County, walking forward with steady, grounded steps.
位於彰化縣西南角、素有「風頭水尾」稱號的鄉中心校——以穩健踏實的步伐，百年而行。

Dacheng Elementary sits at the southwest corner of Changhua County, in a region locally known as "the Windy Head and Water End". With steady, grounded steps, our team has built a campus anchored in six values — Safety, Reading, Joyful Learning, Sustainability, International outlook, and Technology. We accompany every child to learn through experience, to shine in everyday life, and to grow into their fullest potential.

大城國小位於彰化縣西南角的大城鄉，素有「風頭水尾」稱號。我們以穩健踏實的步伐，打造一所「安全、愛閱、樂學、永續、國際、科技」的學習校園，陪伴孩子在體驗中學習、在生活中發光，讓每一位孩子大展長才、成就未來。

Founded in 1915, our school serves the villages of Dacheng, Donguent, Xicheng, Shanjiao, and Tsailiao, with a reach extending into Shangshan, Yonghe, and Gongguan.

創校於 1915 年 4 月，學區涵蓋大城村、東城村、西城村、山腳村、菜寮村，並延伸服務上山村、永和村、公館村。

"Enchanting Dacheng — Journeying Toward a Brighter Future."
「品戀大城・幸福前行」——學校核心精神。

III.

Dacheng Kids For Wheat

W.H.E.A.T. 防災五力 · 學校的招牌課程
Signature Curriculum · 招牌課程
Rising with Resilience
大城起飛 × 品牌深耕：打造韌性校園

Reflecting our local environment, Dacheng has long deepened its work in Disaster Prevention Education and built a campus engineered for resilience. In Year 113 (2024), we received the Ministry of Education's highest honour for advanced disaster prevention schools — the "Seed School" certification. 因應在地環境特色，學校長期深耕防災教育、建置防災校園，更於 113 年榮獲教育部防災校園進階學校最高榮譽——「種子校園」的肯定。

Through our signature school-developed curriculum "Dacheng Kids For Wheat" — a Taiwanese-language play on the local phrase "大城去佗位" ("Where are we going, Dacheng?") — we integrate the four disaster steps Mitigation, Preparedness, Response, and Recovery into seven core learning domains and major issues, and align tightly with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). From early on, our children build the right disaster-response mindset and the resolve to act. 以「Dacheng Kids For Wheat」【大城去佗位】發展防災校訂課程，將「減災、整備、應變、復原」融入七大領域及重大議題，並能扣緊 SDGs 永續發展目標，培養學生防災五力，讓孩子從小培養正確的防災觀念與行動力。

IV.

Five Living Arcs

五大敘事弧 · 校長親述的大城教育版圖
📖
Reading Anchors · Lifting Learning
閱讀扎根 × 學力提升
Through reading classes, newspaper-reading lessons, themed book exhibits, and weekly reading quizzes, we cultivate comprehension, expression, and thinking. At Dacheng, reading is not a tool for tests — it is the key that opens the world.
學校積極推動閱讀教育，透過閱讀課程、讀報教育、主題書展與每週的閱讀測驗，培養學生理解力、表達力與思考力。在大城國小，閱讀不是考試的工具，而是開啟世界的鑰匙。
🥁
Diverse Clubs · Talents That Shine
多元社團 × 天賦發光
We offer a wide range of clubs — Beiguan music, theatre, taiko drumming, music ensemble, table tennis, diabolo, and visual arts — encouraging every child to try, to discover, and to build confidence through art, sport, and culture. Every child deserves to be seen.
大城國小開設多元社團——北管、戲劇、太鼓、音樂、桌球、扯鈴、美術創作等，鼓勵孩子勇於嘗試、發掘興趣，在藝術、體育與文化中建立自信。我們相信，每個孩子都值得被看見。
🥇 114 學年度全縣戲劇舞台劇第一名 · 綜合偶戲第二名 · 躲避球比賽亞軍
🌾
Local Roots · Sustainable Learning
在地連結 × 永續學習
Holding to the spirit of "the more local, the more international", we weave community resources and local features into food & agriculture, art-making, and walking-discovery (走讀) curricula. Learning steps out of the classroom and into life itself.
秉持「越在地，越國際」理念，學校結合社區資源與在地特色，發展食農、藝創與走讀課程。在大城國小，學習走出教室、走進生活。
⭐ 112 學年度起接管潭墘校區 · 成立【彰化縣特色遊學中心】
112 學年度起接管潭墘校區，成立【彰化縣特色遊學中心】——除了負責全縣遊學課程的行政運作，也同步規劃在地遊學體驗課程，培養孩子關懷家鄉、尊重環境、面向未來的能力。
🌍
From Daily Life to the World
生活出發 × 接軌世界
We believe English learning must take root early. Through life-based, situational English teaching, our students naturally open their mouths to speak. We actively recruit resident foreign teachers (Teacher Alyssa currently leads our daily conversation classes) and combine English curriculum with themed activities and rich resources — so that rural children, too, hold the language confidence to walk toward the world.
大城國小重視英語教育向下扎根，透過生活化、情境式的英語教學，讓孩子在日常學習中自然開口說英語。學校積極申請外師駐校（目前由 Alyssa 老師帶領日常對話課程），結合英語課程、主題活動與多元教學資源，讓偏鄉孩子同樣擁有走向世界的語言自信。
💻
Tech Exploration · Competitive Edge
科技探索 × 競爭給力
We promote digital self-learning to meet the needs of digital-native students. In Year 115, we introduce a Gigo Bricks curriculum structured in four progression stages — spatial & quantitative assembly → gear mechanics → renewable energy → programming — letting students think, build, and solve in the classroom and grow into the technology talents of tomorrow.
因應數位原民的學習，學校積極推動數位自學。115 年並引入智高積木課程，規劃 4 階段學習進程——空間數量組裝 → 齒輪力學 → 再生能源 → 程式設計，讓學生在課堂中學會思考、動手做、解決問題，培養未來優質的科技人才。

"Dacheng Elementary — accompanying every child's childhood with care."
「大城國小，用心陪伴每一個孩子的童年。」
在這裡，孩子學會防災素養保護自己與家人、愛上閱讀適性學習、適性探索天賦發光、愛戀故鄉關懷世界、具備國際移動與科技競合力。
V.

Our Story

校史時間軸 · 百年而行
1915
Founded as Erlin Public School Dacheng Branch
創校之初為「二林公學校大城厝分校」。
1918
Became an independent school: Dachengcuo Public School
獨立為「大城厝公學校」。
1925
Renamed Dacheng Public School
改名為「大城公學校」。
1945
Renamed Dacheng National School after WWII
戰後改稱「大城國民學校」。
1968
Renamed Dacheng Elementary School with the launch of nine-year compulsory education
配合九年國民義務教育，更名為「大城國民小學」，沿用至今。
2023
Took over Tanqian Campus, established the Changhua Special Excursion Centre
112 學年度起接管潭墘校區，成立【彰化縣特色遊學中心】，承擔全縣遊學課程的行政與在地遊學體驗規劃。
2024
Recognised as a Ministry of Education "Seed School" for Disaster Prevention
榮獲教育部防災校園進階學校最高榮譽——「種子校園」認證。
2024
Distinguished Digital Learning School (Year 113), Changhua County
獲彰化縣 113 學年度「數位學習績優學校」認證。
2025
Principal Chen Chi-chuan (陳啟川) takes office
陳啟川校長於 2025 年 8 月接任。
2026
Gigo Bricks 4-stage technology curriculum launched
115 年引入智高積木課程，規劃「空間數量組裝 → 齒輪力學 → 再生能源 → 程式設計」4 階段學習進程。
