Hear From Our Children

學生英文自我介紹 · 31 個孩子，每人一分鐘
📺 31 student intros · 學生園地 1 部 · 共 32 部影片

Thirty-one Sijhou children introduce themselves in English — one at a time, one minute each. At Sijhou, learning English isn't about the perfect sentence — it's about finding the courage to open your mouth. 31 位溪州孩子親自用英文自我介紹——每人一分鐘，一個個來。在溪州，學英文不是要學一句最完美的句子，而是練習打開嘴巴的勇氣。
