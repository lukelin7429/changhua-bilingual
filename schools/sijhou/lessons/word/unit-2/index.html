Reading & Learning

閱讀、學習與考試

Word 01
reading (n)
閱讀
Sijhou students do silent reading every morning.
溪州學生每天早上靜默閱讀。
Reading is the L in our Sijhou L.I.F.E. curriculum.
閱讀就是溪州 L.I.F.E. 的 L。
Word 02
storytelling (n)
講故事
We practice storytelling in English class.
我們在英文課練習講故事。
Good storytelling uses voice, hands, and eyes.
好的講故事要用聲音、手與眼神。
Word 03
storytelling contest (n)
說故事比賽
Sijhou holds a school storytelling contest every year.
溪州每年都舉辦校內說故事比賽。
I won second place in the storytelling contest.
我在說故事比賽得了第二名。
Word 04
read aloud competition (n)
朗讀比賽
I practiced for the English read aloud competition for a month.
我為英文朗讀比賽練習了一個月。
In a read aloud competition, clear pronunciation matters.
朗讀比賽中，清楚的發音很重要。
Word 05
journalism (n)
新聞工作
Journalism is the work of reporting the news.
新聞工作就是把新聞報導出來。
Students wrote a school newspaper in their journalism club.
學生在新聞社團寫了一份校刊。
Word 06
hardcover book (n)
精裝書
The library has many beautiful hardcover books.
圖書館有很多漂亮的精裝書。
A hardcover book lasts longer than a paperback.
精裝書比平裝書更耐用。
Word 07
audience (n)
觀眾
The audience clapped at the end of the show.
表演結束時，觀眾鼓掌。
Speak loudly so the audience at the back can hear you.
說大聲一點，讓後排觀眾聽得到。
Word 08
self assessment (n)
自我評量
After each unit, we do a self assessment.
每個單元結束後，我們做自我評量。
Self assessment helps you know what to study next.
自我評量幫你知道下一步要學什麼。
Word 09
online test (n)
線上測驗
We took an English online test on the tablet today.
我們今天用平板做了英語線上測驗。
The online test shows your score right away.
線上測驗會立刻顯示分數。
Word 10
midterm exams (n)
期中考
We have midterm exams in November.
我們的期中考在十一月。
Study a little each day before the midterm exams.
期中考前每天讀一點書。
Word 11
observe (v)
觀察
Scientists observe carefully before they draw a conclusion.
科學家會先仔細觀察，再下結論。
Observe the bean plant and write what changes.
觀察豆子植物，把變化寫下來。
Word 12
height (n)
身高
We measured our height at the nurse's office.
我們在保健室量身高。
She has grown 5 cm in height this year.
她今年身高長高了 5 公分。
Word 13
PaGamo (n)
打Game學
Students play PaGamo to review math.
學生用 PaGamo 複習數學。
PaGamo turns review into a game.
PaGamo 把複習變成遊戲。
Word 14
SEL (n)
社會情緒學習
SEL stands for Social Emotional Learning.
SEL 是「社會情緒學習」的縮寫。
Sijhou teachers learn SEL together every term.
溪州老師每學期一起進修 SEL。
Word 15
professional development (n)
專業進修
Teachers attend professional development workshops.
老師參加專業進修工作坊。
Professional development helps teachers grow.
專業進修幫助老師成長。
