Word 01
prize (n)
獎品
Students won a prize for their hard work.
學生們努力工作，獲得獎品。
First place gets the biggest prize.
第一名可以獲得最大的獎品。
Word 02
breathing light (n)
呼吸燈
The breathing light slowly fades on and off.
呼吸燈會慢慢地亮起又熄滅。
We made a breathing light in science class.
我們在科學課做了一個呼吸燈。
Word 03
remote control toy (n)
遙控玩具
A remote control toy is fun to play with friends.
遙控玩具和朋友一起玩很好玩。
I got a remote control toy car for my birthday.
我生日收到一台遙控玩具車。
Word 04
craft activity (n)
勞作活動
Today's craft activity is making paper lanterns.
今天的勞作活動是做紙燈籠。
I love every craft activity at Sijhou.
我喜歡溪州每一場勞作活動。
Word 05
craft (n)
手工藝品
The students made a craft from recycled paper.
學生用回收紙做了一件手工藝品。
Sijhou students sell their crafts at the school fair.
溪州學生在校慶園遊會賣自己的手工藝品。
Word 06
cup phone (n)
杯子電話
We made a cup phone from two paper cups and a string.
我們用兩個紙杯和一條線做了杯子電話。
A cup phone teaches us how sound travels.
杯子電話讓我們知道聲音怎麼傳遞。
Word 07
dye (v)
染
Students dye cloth with indigo.
學生用藍靛染布。
We can dye a T-shirt using natural plants.
我們可以用天然植物染 T 恤。
Word 08
cyanotype (n)
藍曬
Cyanotype turns leaves into beautiful blue prints.
藍曬會把葉子變成漂亮的藍色印畫。
We made a cyanotype using sunlight.
我們用陽光做了一張藍曬作品。
Word 09
indigo dyeing (n)
藍染
Indigo dyeing is a traditional Taiwanese craft.
藍染是台灣的傳統工藝。
Sijhou students learn indigo dyeing in art class.
溪州學生在美術課學藍染。
Word 10
bubble painting (n)
泡泡水彩畫
Bubble painting creates round, colourful patterns.
泡泡水彩畫會做出圓圓的、彩色的圖案。
We tried bubble painting in art class today.
今天美術課我們嘗試泡泡水彩畫。
Word 11
clay (n)
黏土
Fifth-graders learn to shape clay at Sijhou.
溪州五年級的學生學習用黏土塑形。
We rolled the clay into small balls.
我們把黏土搓成小球。
Word 12
popcorn (n)
爆米花
We eat popcorn while we watch a movie.
我們邊看電影邊吃爆米花。
Popcorn smells great when it is freshly made.
剛做好的爆米花聞起來很香。
Word 13
bake (v)
烘烤
We bake bread in the mud oven once a year.
我們每年用土窯烘烤一次麵包。
Bake the cookies for 10 minutes at 180 degrees.
用 180 度烘烤餅乾 10 分鐘。
Word 14
fire (n)
火
Never touch a fire with your hand.
千萬不可以用手碰火。
We made a small fire with the teacher's help.
我們在老師幫忙下生了一小堆火。
Word 15
colored salt (n)
彩鹽
We layered colored salt into a glass bottle.
我們把彩鹽一層一層裝進玻璃瓶。
Colored salt art is easy and pretty.
彩鹽藝術簡單又漂亮。
Word 16
light experiment (n)
光影實驗
We did a light experiment with mirrors today.
今天我們用鏡子做光影實驗。
A light experiment shows how shadows work.
光影實驗讓我們了解影子怎麼形成。
Word 17
lever (n)
槓桿
A lever helps us lift heavy things.
槓桿幫助我們抬起重物。
The seesaw is a kind of lever.
翹翹板就是一種槓桿。
Word 18
air (n)
空氣
We breathe air every minute.
我們每一分鐘都在呼吸空氣。
Clean air keeps us healthy.
乾淨的空氣讓我們健康。
Word 19
guava orchard (n)
芭樂園
Sijhou is surrounded by guava orchards.
溪州被芭樂園圍繞。
We visited a guava orchard on our field trip.
校外教學時，我們參觀了一座芭樂園。
Word 20
water buffalo (n)
水牛
A water buffalo stood in the rice field.
一隻水牛站在稻田裡。
Long ago, every Sijhou farm had a water buffalo.
很久以前，溪州每一個農場都有一隻水牛。
Word 21
incense (n)
香
People light incense at the temple.
人們在廟裡點香。
The smell of incense reminds me of Lunar New Year.
香的味道讓我想起農曆新年。
Word 22
agriculture class (n)
食農課
In agriculture class, we plant our own vegetables.
食農課上，我們親自種菜。
Agriculture class teaches us where our food comes from.
食農課讓我們了解食物從哪裡來。
Word 23
SDGs (n)
聯合國永續發展目標
SDGs stands for Sustainable Development Goals.
SDGs 是「聯合國永續發展目標」的縮寫。
Sijhou lessons connect with the UN SDGs.
溪州的課程連結聯合國 SDGs。
Word 24
exchange program (n)
交換計劃
Our exchange program with Trinidad Union began in 2025.
我們與 Trinidad Union School 的交換計劃從 2025 年開始。
An exchange program helps children see another country up close.
交換計劃讓孩子近距離看另一個國家。
