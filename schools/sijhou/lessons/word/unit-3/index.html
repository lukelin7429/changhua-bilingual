Word of the Day · Unit 03

Recess & Play

下課與遊戲

Word 01
dodgeball (n)
躲避球
We play dodgeball at recess.
我們下課時玩躲避球。
Sijhou won the county dodgeball tournament.
溪州贏得縣賽躲避球比賽。
Word 02
frisbee (n)
飛盤
Throw the frisbee to your friend.
把飛盤丟給你的朋友。
Frisbee is a great after-school game.
飛盤是很棒的放學後遊戲。
Word 03
swing (v)
盪鞦韆
Children love to swing high.
小朋友喜歡盪鞦韆盪得高高的。
Take turns when you swing on the playground.
在遊樂場盪鞦韆要輪流。
Word 04
compete (v)
比賽
Sijhou teams compete at the county games every year.
溪州校隊每年都在縣賽中比賽。
Do your best, but remember why you compete.
盡力比賽，但別忘了為何而戰。
Word 05
recorder (n)
直笛
I practice the recorder after dinner.
我吃完晚餐後練習直笛。
The school recorder band plays at every ceremony.
校內直笛隊每場典禮都會演奏。
Word 06
balloon (n)
氣球
We blew up balloons for the party.
我們為派對吹氣球。
A balloon flew up into the sky.
一顆氣球飛上天空。
Word 07
board games (n)
桌遊
Rainy days are perfect for board games.
下雨天最適合玩桌遊。
Our class has a shelf of board games.
我們班有一整櫃的桌遊。
Word 08
building blocks (n)
積木
First-graders love playing with building blocks.
一年級的學生很愛玩積木。
We built a tall castle with building blocks.
我們用積木蓋了一座高高的城堡。
Word 09
3D movie (n)
3D電影
We watched a 3D movie on the school trip.
校外教學時我們看了一部 3D 電影。
Put on the glasses before the 3D movie starts.
3D 電影開始前要戴上眼鏡。
Word 10
top (n)
陀螺
He spun the top and it kept going for a minute.
他轉陀螺，陀螺一直轉了一分鐘。
A wooden top is a classic Taiwanese toy.
木陀螺是台灣的經典童玩。
Word 11
blow dart (n)
吹箭
We tried a paper blow dart in art class.
美勞課我們嘗試做紙吹箭。
Aim carefully before you shoot the blow dart.
瞄好目標再吹吹箭。
