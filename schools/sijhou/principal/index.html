II.

Education Philosophy

教育理念 · 愛是圓心，榜樣是半徑
📐 The Fröbel Compass · 福祿貝爾的羅盤
"To educate is simply this — to love, and to set an example."
「教育之道無他，愛與榜樣而已。」
—— Friedrich Fröbel · 鄭麗雅校長辦學核心信念

At Sijhou, the principal's compass has only two coordinates: Love is the centre, and Example is the radius. The first opens the school's heart to every child; the second decides how far that heart can reach.
在溪州，校長的辦學羅盤只有兩個座標：「愛」是圓心，「榜樣」是半徑。前者決定學校會不會把每個孩子當回事，後者決定這份心能走多遠。

❤️
Love
愛 · 圓心
Every Sijhou decision starts from "what does this child actually need?" — not "what's easier to administer?"
每一個校務決定，先問「這個孩子需要的是什麼」，再問「行政上比較好處理的是什麼」。
🧭
Example
榜樣 · 半徑
Teachers model patience and curiosity; the principal models reading and showing up. Children copy what we do, not what we say.
老師示範耐心與好奇心，校長示範閱讀與準時到校。孩子模仿的是我們做了什麼，不是我們說了什麼。
