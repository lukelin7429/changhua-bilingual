Word of the Day

每日一字 · 一天一個英文小詞

Ten short clips. One English word a day. From late November to the last day of the year — quick, daily, easy to keep up with.

One word, one day, one classroom.

Luoqing's Word of the Day series is a daily English habit — recorded as short videos by our students and teachers, dated by the day they were posted. Watch one a day, or binge the whole month. Either way, every minute leaves you holding a new word.

「每日一字」是螺青孩子每天早上的英文小儀式——由師生親自錄製、按日期上架。每天一支、慢慢累積；也可以一次看完一個月——你會帶著一個新詞回家。
