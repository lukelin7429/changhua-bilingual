I.

About Luoqing

關於螺青 · 一所從書院走進童話王國的小學
From a Qing-dynasty academy named "Luoqing," to a fairytale campus where every corner is a lesson.
從清代「螺青書院」之名而來，今天的螺青國小是一座把每個轉角都變成課堂的童話王國。

Welcome to Luoqing Elementary School, a public elementary school in Beidou Township, Changhua County. Our name — Luoqing — was borrowed in 1953 from a Qing-dynasty academy that once stood here, training local scholars in Chinese letters. We carry that name forward as a promise: that a school can love the classics and still imagine like a child.

歡迎來到螺青國小，一所位於彰化縣北斗鎮的公立國民小學。我們的校名「螺青」沿用自清代設於本地的「螺青書院」——一座培育北斗子弟漢學的書院。我們承繼這個名字當作一份承諾：學校可以同時珍視典籍，也允許孩子用童話般的眼光去看世界。

We are best known as a fairytale campus: a Calligraphy Corridor lined with paper-cut kirin, a Poetry Garden where every pillar holds a fill-in-the-blank poem, an Owl Theatre under a banyan tree, a Rainbow Track in four colors, and a Memory Slope painted with characters from Thumbelina, the Tortoise and the Hare, and Jack and the Beanstalk. The campus itself teaches.

我們最廣為人知的特色是「童話王國」校園——翰墨遊廊上的剪紙麒麟、青青詩境上的填字立柱、榕樹下的貓頭鷹劇場、四色彩虹跑道、青青農場上嫦娥奔月與童話彩繪牆。校園本身就在說話、就在上課。

Now in its third year of bilingual education, Luoqing pairs traditional culture with English fluency — dragon dance and recorder ensemble alongside English-medium PE classes and a Tang-poetry singing program. Together they shape children who can read the world in two languages without forgetting where they stand.

進入第三年的雙語教育，螺青讓傳統與英語並肩——舞龍社、直笛社、書法、唱唐詩，與雙語體育課和外師教學一同並進。我們希望養出能用兩種語言讀世界、卻不忘本地土地的孩子。

"Tradition is not a museum — it is a child running across a rainbow track."
「傳統不是博物館——是一個孩子在彩虹跑道上奔跑的身影。」
—— 螺青國小辦學意象
II.

A Campus Like a Fairytale

童話王國校園 · 八個用設計教書的角落

Eight named spaces across the Luoqing campus — each designed so children learn just by walking through.

校園裡有八個被設計過的角落，每一個都是「不開口的老師」——孩子在路過的時候，就把美感、文學、自然、品格學進去了。

🖋️
Calligraphy Corridor
翰墨遊廊
A walkway alive with literary imagery — pillars and walls dressed in paper-cut kirin, brushwork, and Chinese characters. Walk through it and you've already had a lesson.
📜
Green Poetry Garden
青青詩境
A row of pillars under banyan trees — each one a Chinese poem with a missing word. Daily passers-by become daily readers.
🦉
Owl Theatre
貓頭鷹劇場
An open-air stage under the banyan canopy. Tiny owls — symbols of wisdom — perch on the trunk; flowering balls drape overhead like a musical staff.
🌈
Rainbow Track
彩虹跑道
A four-color PU track — blue, white, red, green — that makes morning laps feel like running through a painting.
🌻
Memory Slope
青青農場 · 拾憶坡
A green hill dressed with windmills and storybook murals — Chang'e flying to the moon, the Jade Emperor, fairytales painted in open air.
🌳
Knowledge Tree
知識樹
Painted on the bathroom walls — a tree heavy with fruit labeled astronomy, geography, nature, humanities, fairytales. Even bathrooms teach.
🎼
800 Tile Mosaic
800 片瓷板
Behind the assembly stage — 800 ceramic tiles painted around five themes: strings, recorder, percussion, dance, reading. A wall that sings.
🐆
Sunshine Cheetah
陽光捷豹吉祥物
Our mascot — fast, sun-warmed, watching over the campus. The character every Luoqing child can draw with closed eyes.
III.

Featured Programs

特色課程與社團
🐉
Dragon Dance Club
舞龍社 · 民俗技藝的雙語門面
Our flagship folk-art club — training students in dragon-dance choreography while learning the cultural stories behind it. The team has performed at the "Flowers in Changhua" exhibition (2019) and at the Changhua County 300th-Anniversary School Showcase (2022).
螺青招牌民俗社團——訓練學生民俗技藝、體認傳統文化之美。曾受邀參與 2019 年「花在彰化」溪州公園展演，及 2022 年「彰化縣建縣 300 · 藝揚 300 北斗區學生社團展演」。
🏆 County showcase, 2019 & 2022
🎶
Recorder Ensemble
直笛社 · 校園裡的合鳴
A school-wide tradition — children learn the recorder from low grades, with the ensemble performing at ceremonies, parent visits, and inter-school events. Music as the second language all children share.
螺青全校延續的音樂傳統，從低年級就開始學直笛。直笛社於校內外典禮、親師會、跨校活動演出，是孩子們共享的「第二語言」。
🖌️
Chinese Calligraphy
書法課程 · 與書院之名同源
Calligraphy isn't decoration here — it's curriculum. Every student takes brush in hand. The same characters that line the Calligraphy Corridor walls are the ones our students learn to write themselves.
書法在螺青不是裝飾，是課程。每位學生都會拿筆練字——翰墨遊廊上看到的字，就是孩子們會學著寫的字。這也是與「螺青書院」之名最直接的呼應。
🌙
Tang Poetry Singing
螺青兒女唱唐詩
"Luoqing Children Singing Tang Poems" — a school-wide initiative that turns 1,000-year-old verses into songs. Children memorize the classics not by drilling, but by humming.
「螺青兒女唱唐詩」——把千年前的唐詩變成可以唱的歌。孩子們不是背誦經典，是把經典哼成自己的歌。延續「螺青書院」漢學傳承的當代版本。
🏀
Bilingual PE
雙語體育 · 在場上學英語
Our signature bilingual subject — basketball, running, even calligraphy class taught with English instructions. 31 PE units sequenced from beginner to advanced, plus a foreign English teacher (Teacher Quennie) who walks every campus space.
螺青招牌雙語科——籃球、跑步、甚至書法課都用英語指令進行。雙語體育分 31 個單元由淺入深，搭配外師 Quennie 老師駐校。詳見 課程頁。

V.

Our Story

校史時間軸 · 從 1943 至今的螺青
1943
Founded as a branch of Beidou East National School
民國 32 年 4 月 10 日創校，為「北斗東國民學校分教場」。
1946
Independent — established as Northeast Beidou National School
民國 35 年 2 月 23 日獨立建校，更名為「東北斗國民學校」。首任校長邱秉南。
1953
Renamed Luoqing — borrowing the name of a Qing-dynasty academy
民國 42 年 8 月 1 日，沿用清代「螺青書院」之名，更名為「彰化縣立北斗鎮螺青國民學校」。校名典故的起點。
1968
Renamed Luoqing Elementary with 9-year compulsory education
民國 57 年 10 月 15 日配合九年國教改稱「彰化縣立北斗鎮螺青國民小學」，沿用至今。
2020
Principal Hsu Hsiao-lin takes office (13th principal)
民國 109 年 8 月，許孝麟校長接任第 13 任校長。
2023
80th Anniversary celebration · launch of bilingual sub-site
民國 112 年，創校 80 週年校慶；雙語子網站 luoqing.mycultureconnect.org 上線，雙語教育起步。
2026
Third year of bilingual program · joins Changhua Bilingual Hub
民國 115 年，雙語教育邁入第 3 年，正式加入彰化雙語資源網平台，課室英語、廣播英語、節慶英語等共用內容上線。
