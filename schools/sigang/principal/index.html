II.

Where Sigang Is Going

辦學軸線 · 風頭水尾的小校如何走下一段

Sigang Elementary is one of the smallest schools in Changhua County — 75 students, 13 teachers, 6 grades, one class each, all rooted on the western edge of the Changhua Plain, where the Zhuoshui River meets the Taiwan Strait. We sit at what is known in Taiwanese as 風頭水尾 — "the Windy Head and Water End" — and what looks like a margin on the map is the heart of how we teach.

西港國小是彰化縣最小規模的學校之一——75 名學生、13 位教師、一至六年級各一班，座落在彰化平原西南角，濁水溪入海口與台灣海峽之間。地圖上的邊陲位置，正是我們教學的核心：站在風頭水尾，孩子比都市校園更早學會「土地會說話、海會說話」。

My goal as principal is simple: keep Sigang small by design, not by neglect. A school where every child is known by name. A school where curriculum starts at the seawall, the rice field, and the artist's studio next door — not from a textbook two cities away. A school where being rural is not a deficit to apologise for, but a clarity to teach from.

身為校長，我的方向很單純：讓西港因設計而保有小校的好，而不是因被遺忘而剩下小校的苦。要做一所「每個孩子都被叫得出名字」的學校；要做一所課程從海堤、稻田、與隔壁的藝術家工作室開始發芽的學校；要讓「在偏鄉」不是必須道歉的劣勢，而是教給孩子最清楚的一種視角。

We do this through a handful of anchor programmes that pull weight together: food-and-agriculture education rooted in our farming villages, marine environmental education facing the Strait, our resident artist programme with Ms. Wang Li, and a long-running partnership with Erhlin Community College that brings ecology educators into our classrooms.

我們靠幾條互相支撐的主軸課程走下去：紮根農村的食農教育、面海的海洋環保課程、由駐校藝術家王莉老師帶入日常的藝術教學，以及與二林社區大學長期合作把生態教育帶進校園——每一條都是「在地的根」與「未來的志」綁在一起。

And in 2021, when Dingzhuang Elementary closed and 20+ of its children walked into Sigang's gate, we learned that being a small school means being the place where bigger losses get carried gently. That, too, is part of what we are now.

2021 年大城鄉頂莊國小廢校，二十多位孩子轉入西港——我們學會了一件事：當「小校」，就是當一個能溫柔承接更大失去的場所。這也是我們現在的樣子。

III.

Four Beams of Light

四道光 · 校訓的四個方向

Our school motto pictures Sigang as a lighthouse, sending out four beams of light — humanities, environment, character, and learning. These aren't abstract values bolted onto the walls; they are the four questions every class quietly asks: How do we treat each other? How do we treat the land? How do we treat ourselves? How do we keep wanting to learn? 西港的校徽是一座燈塔，四道光指引——人文、環境、品格、學習。它們不是貼在牆上的口號，而是每堂課靜靜在問的四個問題：我們怎麼對待彼此？怎麼對待這片土地？怎麼對待自己？怎麼讓自己一直想學？

IV.

A Hundred Years of Principals

校長簡史 · 從 1945 到今天

Since 1945, 30 principals have served Sigang Elementary. Each carried forward a school that has never been large, but has always been steady. The lighthouse never moved; only the hands that kept it lit changed. 自 1945 年 12 月起，西港國小已歷經 30 任校長——學校從未變得龐大，但始終穩健。燈塔沒有移動過，只是守護它的手換了一雙又一雙。
