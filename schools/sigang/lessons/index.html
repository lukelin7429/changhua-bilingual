Local land. Open sea. A studio next door.

Sigang's curriculum doesn't start in a textbook — it starts at the seawall, the rice field, and the resident artist's studio. Four anchor programmes carry the weight, each pairing where we are with what we want our children to know. 西港的課程不從課本開始——而是從海堤、稻田，與駐校藝術家的工作室開始。四條主軸課程把「我們所在的地方」與「我們想讓孩子知道的事」綁在一起。

I.

Four Anchor Programmes

四條主軸 · 西港的招牌課程
🌾
Food & Agriculture Education
食農教育

From local crops to the family table — students learn how food, land, and community are woven together. The curriculum runs across the full agricultural cycle: planting, daily care, harvest, and cooking what the school grew.

從在地農作出發，連結食物、土地與社區——孩子完整走過一輪農作週期：播種、田間照顧、採收、把自己種出來的東西端上餐桌。讓「吃」變回一件有來歷的事。
🌱 Whole-year Curriculum
🌊
Marine Environmental Education
海洋環保課程

With the Taiwan Strait at our doorstep, our students learn what it means to "live facing the sea" — coastal ecology, conservation, and beach stewardship. The west coast of Dacheng Township is not a backdrop; it is part of the syllabus.

大城鄉沿海地理優勢——孩子在西海岸學「面海的生活」：海洋生態、海岸保育、淨灘行動。台灣海峽不是背景，而是課表的一部分。
🐚 Coastal Stewardship
🎨
Resident Artist Programme
駐校藝術家 · 王莉老師

Artist Wang Li serves as our resident artist, bringing creative practice into everyday classroom life. Resident artist programmes are rare in small rural schools — Sigang has chosen to make this a permanent fixture of the campus.

王莉老師為西港駐校藝術家，把藝術創作帶入日常的課堂與校園角落。「駐校藝術家」是大型都市學校才常見的配置——西港選擇讓這件事在偏鄉小校長期存在。
🖌️ Long-term Resident
🤝
Erhlin Community College Partnership
與二林社區大學合作

A long-running partnership with Erhlin Community College brings ecology educators and community resources into the classroom. Environmental education at Sigang is taught not just by teachers, but by the community itself.

與二林社區大學長期合作，將生態認識教學與在地社群資源帶進校園——讓「環境教育」不是一個科目，而是一段社區關係。
🌐 Community-Taught
🧸
Affiliated Preschool · Since 1987
附設幼兒園 · 39 年歷史

Established in 1987 (formally re-designated in 2012), our affiliated preschool has nurtured generations of Sigang children before they enter elementary school — a continuous learning thread from age 3 to age 12, all on one campus.

自 1987 年設立、2012 年改制為附設幼兒園，39 年來陪伴一代又一代西港孩子踏上學習旅程。3 歲到 12 歲的學習，可以在同一個校園裡連起來。
👶 Ages 3–12 in One Campus
"Life is Good!"
校訓——我們希望孩子們學會的，不是分數，而是熱愛生活的方式。
