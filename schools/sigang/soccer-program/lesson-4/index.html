Lesson 04 · Sigang Soccer Programme
Shoot for the Goal!
射門得分 · 一腳定勝負
I.

Today's Agenda

今天的四件事
01

What is Shooting?

Kicking the ball to make it go into the goal.

用力把球踢進球門。

02

Where to Aim

The corners — the goalkeeper can't reach them easily.

瞄準球門兩個角落——守門員不容易接到。

03

Meet the Goalkeeper

The only player who can use hands — and the bravest!

唯一可以用手的球員——也是最勇敢的！

04

Score & Celebrate

If the ball crosses the line — it's a goal!

球越過白線——進球了！可以慶祝！

II.

The Best Part of the Game

比賽最棒的一刻

To score a goal, you have to shoot. Shooting is different from passing — you use more power, and you aim at the goal, not at a friend. Hit the ball with the top of your foot (like the laces of your cleats), and follow through.

But the goal is not empty! Standing in front of it is the goalkeeper — the only player on the field who can touch the ball with their hands. A great goalkeeper can dive left, right, or up high to save the ball. Without the goalkeeper, every shot would go in.

Sometimes you'll miss. That's normal — even the best players in the World Cup miss many shots. Just smile, run back, and try again.

想「進球（score a goal）」，就要「射門（shoot）」。射門和傳球不一樣——力道大、瞄準球門而不是朋友。用腳背（球鞋鞋帶那邊）踢球，踢完腳要跟著向前送。但球門前面有一個人——「守門員（goalkeeper）」——全場唯一可以用手的人。好的守門員可以飛撲向左、向右、向上把球擋下來。沒有守門員，每一球都會進。有時候你會「miss 沒踢進」，這很正常——就算世界盃球星，也會射失好多次。笑一笑，跑回去，再試一次。

IV.

Two Shooting Drills

兩個射門練習
🎯
Aim for the Corner
瞄準球門角落
What
Place a cone in the corner of the goal. Shoot to hit it.
在球門角落放三角錐，射門打中它。
Why
To learn aiming — corners are where you score most.
練瞄準——角落最容易進球。
Coach says
"Use your top of foot. Don't kick with your toe!"
「用腳背踢，不要用腳尖！」
🧤
Goalkeeper Drill
守門員練習
What
Take turns being the goalkeeper. Friends shoot softly — you save.
輪流當守門員，朋友輕輕射，你來擋。
Why
Everyone should know how to be a keeper — it's a brave job!
每個人都該試試當守門員——很勇敢的工作！
Coach says
"Watch the ball. Use your hands. Dive if you need to!"
「看球、用手、需要的話就飛撲！」
