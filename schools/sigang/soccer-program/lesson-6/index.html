Match Day!
比賽日 · 終於要上場了
I.

Today's Agenda

今天的四件事
01

Before the Match

Warm up, drink water, listen to the captain.

暖身、喝水、聽隊長說話。

02

The Whistle Blows

The referee starts the game. Kickoff!

裁判吹哨——開球！

03

Fair Play

Follow the rules. Be kind to the other team.

遵守規則，對對手友善。

04

After the Match

Win, lose, or draw — shake hands and smile.

贏、輸、平手都好——握手、微笑。

II.

From the First Whistle to the Last

從第一聲哨音到最後一聲

Today is finally Match Day! All your practice — dribbling, passing, shooting, teamwork — comes together right here. Take a deep breath. You are ready.

The referee will blow the whistle to start the game. We call this kickoff. Two teams play for about 30 to 60 minutes (in a school game). Every time someone scores, the whistle blows and the game starts again from the middle.

The most important rule isn't a soccer rule — it's fair play. Don't push. Don't trip people. Don't argue with the referee. When you win, don't laugh at the other team. When you lose, don't cry — say "Good game!" and shake hands. That's how real soccer players act.

And remember — your friends, your parents, and your school are cheering for you. Even if the score is 0-5, the most important thing is the smile on your face when the final whistle blows. That's why we play.

今天終於是「比賽日（Match Day）」！這幾週練的帶球、傳球、射門、團隊合作，全部都會在這場比賽派上用場。深呼吸——你已經準備好了。「裁判（referee）」會吹哨開賽，這叫「開球（kickoff）」。學校比賽通常踢 30 到 60 分鐘，有人進球就回到中圈重開。最重要的規則不是足球規則，是「fair play 公平競賽」：不能推人、不能絆人、不能跟裁判頂嘴；贏了不要嘲笑對方，輸了不要哭，要說「Good game! 好球！」並握手——這才是真正的球員。記得，朋友、家人、學校都在為你加油。就算 0–5 也沒關係，最重要的是終場哨音響起時你臉上的笑容。這才是我們踢球的原因。

IV.

Two Match-Day Drills

兩個賽前準備練習
🤸
Team Warm-Up
全隊熱身
What
Together: jog one lap, stretch, then 10 passes with a partner.
一起繞場一圈，伸展，再和夥伴傳 10 球。
Why
To get your body ready for the match.
讓身體準備好上場。
Captain says
"Stay close. Stay positive. Listen for the whistle!"
「靠近、保持正向、聽到哨聲就上！」
🎯
Mini Match
小型比賽
What
Divide into two teams. Play a 5-minute mini match with a referee.
分成兩隊，打 5 分鐘小比賽，有裁判。
Why
To practice all the rules and feel a real game.
練習比賽規則，感受真正的賽場氣氛。
Coach says
"Fair play always. No pushing, no shouting at the ref!"
「永遠 fair play！不能推人、不能對裁判大叫！」
V.

Roleplay Scripts

三個情境腳本 · 點 🔊 聽逐句範讀
SCENARIO 01
Before Kickoff
Goal: Greet the other team with respect.
開賽前，向對方致意。
Referee
Captains, come shake hands. Kickoff in one minute!
Captain
Good luck — let's have a good match!
Referee
Ready? *Whistle!* Play!
SCENARIO 02
A Foul Happens
Goal: Say sorry and accept the referee's call.
不小心犯規時要道歉。
Referee
*Whistle!* Foul! You pushed her.
You
Sorry! I didn't mean to. Are you OK?
Opponent
I'm fine. Thanks for asking.
You
That's fair play. Let's keep playing!
SCENARIO 03
The Final Whistle
Goal: End the match with class — win or lose.
比賽結束，無論輸贏都要有風度。
Referee
*Long whistle!* Full time. Final score: 2 - 2 — it's a draw!
You
Good game, everyone! Let's shake hands!
Opponent
Good game! You played really well.
You
Thank you. See you next match!
