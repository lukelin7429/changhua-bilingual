Lesson 03 · Sigang Soccer Programme
Pass and Receive
傳球與接球 · 兩個人才好玩
I.

Today's Agenda

今天的四件事
01

Why We Pass

One player cannot win — we share the ball.

一個人贏不了球——所以要分享球。

02

How to Pass

Use the inside of your foot. Aim at your friend.

用腳的內側踢，瞄準你的朋友。

03

How to Receive

Soft foot. Stop the ball gently.

腳放軟，輕輕把球停下來。

04

Call Your Friend

"On your right!" "I'm open!"

大聲喊出來：「右邊！」「我有空！」

II.

Soccer Needs a Friend

足球需要朋友

In Lesson 2, you learned to dribble — that's just you and the ball. But soccer is a team sport. The fastest way to move the ball up the field is not to dribble, but to pass. A good pass moves the ball faster than your feet can run.

To pass, you turn your foot so the inside (the side near your big toe) hits the ball. Aim where your friend can reach. Don't kick too hard — a pass is not a shot.

To receive, watch the ball coming. Put your foot out softly, like you're catching a baby bird. If your foot is hard, the ball bounces away. If your foot is soft, the ball stops right next to you. That's a great first touch.

第二堂課你學會「帶球」——那是你跟球的事。但足球是團隊運動，把球往前推進最快的方法不是帶球，是「傳球」。一次好的傳球比你跑得還快。傳球時，腳轉一下，用「內側」（大姆指那一邊）去碰球，瞄準朋友踩得到的地方，力道不能太大——傳球不是射門。接球時，眼睛看著球飛來，腳輕輕伸出去，就像捧著一隻小鳥；腳硬球會彈走，腳軟球就停在你旁邊。這叫「第一觸（first touch）」，做得好你就是真正的球員。

IV.

Two Partner Drills

兩個雙人練習
👫
Pair Passing
兩人對傳
What
Stand 5 steps from your partner. Pass the ball back and forth 10 times.
和夥伴相距 5 步，來回傳 10 次。
Why
To get used to the inside foot pass and a soft touch.
熟悉內側傳球和柔接球。
Coach says
"Make it accurate. Don't kick too hard."
「傳準一點，不要踢太大力。」
🔺
Triangle Passing
三角傳球
What
Three players in a triangle. Pass to the next person and run to a new spot.
三人站成三角形，傳完球之後跑到下一個位置。
Why
To practice passing while moving and to call for the ball.
練習邊動邊傳，並學會喊球。
Coach says
"Call your name when you're open!"
「有空檔就大聲喊出來！」
V.

Roleplay Scripts

三個情境腳本 · 點 🔊 聽逐句範讀
SCENARIO 01
First Pair Pass
Goal: Pass back and forth with a partner.
第一次和夥伴對傳。
Partner
Ready? I'll pass first.
You
OK! Use your inside foot!
Partner
Nice receive! Now pass it back.
You
Here it comes — accurate this time!
SCENARIO 02
Calling for the Ball
Goal: Tell your friend you're open.
告訴朋友你有空檔。
You
I'm open! Pass to me!
Friend
Got it! On your right!
You
Thanks! Soft touch — yes!
SCENARIO 03
When a Pass Goes Wrong
Goal: Be kind when a friend misses.
朋友傳錯球時，要鼓勵他。
Friend
Sorry! That pass was too hard.
You
No problem. Try again — softer this time!
Friend
Thanks. You're a kind partner.
