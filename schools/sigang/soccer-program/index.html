Sigang Soccer

西港足球課 · From First Kick to Match Day

"On a small coastal campus, every child gets to wear a jersey, learn the calls in English, and play the world's game."

A game the whole world plays — let's play it in English too.

Soccer is the most-played sport on Earth. At Sigang, our principal believes that every child — boy or girl, tall or small — deserves the chance to step onto a real grass field, learn the rules, and shout the calls in English with their teammates. These six lessons walk you from your very first kick to a real match day.

足球是全世界踢的人最多的運動。校長相信每一個西港孩子都應該有機會踏上真正的草地，學會規則，用英文和隊友一起喊出比賽的口令。這六堂課，會帶你從第一腳開始，一路走到比賽日。
