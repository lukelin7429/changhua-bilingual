School & Bilingual Life

English Life

校園英語 · 把英文織進每一天

Foreign-teacher visits, VR conversation practice, MCC's classroom-English and announcement playlists — and Sigang's own Word of the Day on camera.

English at Sigang isn't a subject — it's a habit of the campus.

在西港，英文不是一個科目，而是校園生活的一種習慣。

Being a small coastal school doesn't mean a small world. This page collects two ways Sigang brings English into the rhythm of everyday school life — both made possible by the shared video libraries produced by My Culture Connect 人師教育協會. 沿海小校不等於小世界。本頁彙整西港把英文「織進日常」的兩種方式——兩條由人師教育協會製作、跨校共用的影音資源庫。

I.

Classroom English

課室英語 · 給老師的口袋句庫

A teacher-facing video library, hosted by Sarah Thomas, walking through ten core classroom situations — from greetings and roll call, through giving feedback and managing the room, to wrapping up and running quizzes. Bite-size enough that any teacher can warm up a phrase before walking into class. 由 Sarah Thomas 老師主講的「課室英語」影音資源——把日常課堂十大情境（問候點名、課前準備、講解、回饋讚美糾正、教室管理、課堂活動、分派作業、課堂收尾、考試……）一支一支講清楚。每支影片夠短，老師可以走進教室前先把一句話練順。

II.

Bilingual Announcements

雙語廣播 · 校園的英語之聲

A campus-broadcast library hosted by Sarah Thomas and Susan Rose — short bilingual episodes introducing the four main offices of a Taiwanese elementary school (Academic Affairs, Student Affairs, General Affairs, Counselling). Designed to ride on the morning broadcast system so the whole school can hear English in the most everyday situation: an announcement. 由 Sarah Thomas 與 Susan Rose 老師主持的雙語廣播影音資源——以英／中雙語介紹台灣國小四大處室（教務處、學務處、總務處、輔導室）。設計上可直接走學校的晨間廣播系統，讓全校在最日常的情境裡聽到英語：一則校園廣播。

Sigang on Camera

西港國小自製影片 · Word of the Day

Sigang's own contribution to the Changhua Bilingual Hub Word of the Day library — short bilingual mini-lessons recorded on campus, each unpacking one English word in 50 seconds. Three episodes so far: homework, shoot, and Push Hands. More on the way. 這條 playlist 是西港國小自己錄製、加入彰化雙語 Hub「每日一字」影音資源庫的西港版作品——每支 50 秒，講透一個英文字。目前 3 支：homework / shoot / Push Hands，持續累積中。
