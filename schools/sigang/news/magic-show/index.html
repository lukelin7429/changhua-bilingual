Sleight of Hand from Japan

日本藏谷和夫蒞校巡迴魔術表演

Today, Japanese magician Kazuo Kuratani (藏谷和夫) brought his touring magic show to Sigang. From the first trick the campus was wide-eyed, and the gasps that followed kept arriving on schedule. Mid-show, students were pulled up to take part in the magic — a class period turned into a small theatre of delight, and everyone, magician and audience, went home a little brighter. 日本藏谷和夫蒞臨本校巡迴魔術表演，當日神奇魔術讓本校師生耳目一新、驚呼連連，過程中更讓學生參與一同演出，讓本校師生度過一堂充滿驚喜的魔術表演課程，賓主盡歡！

"An afternoon when classroom and theatre became the same room."
一個下午，教室和劇場是同一個房間。
