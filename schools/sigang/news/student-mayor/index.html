Sigang's Newest Student-Mayor

115 學年自治鄉長選舉 · 全校投出最神聖的一票

This morning the whole school gathered in the audio-visual hall to cast their vote for the 115 academic-year student-mayor. From candidate policy speeches and campaign posters to the actual polling line, teachers walked the children through a complete miniature of democracy — making the word election something to do, not just to read about. 今天早上，全校學生齊聚視聽教室，鄭重地投下 115 學年自治鄉長選舉的這一票。從候選人政見發表、競選海報展示，到最後投票流程，師長帶著孩子完整走過一次「小型民主」的演練，讓選舉不只是課本上的名詞，而是親身參與的學習。

The race was a tight one. In the end, Kang of Grade 5 won the role with a clear platform and steady delivery — congratulations! We look forward to seeing the new mayor work with class representatives over the year to come, and make campus life that little bit warmer for everyone. 此次選舉競爭激烈，最後由五年級學生康同學以亮眼政見與穩健表達脫穎而出，當選新一任自治鄉長，恭喜！期待新任鄉長在未來一年帶領各班級代表，把校園生活經營得更有溫度。

"Democracy is something children practice, not something they wait to inherit."
民主，是孩子親身演練的功課，不是長大後才繼承的東西。
