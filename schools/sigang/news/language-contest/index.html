A Double Win in the Language Contest

語文競賽傳捷報 — 國語朗讀＋字音字形雙雙獲獎

Congratulations! At this year's Mandarin language contest, two Sigang students brought home prizes. 賀！語文競賽傳捷報　國語朗讀與字音字形雙雙獲獎。

In the Mandarin Recitation division, Tsai Yi-hsuan earned third place. In the Pronunciation & Calligraphy division, Chen Yu-hsiang earned Honourable Mention. 「國語朗讀」組蔡乙瑄同學榮獲第三名，「字音字形」組陳瑀翔同學榮獲優勝。

Both students stood out through steady, daily practice and steady performance under the lights — a quiet credit to Sigang. 恭喜兩位同學憑藉平日努力練習與穩健表現，在競賽中脫穎而出，為校爭光！

Special thanks to Teacher Hsieh Chia-wei (謝佳維) for accompanying and coaching the students after school hours — drilling articulation, character recognition, and word distinctions one careful round at a time. Today's results belong to that work. 特別感謝謝佳維老師用心陪伴與耐心指導，利用課餘時間細心訓練學生，從發音咬字到字詞辨析，皆傾囊相授，成就今日佳績！

Once again, congratulations to our award-winners — and thank you to a teacher who made the difference. 再次恭喜獲獎同學，也感謝老師的辛勞付出。
