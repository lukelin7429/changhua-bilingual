Fan-Dance Meets Taiko

彰化縣 115 年度二林區學生才藝社團展演 · 舞動齊放　大 SHOW 藝彩

In a hall thick with anticipation, Sigang's students took the stage at Zhutang Elementary today for the Erlin District Student Club Showcase, performing their signature piece: a fan dance paired with taiko drums — a study in youthful energy and team timing that earned the school real applause. 在熱鬧與期待交織的氛圍中，本校學生於今天在竹塘國小參加二林區社團才藝展演，以別具特色的「扇子舞搭配太鼓」表演，展現出青春活力與團隊默契，為校爭光。

Our thanks to Teachers Jing-wen, Jia-jun, and Jin-yuan for the careful months of coaching that made the performance possible. With creativity and confidence, the students put the many-sidedness of Sigang's club life on show — proof that campus life carries far more than what fits on a timetable. 感謝瀞文老師、家鈞老師及僅媛老師用心的指導，讓學生以創意與實力展現校園社團的多元風貌，為校園生活增添光彩，也讓更多人看見學生在課堂之外的無限潛能。

"There is more in a child than what fits on the timetable."
孩子身上的潛能，比課表能裝下的多得多。
