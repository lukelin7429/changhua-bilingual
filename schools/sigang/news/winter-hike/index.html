End-of-Term: Walk, Clean-Up, and Taiwanese-Language Concert

期末動起來 · 健走頂庄校區 × 環保行動 × 閩南語樂學

With the school term winding down, Sigang turned the last day into a walk-and-do. The whole school hiked together to the Dingzhuang Campus, reading the neighbourhood by foot and stretching out winter legs along the way. 學期尾聲，本校舉辦健走活動，帶著全校學生一路走到頂庄校區，邊走邊認識社區、活動筋骨。

As soon as we arrived, brooms came out. Children fanned across the grounds, sorted recyclables, and worked the place into a clean welcome for the new term. 抵達後大家立刻動手打掃環境，分工合作讓校園煥然一新。

Then came the noise: a lively Taiwanese-language singing contest and a round of riddles and “tail-cut” sayings. Children sang their hearts out and shouted answers before the host could finish — language and culture learned in the same breath as laughter. 接著登場的是熱鬧的閩南語歌唱比賽與猜謎、歇後語活動，同學們唱得開心、答得踴躍，在笑聲中學語言、認識文化。

A day that moved between motion and music — and a perfect bookend for the term. 動靜皆宜的一天，為學期留下滿滿回憶！

"Children learn a language fastest when they're singing it."
孩子最快學會一種語言，是在他們唱它的時候。
