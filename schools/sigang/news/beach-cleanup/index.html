Loving Dacheng Clean — Seawall Beach Cleanup

0523 親淨大城 · 淨堤暨河川管理宣導活動

0523 — Loving Dacheng Clean: Seawall Beach Cleanup & River-Management Outreach. 0523 親淨大城　淨堤暨河川管理宣導活動。

Beside Sigang Haishan Temple, our students joined the Dacheng Township Office's seawall cleanup — picking up trash, learning the basics of river-and-sea management, and doing a small share of stewardship for the rivers and coast that shape every Sigang childhood. Care the township well, and the township will care for itself. Everyone, in for the earth. 西港海山廟旁海堤～學生參加大城鄉公所主辦淨堤活動，為愛護河海環境盡一份心力，永保美好城鄉，大家一起愛地球。
