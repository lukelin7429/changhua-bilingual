2026-04-13 · 榮譽榜
66th National Science Fair — Chemistry, 3rd Place
科展榮獲國小組化學科第三名

Jianxin's student team placed third in chemistry at the 66th National Elementary Science Fair — back-to-back national-level recognition after the 64th-edition biology honour. A six-class coastal school on the national podium.

2026-04-13 · 榮譽榜
Education Cup · 13-Form Tai Chi — Gold & Silver
教育盃太極拳錦標賽 國小 A 組 13 式佳績

Jianxin's Tai Chi students Hong Jian-hao (洪健皓) and Hong Die-ru (洪啑茹) of Class 5-Zhong took first and second place in the 13-Form competition at Changhua's 114th-year Education Cup.

Tai Chi · Push-Hands
2026-04-13 · 榮譽榜
Education Cup · Push-Hands Championship
教育盃推手錦標賽 優異成績

Beyond the 13-Form competition, Jianxin students also earned medals in the Push-Hands championship — a more dynamic Tai Chi format that tests balance, sensitivity, and quick reading of an opponent.

2026 International Sea Ox Festival — Art & Writing Competition
2026 國際海牛文化節「海牛創藝著色寫生比賽」

Jianxin students enrolled in the 2026 International Sea Ox Festival creative competition (June 7) — drawing and writing about Fangyuan's coastal heritage, recognized as intangible cultural heritage since 2020.
