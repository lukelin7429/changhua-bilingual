Dear Jianxin families, friends, and visitors —

It is my honour to serve as the principal of Jianxin Elementary School. I joined this campus in August 2025, after four years leading Chang'an Elementary School in Zhutang Township. The county's 114th-year selection brought me here — to the sand, the sea wind, the carrot fields, and the children who run through them every morning.

親愛的建新家長、朋友與每一位到訪這個網頁的人：

我很榮幸能擔任建新國小校長。我於 2025 年 8 月就任，前一站是任職四年的彰化縣竹塘鄉長安國小。在縣府 114 學年度第一階段校長遴選中，建新成為我下一段教育旅程的所在——這裡有沙地、海風、紅蘿蔔田，與每天早上奔跑穿過田埂的孩子。

What drew me to Jianxin is what makes Jianxin uncommon: a six-class school with two consecutive national science-fair placings, county-champion Tai Chi students, Cool English Speaking Kings, and a campus named Reading-Rock Launch School by the Ministry of Education. Small does not mean less. It means more time per child, more space per question, more room to grow.

真正吸引我來到建新的，是這所學校罕見的氣質：一所只有 6 班的偏鄉小校，卻能拿下連續兩屆全國科展殊榮、縣級太極拳冠軍、Cool English 口說王，並獲教育部認證為閱讀磐石啟航學校。「小」不代表「少」，「小」意味著每個孩子分到更多時間、每個問題分到更多空間、每個生命分到更多長大的餘地。

In the years ahead, I want to hold two compass points in balance:

未來幾年，我希望讓建新緊握兩個指南針點：

Roots in Xinsheng. The sand-built campus, the sea-ox coast, the carrot harvest, the Tai Chi forms — these are not "local-flavour add-ons" to a curriculum. They are the curriculum. A Jianxin child should leave us able to explain her home to the world, in two languages.

扎根新生村。沙地上的校舍、海牛海岸、紅蘿蔔田、太極拳形——這些不是課程裡的「在地調味」，這些就是課程。每一個從建新畢業的孩子，都應該能用兩種語言，把家鄉介紹給世界。

Reach into the wider world. STEAM, bilingual reading, Cool English, international classroom partnerships, this bilingual website — all are bridges. We use them to bring the world to Xinsheng, and to send Xinsheng's children, with confidence, into the world.

延伸到更寬廣的世界。STEAM、雙語閱讀、Cool English、國際教室合作、這個雙語網站——這些都是橋。我們用它們，把世界帶到新生村；也讓新生村的孩子，有自信地走進世界。

Thank you to every parent, every teacher, every community partner — and to My Culture Connect 人師教育協會, whose volunteers built this bilingual home for our school. May the next decade at Jianxin be one where small means strong, and where every child finds her own way to stand tall.

謝謝每一位家長、每一位老師、每一位社區夥伴——也謝謝為建新打造這個雙語家的人師教育協會（My Culture Connect）志工團隊。願建新接下來的這個十年，「小」就是「強」，讓每一個孩子都找到屬於自己挺直腰桿的方式。

Lin Chia-hui · Principal

II.

Vision for Jianxin

建新辦學願景 · 四個方向
🌱
Roots First
先扎根
A curriculum that starts with the carrot field, the Tai Chi mat, the sea-ox coast, the Xinsheng community — before it reaches for anywhere else. Children who know their own ground stand on it more steadily.
🌏
Reach Outward
再延伸
English, STEAM, international classroom partnerships — not as separate "advanced tracks" but as extensions of who we already are. We bring the world to Xinsheng so that Xinsheng can speak back.
📖
Read Twice Daily
每日雙閱讀
Continue and deepen Jianxin Double Reading — Chinese storybooks in the morning, English practice (Cool English, PaGamO) in the afternoon. Reading is the quietest, most reliable engine of growth in a small school.
🤝
School as Community
學校即社區
Honour the founding vision — "a school becomes the cultural centre of its community". Parents, alumni, community partners, civic and cultural organisations — all welcome through our gate.
III.

Career & Path

校長學經歷 · 時間軸
~2021
Qualified as Reserve Principal · Changhua County
通過彰化縣候用校長甄選，進入校長儲備行列。
2021 · Aug
Appointed Principal of Chang'an Elementary School
110 學年度（民國 110 年 8 月），就任彰化縣竹塘鄉長安國小校長——這是林校長的第一所校長任職學校。
2021–2025
Four-year tenure at Chang'an — small-school international focus
在長安國小四年任期內，推動偏鄉小校的國際教育與英語學習路線，建立了「迷你校 × 全球視野」的辦學基底。
2025 · Jul 31
Selected in Changhua County's 114th-year Principal Selection
縣府舉辦 114 學年度中小學校長交接典禮（彰化藝術高中展翼館），由縣長王惠美主持。林家惠校長為 38 位調任校長之一，自長安國小調往建新國小。
2025 · Aug 1
Inaugurated Principal of Jianxin Elementary
民國 114 年 8 月 1 日正式就任建新國小校長，任期至 118 年 7 月 31 日（為期四年）。
2026 · Now
Jianxin Bilingual Site launches
林校長就任首年內，與人師教育協會（My Culture Connect）合作推出建新國小雙語網站——這是新任校期內，學校面向世界的第一份對外作品。
