I.

About Jianxin

關於建新 · 一所建在沙地上的海岸小校
A six-class coastal village school in Xinsheng — built on what was once the Zhuoshui River bed, where carrots now grow and sea oxen still walk.
坐落於新生村的六班海岸小校——這裡曾是濁水溪河床的沙地，如今長著紅蘿蔔，海牛仍在退潮的灘地上行走。

Jianxin Elementary School sits in Xinsheng Village (新生村), the eastern reach of Fangyuan Township on Changhua County's western coast. The land here was once the bed of the Zhuoshui River — all sand, swept by autumn and winter winds. In August 1952, more than fifty local people — Wu Yu-shui, Chen Jin-long, Chen Qing-xiu, Lin Chun-de and forty-six others — pooled donations and gave their own land to open Caohu Elementary's Xinsheng Branch. Nine years later, in August 1961, the branch became independent: Jianxin Elementary School was born.

建新國小位於芳苑鄉新生村，地屬彰化縣西海岸。這片土地原為濁水溪河床、全沙地，每逢秋冬風沙飛揚。民國 41 年（1952）8 月，吳雨水、陳金龍、陳清秀、林春德等五十多位地方人士募資捐地，設立草湖國校新生分班。九年後的民國 50 年（1961）8 月，分校獨立為「建新國民學校」——這就是建新國小的起點。

Today, six classrooms hold around 68 children, with a teacher-to-student ratio of 1 : 5.2. The campus stretches across generous grounds — over 200 square meters per student — leaving room for STEAM benches, Tai Chi practice mats, and the carrot beds of our food-education programme. Small school. Open sky. Deep coast.

如今，全校 6 班、約 68 位孩子，師生比 1:5.2。校地寬敞，平均每位學生擁有超過 200 平方公尺的校園空間——足以放下 STEAM 工作桌、太極練習場，以及食農課程的紅蘿蔔田。一所小學校，一片開闊的天空，一段深長的海岸。

II.

Four School-Based Curricula

四大特色課程 · 建新辦學的四根柱子

Four signature programmes carry every Jianxin child through six years — one for the page, one for the lab bench, one for the field, one for the body.

我們以四大校本課程陪伴每一位建新的孩子走過六年——一個給書頁、一個給實驗桌、一個給田地、一個給身體。

Jianxin Double Reading
建新雙閱讀
A daily ritual of Chinese and English reading, weaving classroom texts with PaGamO online practice. Jianxin is a PaGamO Reading-Literacy Seed School and a Changhua County Reading-Rock Launch School.

STEAM 與探究

Fangyuan's coastal sand has grown sweet potato, peanut, sugarcane, asparagus, and today — some of Taiwan's finest carrots. Our food-education programme takes children from the field to the plate, from soil to story.

III.

Small School, National Stage

小學校，大舞台 · 全國級殊榮
National-Level Honors · 全國級殊榮
68 students. National podium.
六十八個孩子，站上全國的舞台。

Few rural schools with only six classes reach the national science-fair podium. Jianxin has done it two editions in a row — placing in biology at the 64th National Science Fair and taking third place in chemistry at the 66th. Add a county-level sweep in Tai Chi — first and second place in the 13-Form, plus medals in Push-Hands — and Cool English Speaking Kings every year, and the pattern is clear: at Jianxin, the size of the school does not limit the size of the work. 很少有 6 班規模的偏鄉小校能站上全國科展的領獎台——建新做到了兩屆，第 64 屆生物科佳作、第 66 屆化學科第三名。再加上太極拳教育盃 13 式第一、二名、推手錦標賽佳績、Cool English 口說王 14 位獲獎——建新用行動證明：學校的「規模小」，並不限制孩子「表現的尺寸」。

IV.

Programs & Moments

特色課程與校園時刻
📚
Jianxin Double Reading
建新雙閱讀 · 一天兩種語言的閱讀
A daily reading rhythm of Chinese and English — classroom storybooks in the morning, PaGamO and Cool English practice in the afternoon. The Ministry of Education has named Jianxin a Reading-Rock Launch School; PaGamO has named us a Reading-Literacy Seed School.
「建新雙閱讀」是孩子們每日的閱讀節奏：早上中文故事書、下午 PaGamO 與 Cool English 練習。教育部認證為「彰化縣閱讀磐石啟航學校」，PaGamO 認證為「閱讀素養種子學校」。
📖 Reading-Rock Launch School 🌱 PaGamO Seed School
🐃
Sea Ox Coast
海牛文化 · 芳苑海線的活遺產
Fangyuan is the home of Taiwan's last working sea oxen — water buffalo that pull harvest carts into the intertidal zone to gather oysters. The practice is recognised as intangible cultural heritage (2020). Jianxin children join the International Sea Ox Festival art and writing competitions each summer, learning to see their home through the eyes of artists and storytellers.
芳苑是全台僅存「海牛採蚵」文化保留地——水牛拉車進潮間帶採蚵的傳統，於 2020 年被列為無形文化遺產。建新孩子們每年參加「國際海牛文化節」著色寫生比賽，學會用藝術家與說故事的人的眼睛，重新認識自己的家鄉。
🐃 Intangible Heritage 2020 🎨 Sea Ox Festival
🥕
Carrot Country Field Work
紅蘿蔔田 · 食農教育現場
Fangyuan's coastal sand grows some of Taiwan's finest carrots — the latest in a century-long rotation that has also given the village sweet potato, peanut, sugarcane, and asparagus. Our food-education programme takes children into the fields to plant, harvest, wash, and cook — turning the local crop into a curriculum, and the curriculum back into the meal.
芳苑沿海的沙地長出全台最好的紅蘿蔔之一——這是新生村百年農作輪替的最新一章（甘薯 → 花生 → 甘蔗 → 蘆筍 → 紅蘿蔔）。食農教育帶孩子下田種植、採收、清洗、烹煮——把在地作物變成課程，再把課程變回餐桌上的一道菜。
🌱 Field-to-Table 🥕 Coastal Carrot
🥋
Tai Chi as PE
太極拳 · 從體育課到全縣冠軍
Tai Chi runs through the daily PE programme — patience, balance, breath. In 2025, Jianxin students took first and second place in the 13-Form competition at the Changhua Education Cup, plus medals in Push-Hands. For a small school, this is more than sport — it is a way of carrying tradition forward.
太極拳是建新體育課的日常——耐心、平衡、呼吸。2025 年（114 學年度），五忠洪健皓、洪啑茹同學在彰化教育盃 13 式太極拳分獲第一、第二名，加上推手錦標賽佳績。對一所小學校而言，太極不只是運動——是把傳統慢慢傳下去的方式。

Welcome from the Principal

Principal Lin Chia-hui

林家惠 校長 · Inaugurated August 2025
"A coastal village school can stand tall. Our work is to make sure every Jianxin child stands tall with it."

Principal Lin Chia-hui joined Jianxin in August 2025 — the first phase of Changhua County's 114th-year principal selection. She brings four years of principal experience from Chang'an Elementary School, where she led an internationally-minded campus committed to rural-school innovation.

林家惠校長於 2025 年 8 月（民國 114 學年度第一階段遴選）就任建新國小校長。她自 彰化縣長安國小調任而來——在前一所學校的四年任期內，她以「偏鄉小校 × 國際視野」的辦學路線而為同業所知。

As Jianxin enters its seventh decade, Principal Lin is shaping a campus that holds two compass points in balance: roots in Xinsheng — sand, sea, carrot, sea ox, Tai Chi — and reach into the wider world — STEAM, bilingual reading, Cool English, the global classroom. This bilingual website is part of that reach.

建新國小邁入第七個十年，林校長以兩個指南針點為辦學主軸：扎根新生村——沙地、海岸、紅蘿蔔、海牛、太極；延伸到更寬廣的世界——STEAM、雙語閱讀、Cool English、全球教室。這個雙語網站，就是她「向外延伸」的其中一步。

VI.

Our Story

校史時間軸 · 從沙地上的分班到 65 年的學校
1952
Caohu Elementary's Xinsheng Branch opens — funded by 50 villagers
民國 41 年 8 月，吳雨水、陳金龍、陳清秀、林春德等五十多位地方人士募資捐地，設立「草湖國校新生分班」——建新國小的起點。
1954
Branch upgraded to "Branch Campus"
民國 43 年 8 月，改稱「草湖國校新生分校」。
1961
Independent school founded — Jianxin Elementary
民國 50 年 8 月，新生分校奉准獨立設校，定名「建新國民學校」（創校元年）。首任校長：謝瑞禎；首任家長會長：吳雨水。
1975
Renamed to Jianxin Elementary School
民國 64 年，全國「國民學校」改制為「國民小學」，校名改為「建新國民小學」沿用至今。
2009
New campus building completed (NT$57M)
民國 95 年底申請北棟教室結構安全鑑定，民國 98 年 2 月開工、12 月底竣工。工程款 5,707 萬元，採「機能分區、量體分棟、單伸手」設計策略——這就是今日的建新校舍。
2025
Principal Lin Chia-hui takes office (Aug)
民國 114 年 8 月，林家惠校長自彰化縣長安國小調任建新國小——縣府 114 學年度第一階段校長遴選結果。
2026
Jianxin bilingual site launches with My Culture Connect
民國 115 年 5 月，建新國小雙語網站上線——由人師教育協會（My Culture Connect）與校方共同建置，作為林校長新任校期內的首個對外作品。
