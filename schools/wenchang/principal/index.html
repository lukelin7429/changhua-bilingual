A Small School, A Wide Horizon 小校大格局

Wenchang Elementary is one of Changhua County's smaller schools, serving children from two villages — Zhenping and Sanhe — in Fuxing Township's watermelon country. A school this size does not have the resources of a city school; what it does have is intimacy, and a principal whose job is to make sure that intimacy never narrows into smallness of horizon.

Under Principal Chen's leadership, Wenchang has won county-level recognition as a "Setting Sail" school in international education — the framework Taiwan's Ministry of Education uses for schools that move beyond classroom textbooks and into actual cross-cultural exchange. For a school of our size, that is not a casual decoration. It is a deliberate answer to the question of what a rural Taiwanese elementary school in the 2020s should look like.

文昌國小是彰化縣相對小型的學校，學區涵蓋福興鄉鎮平村與三和村兩個聚落。這樣規模的學校沒有都市學校的資源，但它有「親近」——而校長的工作，就是讓「親近」永遠不會收斂成「視野的狹窄」。

在陳宇杉校長帶領下，文昌國小通過彰化縣領航學校認證，取得「課程教學－國際教育／揚帆學校」級別——這是教育部國際教育架構中，給走出教科書、進入真實國際交流的學校的等級。對一所這樣規模的鄉村小學來說，這不是裝飾，而是對「2020 年代的台灣鄉村小學，應該長什麼樣子」這個問題，給出的一個明確答案。

瓜

Rooted in Zhenping 扎根鎮平・食農的孩子

Wenchang's food-and-farm program brings the local watermelon — from seed, to soil, to lunchbox — right into the classroom. A giant watermelon-shaped slide in the schoolyard makes the same point in a simpler way: this is where these children come from, and the school is proud of it.

文昌國小的食農教育，把鎮平的西瓜——從種子、土壤、一直到午餐——帶進教室。校園裡那座大家都記得的西瓜造型溜滑梯，用更簡單的方式說了同一件事：這是這群孩子的家鄉，學校以此為榮。

帆

Setting Sail Globally 小校揚帆・國際教育

As a county-certified Setting Sail school, Wenchang's children have already taken part in online cross-cultural exchanges with classrooms abroad. The work is to make sure that "international" never feels theoretical — that a child in a village classroom can have a real conversation with a child somewhere else in the world.

作為彰化縣領航認證的「揚帆學校」，文昌國小的孩子已經參與過跨國線上文化交流。校方要做的，是讓「國際」這個詞永遠不會只停留在紙上——讓一個鎮平的孩子，能與世界另一端的孩子真實地對話。

機

Hands That Build 動手做・STEM 教育

Wenchang runs a robotics design course and seasonal robotics camps. In a village school of this size, that is unusual — and intentional. We believe the children of Zhenping and Sanhe should leave elementary school with both the literacy of the soil and the literacy of the machine.

文昌國小開設機器人設計課程與機器人營隊。對一所這樣規模的鄉村小學來說，這並不常見——而這正是我們刻意為之的。我們相信，鎮平和三和的孩子，畢業時應該同時帶走兩種「素養」：土地的，與機器的。

Come Visit 歡迎蒞校交流

Educators, parents, and friends from Taiwan and abroad are warmly welcome to visit Wenchang Elementary. We are especially interested in conversations about rural bilingual education, food-and-farm curriculum, and how a small village school holds a wide horizon.

歡迎國內外教育工作者、家長與友人蒞校交流。我們特別期待和您聊聊偏鄉雙語教育、食農課程，以及一所鄉村小學如何維持寬闊的視野。
