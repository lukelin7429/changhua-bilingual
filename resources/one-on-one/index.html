One-on-One Conversation

一對一英語會話課 · A Lesson Kit for Young Tutors

"You don't need to be a teacher yet — just open the page, share your screen, and read the blue boxes out loud." 你還不用是個老師——打開這一頁、分享螢幕，照著藍色框念出來，就能上課。

Teaching one-on-one, made easy.

Each theme below is a complete, ready-to-run 40-minute lesson for a young volunteer teaching English to a younger learner. No lesson planning needed — every minute is mapped out, every line you should say is written for you, and there's a fun game to keep the conversation going.

下面每一個主題都是一堂完整、可直接上的 40 分鐘課程，專為「教年紀更小的孩子英文」的高中生志工設計。不用自己備課——每一分鐘都安排好了，每一句你該說的話都幫你寫好了，還有一個小遊戲讓對話不冷場。

How to run a lesson · 怎麼上一堂課

三個步驟，照著做就好
1
🖥️ Open & shareOpen the theme page and share your screen on Google Meet.
打開主題頁，在視訊上分享螢幕。
2
⏱️ Follow the blocksGo down the time blocks: hello → words → sentences → game → talk.
照時間區塊往下走：打招呼→單字→句型→遊戲→對話。
3
😊 End happyPraise the student and give a take-home question for next time.
稱讚學生，給一個帶回家的問題，下次有話聊。
