Resources · Bilingual Campus

School Teams & Clubs 校隊與社團 · 興趣即詞彙

After-school clubs are where students develop deeper English vocabulary tied to their passions — not the textbook list, but words they actually want to know. Basketball, choir, robotics, eco-club: each becomes a vocabulary universe.

課後社團是學生發展深層英文詞彙的地方——不是課本上的單字，而是學生「真心想知道」的詞。籃球、合唱、機器人、生態社團，每一個都是一座詞彙宇宙。

Why it matters · 政策對齊

Implements the 2030 bilingual policy's guideline on "Bilingual extracurricular activities" — clubs are the most natural place for sustained authentic English use.

實踐 2030 雙語政策「雙語課外活動」要點——社團是最自然的、能長期使用真實英文的場域。

Core principle · 核心原則: 社團不是「多教一點英文」，是「英文就在你已經愛的事情裡」。Clubs aren't a place to add more English — English is just already there, in what students already love.

Five club-life moments, ready for English 社團生活的五個雙語時刻

The five moments below cover the typical year of any club — joining, practicing, creating, serving, competing. Pull the section that matches your club type; don't try to apply all five everywhere.

以下五個時刻涵蓋任何社團的典型年度——入社、練習、創作、服務、比賽。挑符合你社團類型的段落用，不必五段全套照搬。

Activity 01

Joining a Club · 加入社團

從報名到第一次出席

⏱ Sign-up week · 30 minutes

What it is · 做法

The first English-rich moment of the club year is sign-up. Bilingual sign-up forms, an English question-asking ritual, and a welcome handshake from older students set the tone.

社團年度的第一個英文密集時刻是報名。雙語報名表、英文提問儀式、學長姐英文歡迎握手，奠定整年的調性。

Sample script · 範本對話

New member: Hello. I want to join the basketball club. Where can I sign up?
Returning student: Welcome! Sign here. What grade are you in?
New member: Grade 4.
Returning student: Great. We practice every Tuesday at 3:30. Bring your sports shoes. See you next week!

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

sign up 報名schedule 時間表meeting 聚會equipment 裝備see you next week 下週見

Implementation tip · 實施建議

Have older students run the sign-up table, not teachers. Older students naturally use the same simple English a Grade-4 needs. Plus it gives them leadership ownership.

讓高年級學生顧報名桌，不要老師顧。高年級會自然用四年級孩子聽得懂的簡單英文。也讓他們承擔起社團的責任。

Activity 02

Sports Team Practice · 運動隊練習

球場上的英文

⏱ Every practice · 5 minutes warm-up

What it is · 做法

Sports vocabulary is the easiest English students will ever learn — they NEED these words to play. Teach 'pass,' 'shoot,' 'foul,' 'time-out' in the first practice and reinforce every session.

運動詞彙是學生最容易學的英文——他們「需要」這些字才能玩。第一次練習就教 pass / shoot / foul / time-out，之後每次練習都重複使用。

Sample script · 範本對話

Coach: Today we warm up first. Five minutes of jogging — go!
Coach (during play): Good pass! Now shoot! Yes!
Coach (calling pause): Time-out. Everyone, listen up. Let's talk about defense.
Coach (closing): Good practice today. Cool down. See you on Thursday.

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

warm up 暖身pass 傳球shoot 投籃defense 防守time-out 暫停

Implementation tip · 實施建議

Use ONLY English for sports calls during practice — even if the coach also speaks Mandarin. The chaos of a game is the perfect context: students figure out 'pass' the second time they hear it.

練習中發號施令一律用英文——即使教練也會中文。比賽的混亂正是最好的語境：學生第二次聽到 pass 就會懂。

Activity 03

Art / Music Club · 藝術音樂社團

器材與創作的英文

⏱ Per meeting · 5 minutes setup

What it is · 做法

Art and music clubs offer rich vocabulary tied directly to physical objects — brushes, palettes, scores, instruments. Label every shared item bilingually. The label becomes the lesson.

美術社與音樂社提供大量與實體物品直接連結的詞彙——畫筆、調色盤、樂譜、樂器。把每件共用器材貼雙語標籤。標籤就是教材。

Sample script · 範本對話

Music teacher: Today we will practice page 12 in your score. Flutes, start. Strings, ready.
Art teacher: Please get your brush, your palette, and one piece of paper from the shelf.
Art teacher: Mix red and blue. What color do you get? Yes — purple!
Music teacher: From the top, everyone — one, two, three, four!

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

brush 畫筆palette 調色盤score 樂譜rehearsal 排練from the top 從頭

Implementation tip · 實施建議

End every art/music session with a 30-second 'show your work in English' — students hold up their piece and say one sentence about it. Builds presentation muscle alongside creative skill.

每次美術／音樂社結尾留 30 秒「英文展示作品」——學生舉起作品，用一句英文介紹。創作能力與表達能力同步建立。

Activity 04

Service / Eco Club · 服務生態社

公益行動的英文

⏱ Per project · 60 minutes

What it is · 做法

Service clubs (cleaning, recycling, beach clean-ups, food bank visits) give students English tied to action verbs and civic vocabulary — 'volunteer,' 'donate,' 'recycle,' 'community.'

公益社團（打掃、回收、淨灘、食物銀行訪問）讓學生學到與行動相關的英文與公民詞彙——volunteer、donate、recycle、community。

Sample script · 範本對話

Club leader: Today we are going to the beach to clean up trash. Wear gloves. Bring your water bottle.
Volunteer: What kind of trash do we collect?
Leader: Plastic, bottles, and cigarette butts. We separate them into three bags.
Leader (after): Good job, everyone! We collected three bags of plastic. That is three less bags in the ocean.

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

volunteer 志工donate 捐贈recycle 回收community 社區clean up 清理

Implementation tip · 實施建議

Document service projects with bilingual photo captions — those become great content for school news, sister-school exchanges, and parent newsletters. One project, four content uses.

服務專案要拍照並寫雙語圖說——可以同時用於校園新聞、姊妹校交流、家長通訊。一個專案，四種用途。

Activity 05

Tournament Day · 比賽日

比賽中的英文

⏱ Per tournament · all day

What it is · 做法

Tournament day brings out cheering and sportsmanship vocabulary in a way no class can replicate. Pre-teach a small set of cheers and post-game phrases — 'Good game,' 'Well played,' 'See you next time.'

比賽日帶出加油與運動家風範的詞彙，是任何課堂都比不上的真實情境。事先教一組加油詞和賽後用語——「Good game」「Well played」「下次再戰」。

Sample script · 範本對話

Cheerleader: Go, team, go! Let's go, let's go, L-E-T-S G-O!
Coach (timeout): Catch your breath. Drink water. We have two more minutes.
Player (post-game, to opponent): Good game. Well played. See you next time.
Coach (closing): Win or lose, we played with respect. That's what matters.

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

opponent 對手referee 裁判sportsmanship 運動家風範good game 好球respect 尊重

Implementation tip · 實施建議

Teach 'Good game' as the unconditional post-match phrase — whether you win, lose, or tie. It's the one piece of English that travels with these students for life.

教孩子「Good game」是賽後無條件的招呼——贏、輸、平手都說。這句英文會跟孩子一輩子。
