Resources · Bilingual Campus

School Tours 校園導覽 · 雙語走讀

A campus tour led by students is one of the highest-confidence English-speaking moments of the year. Foreign visitors, parents, and sister-school delegates often visit Taiwanese schools — turn that visit into a vocabulary-rich student stage instead of a teacher monologue.

學生帶隊導覽校園，是全年最能讓學生展現英文自信的場合之一。外國訪客、家長、姊妹校代表常造訪台灣學校——把這個場合留給學生當主角，比老師獨白更能累積詞彙。

Why it matters · 政策對齊

Aligns with Bilingual Daily Life on Campus implementation guideline 4: "Use existing school resources as bilingual learning materials." School tours convert physical campus assets into living English vocabulary.

對齊雙語生活化校園實施要點第 4 條：「善用校園既有資源作為雙語學習素材」。校園導覽把實體校園資產轉化為活的英文詞彙。

Core principle · 核心原則: 學校本來就要接待訪客，讓學生當主角就好。Tour-giving turns visitor reception into a student-led teachable moment instead of a teacher-only task.

Six classroom-ready stations 六個立即上線的雙語站

Each station below comes with a bilingual script, key vocabulary, and a tip from real Changhua campuses. Pair students up, hand them the cards, and rehearse twice in English class before the visit.

以下六站皆附中英對話、關鍵詞彙、與彰化校園的實施建議。學生兩兩搭配，握著小卡，英文課裡彩排兩次即可上場。

Activity 01

The Front Gate · 校門口

歡迎與校史開場

⏱ 1–2 minutes · 一到兩分鐘

What it is · 做法

Two student guides welcome visitors at the gate, ask their country of origin, and walk them through what they'll see in the next 15 minutes.

兩位學生導覽員在校門口迎接訪客，請對方介紹自己的國家，並告訴對方接下來 15 分鐘會看到的內容。

Sample script · 範本對話

Guide A: Good morning! Welcome to our school. May I ask your name?
Guide B: We are so happy to meet you. Where are you from?
Guide A: Today we will show you our library, our classroom, and our playground. Are you ready?
Guide B: Please follow us. Watch your step!

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

welcome 歡迎school motto 校訓security guard 警衛principal 校長watch your step 小心台階

Implementation tip · 實施建議

Pair shy and confident students together. Print the script on a small card the guides can hold — visitors expect this and it lowers anxiety.

讓害羞和外向的學生兩兩搭配。把腳本印在小卡片上讓導覽員握著——訪客其實期待這樣的工具，孩子也比較放鬆。

Activity 02

Principal's Office & School History · 校長室與校史

用英文介紹百年校史

⏱ 2 minutes · 兩分鐘

What it is · 做法

A short introduction of the principal, the founding year, the student count (rounded), and one award or distinction the school is proud of.

簡介校長、創校年（不寫死數字，用「百年以上」「超過五十年」等表達）、學生人數（取整數），與學校最自豪的一項榮譽。

Sample script · 範本對話

Guide A: This is our principal's office. Our school was founded in 1955.
Guide B: We have around 200 students from Grade 1 to Grade 6.
Guide A: Last year, our calligraphy team won the county championship.
Guide B: Our principal will say a few words. Please come in.

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

founded in 創立於award 榮譽champion 冠軍around 大約history 歷史

Implementation tip · 實施建議

Encourage 'around' / 'about' instead of exact numbers — easier to remember and more natural English. Update once a year, not constantly.

教學生用「around」「about」代替精確數字——既好記，英文也更自然。一年更新一次即可，不必每月改。

Activity 03

The Library · 圖書館

書區與閱讀風景

⏱ 2 minutes · 兩分鐘

What it is · 做法

Tour of book sections (picture books, chapter books, English shelf), reading nooks, and the weekly reading routine.

介紹書區（繪本、章節書、英文書架）、閱讀角落，以及每週固定的閱讀時間。

Sample script · 範本對話

Guide A: Welcome to our library. We have over 5,000 books.
Guide B: This shelf is for picture books. This one is for chapter books.
Guide A: Here is our English shelf — these are books donated by foreign teachers.
Guide B: Every Wednesday, we read silently here for 30 minutes. Do you like reading?

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

library 圖書館picture book 繪本chapter book 章節書donate 捐贈silent reading 默讀

Implementation tip · 實施建議

If your library has a 'foreign teachers donated this' shelf, lead visitors to it — they almost always smile and recognize a title.

如果圖書館裡有「外師捐書」專區，特別帶訪客過去看——他們幾乎一定會笑、認出某本書，氣氛立刻溫暖起來。

Activity 04

The Classroom · 教室

走進日常上課的空間

⏱ 2–3 minutes · 兩到三分鐘

What it is · 做法

Brief tour of a typical classroom — seating, smart screen, schedule on the wall, student artwork. Ideally enter a class during a non-test period so visitors see real teaching.

簡介一般教室——座位安排、智慧螢幕、牆上課表、學生作品。最好挑非考試時段，讓訪客看見真正在上的課。

Sample script · 範本對話

Guide A: This is our Grade 5 classroom. There are 28 students in this class.
Guide B: On the wall, you can see our weekly schedule and our class agreements.
Guide A: These are paintings from our last art class. Do you like them?
Guide B: Please say hello to our classmates. Class — let's welcome our guests!

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

classroom 教室smart screen 智慧螢幕schedule 課表agreement 約定artwork 作品

Implementation tip · 實施建議

Brief the teacher of the chosen class one day in advance. A 20-second classroom interaction often becomes the visitor's strongest memory of the tour.

提前一天通知該班導師。教室裡短短 20 秒的互動，往往是訪客整趟參訪最印象深刻的一段。

Activity 05

The Cafeteria · 學生餐廳

用英文介紹台灣校園午餐

⏱ 1–2 minutes · 一到兩分鐘

What it is · 做法

Visitors are almost always curious about Taiwan's school lunch system. Show the kitchen window, today's menu, and the recycling station.

訪客對台灣的營養午餐制度幾乎都好奇。帶他們看廚房窗口、今日菜單、與餐後分類回收站。

Sample script · 範本對話

Guide A: This is our cafeteria. We eat lunch in our classroom, but the food is prepared here.
Guide B: Today's lunch is rice, fish, vegetables, and miso soup.
Guide A: After eating, every class takes turns washing the lunch boxes.
Guide B: We separate food waste, plastic, and paper. The school is very strict about this!

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

cafeteria 餐廳school lunch 營養午餐menu 菜單recycle 回收food waste 廚餘

Implementation tip · 實施建議

If visitors arrive at lunch time, invite them to eat with the students. The shared meal is the most memorable bilingual exchange of the day.

若訪客剛好在午餐時間到，邀他們和學生一起吃飯。共桌一餐，比任何導覽都更能成就雙語交流。

Activity 06

The Playground · 操場

全校最熱鬧的空間

⏱ 1–2 minutes · 一到兩分鐘

What it is · 做法

End the tour at the playground — running track, basketball court, weekly assembly area. Open question time here under the trees.

在操場作為導覽尾聲——跑道、籃球場、每週升旗集合處。在大樹下開放訪客提問。

Sample script · 範本對話

Guide A: This is where we have morning assembly every Monday.
Guide B: The basketball court is for Grades 5 and 6 during recess.
Guide A: We have a 200-meter track around the field. Some students run every morning.
Guide B: Do you have any questions about our school? We will try our best to answer!

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

playground 操場track 跑道basketball court 籃球場recess 下課時間question 問題

Implementation tip · 實施建議

Always end at the playground with open Q&A. Students who were quiet during the scripted tour often light up when asked about their hobbies or favorite teacher.

導覽結尾一定要回到操場開放問答。腳本講過的孩子常會緊張，但被問「最喜歡哪位老師、最愛什麼運動」時，反而閃閃發亮。
