Resources · Bilingual Campus

Summer Fun Program 暑期樂活營 · 一週密集雙語

When the school year ends, English doesn't have to. A one-week summer camp built around games, crafts, and sports — all in light English — gives students 25 hours of bilingual time per year. Better still: students sign up voluntarily.

學年結束，英文不必跟著放假。一週暑期營以遊戲、手作、運動為主軸，全程以輕鬆英文進行，能為學生每年多累積 25 小時的雙語時間。重點是：學生自願報名。

Why it matters · 政策對齊

Aligns with Ministry of Education's "夏日樂學" summer enrichment program and 2030 bilingual policy's voluntary out-of-class learning opportunities.

對齊教育部「夏日樂學」暑期增能計畫，並符合 2030 雙語政策「自願性課外英語接觸機會」精神。

Core principle · 核心原則: 暑假最稀缺的不是時間，是「孩子願意學英文的時間」。Summer's scarcest resource isn't hours — it's hours when kids choose to use English.

Five days, five layers 五天，五個層次

The five sections below give you the full camp shape — daily schedule, activity buckets, English-rich games, the closing performance, and the parent reception. Print, adapt, run.

以下五段給你完整營隊架構——每日作息、活動分類、英文密集遊戲、成果發表、家長交流。印出來、改寫、執行。

Activity 01

Daily Camp Schedule · 每日營隊作息

從上午到下午的雙語節奏

⏱ Per camp day · 8:30am–3:30pm

What it is · 做法

A consistent daily schedule is half the camp. Morning briefing in English, four rotating activity blocks, lunch, two more blocks, closing circle. Repeat for 5 days.

穩定的每日作息就是營隊的一半。早晨英文集合 → 4 個輪替活動 → 午餐 → 2 個活動 → 結業圈圈。5 天循環。

Sample script · 範本對話

Camp leader (morning): Good morning, campers! Today is Day Three. Let's check the schedule on the board.
Camp leader: Group A — you start at the science station. Group B — start at sports. Switch in 45 minutes.
Camp leader (afternoon closing): Everyone sit in a circle. What was your favorite moment today? Share one word.
Camper: Splash! (from water games this morning)

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

schedule 作息rotation 輪換group 組別camper 營員closing circle 結業圈圈

Implementation tip · 實施建議

Use 'Day One,' 'Day Two,' 'Day Three' rather than dates — feels more like camp and easier for English. By Day Five, students will count down on their own.

用 Day One / Day Two 而非日期——更有營隊感、英文也好理解。第五天時學生會自己倒數。

Activity 02

Activity Buckets · 活動分類

四種主題，輪流上場

⏱ Per block · 45 minutes

What it is · 做法

Four activity buckets cover camp without exhausting any teacher: SPORTS (outdoor games), CRAFTS (art and making), SCIENCE (simple experiments), DRAMA (skits and storytelling). Each bucket has 5 prepped activities — one per day.

四種活動分類就足以涵蓋一整週、不操爆任何老師：運動（戶外遊戲）、手作（藝術與動手做）、科學（簡單實驗）、戲劇（短劇與說故事）。每類預備 5 個活動——每日一個。

Sample script · 範本對話

Sports station leader: Today's game is 'Capture the Flag.' Two teams. The blue team's flag is over there. Ready?
Crafts station leader: Today we are making paper boats. Take one piece of paper. Watch — fold, fold, fold.
Science station leader: What happens when we mix baking soda and vinegar? Let's see! Pour it in — wow!
Drama station leader: Today's story is 'The Tortoise and the Hare.' Who wants to be the tortoise? The hare?

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

station 站別rotation 輪換supplies 物料experiment 實驗skit 短劇

Implementation tip · 實施建議

Stagger the four stations so no two are loud at the same time. Sports outside, drama in the gym, crafts in the cafeteria, science in a classroom — geography prevents overlap.

四站錯開避免吵：運動在戶外、戲劇在禮堂、手作在餐廳、科學在教室——靠空間區隔解決音量衝突。

Activity 03

English-Integrated Games · 英文遊戲

玩中學的設計

⏱ Per game · 15–30 minutes

What it is · 做法

Three classics that work at any skill level — Bingo (with vocabulary cards instead of numbers), Scavenger Hunt (English clues hidden around the school), Charades (act out the word the FET shows you).

三個經典遊戲，任何程度都能玩——賓果（用詞彙卡而非數字）、尋寶（藏英文線索在校園各處）、比手畫腳（外師給字，學生比劃，全組猜）。

Sample script · 範本對話

Bingo caller: Next word — 'rainbow.' If you have 'rainbow' on your card, mark it!
Scavenger hunt leader: Clue number three: 'I am red, round, and grow on a tree. The teacher eats me at lunch.' Where do we go?
Charades player (acting): [swimming motion]
Team: Swim! Swimming! Pool!

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

turn 輪到score 得分winner 贏家again 再一次guess 猜

Implementation tip · 實施建議

Charades is the highest-leverage English game in camp — total physical commitment unlocks vocabulary even shy kids will shout. Mix in 2-3 rounds every day.

比手畫腳是營隊裡英文槓桿最大的遊戲——全身投入會逼出連害羞孩子都不自覺喊出來的詞彙。每天混 2-3 輪。

Activity 04

Closing Showcase · 成果發表

最後一天的舞台

⏱ Friday afternoon · 60 minutes

What it is · 做法

On Day 5, each group performs a 3-minute skit they wrote together. Parents are invited. The skit doesn't need to be polished — bumbling through it together is the achievement.

第五天，每組演一齣 3 分鐘自編短劇，邀家長到場觀賞。短劇不必精緻——能一起跌跌撞撞演完，就是成就。

Sample script · 範本對話

MC (camper): Welcome to our Camp Showcase! First up, the Lion Group will perform 'A Day at the Beach.'
Group performer: It's a sunny day. Let's go to the beach!
Group performer 2: I forgot my swimsuit! Oh no!
MC: Let's give them a big hand. Next up — the Tiger Group with 'The Lost Dog.'

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

perform 演出audience 觀眾applause 掌聲MC 主持人thank you 謝謝

Implementation tip · 實施建議

Record every group's performance and share the videos with parents via QR code. The video becomes the family memento — and shows up at the kid's wedding twenty years later.

每組演出都錄影，用 QR Code 分享給家長。這支影片會變成家族紀念品——二十年後孩子的婚禮上會被重播。

Activity 05

Parent Reception · 家長迎接

結業時的家長英文

⏱ Friday · 30 minutes

What it is · 做法

After the showcase, a 30-minute parent reception. Each camper introduces their parents to one teacher in English ('This is my mom. This is Teacher Sarah.'). A 2-sentence ceremony bridges school and home.

成果發表後，30 分鐘的家長交流。每位營員用英文把家長介紹給一位老師：「這是我媽媽。這是 Sarah 老師。」兩句話的儀式，連結學校與家庭。

Sample script · 範本對話

Camper: Mom, this is Teacher Sarah. She teaches our drama class.
Parent: Hello. Thank you for taking care of my child this week.
Teacher: It was my pleasure. Your son is very brave on stage.
Camper (closing): We made paper boats today. Can I show you?

Key vocabulary · 關鍵詞彙

introduce 介紹parent 家長certificate 證書congratulations 恭喜family 家庭

Implementation tip · 實施建議

Give parents a printed bilingual 'phrases for tonight' card when they arrive — five short English sentences they can use with the FET. Adult anxiety drops; parent-teacher relationship deepens.

家長到場時發一張雙語「今晚可以說的話」卡——五句可以對外師說的短英文。大人焦慮感降低，親師關係加深。
