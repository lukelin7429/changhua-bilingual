SDG 16 · Quiz · 測驗

Peace, Justice & Strong Institutions

和平、正義與健全制度

Five questions · 五題選擇題. Pick the best answer for each. When you're done, hit Submit.

五題選擇題。每題選最佳答案，全部完成後按 Submit。

Question 1

Why do we have laws?

為什麼要有法律？

Laws set fair rules everyone follows.

法律是大家都遵守的公平規則。

Question 2

'Justice' means …

「正義」是指……

Justice = fairness for all.

正義=對所有人公平。

Question 3

To solve a fight peacefully you can …

和平解決衝突可以……

Listen first, then talk.

先傾聽，再對話。

Question 4

Strong institutions are ones that …

健全的制度是……

Trustworthy = strong.

值得信任=健全。

Question 5

Goal 16 is …

目標 16 是……

Goal 16 = Peace, Justice & Strong Institutions.

目標 16 是和平、正義與健全制度。
