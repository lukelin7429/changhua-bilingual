SDG 01 · People · 人類

No Poverty

終結貧窮

In one sentence 一句話

Make sure everyone has enough to live a safe, healthy life.

讓每個人都能擁有安全、健康的生活。

Vocabulary 單字

English中文Definition · 釋義
poverty貧窮the state of being extremely poor.
極度缺乏的狀態。
income收入money received from work.
工作得到的錢。
basic needs基本需求food, water, clothing, shelter.
食物、水、衣服、住所。
inequality不平等when some have much more than others.
有人擁有比別人多很多。

Classroom Activity 課堂活動

Class kindness map

班級善意地圖

On a big sheet of paper, draw your school. Mark with stickers: (1) where students who forgot lunch can go, (2) where to find spare uniform, (3) who helps when family money is tight. Discuss: what's missing? What's one thing the class could add?

在大張紙上畫出學校。用貼紙標出：(1) 忘記帶午餐的同學可以去哪裡、(2) 哪裡可以借校服、(3) 家裡經濟困難時誰能幫忙。討論：還缺什麼？班上可以多做一件什麼事？

Discussion prompts 討論題

  • Are there students in your school who don't have lunch money? How can you help?
  • What's one thing your family does to help neighbors in need?
  • Brainstorm 3 ways your class could support a local charity.
