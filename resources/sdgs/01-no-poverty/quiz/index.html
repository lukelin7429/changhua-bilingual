Question 1

What does 'poverty' mean?

「poverty」是什麼意思？

Poverty = lacking basic needs.

貧窮就是缺乏基本所需。

Question 2

Which is a 'basic need'?

哪一項是「基本需求」？

Clean water is a basic need.

乾淨的水是基本需求。

Question 3

What is 'income'?

「income」是什麼？

Income = money received.

收入就是得到的錢。

Question 4

Goal 1 = No Poverty.

目標 1 是終結貧窮。
