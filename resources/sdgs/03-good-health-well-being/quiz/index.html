Good Health & Well-being

健康與福祉

Five questions · 五題選擇題. Pick the best answer for each. When you're done, hit Submit.

五題選擇題。每題選最佳答案，全部完成後按 Submit。

Question 1

How many hours of sleep do students need?

學生每天大約需要幾小時睡眠？

Most children need 8-10 hours.

大部分小朋友需要 8-10 小時。

Question 2

Which is good 'exercise'?

哪一項是好的「運動」？

Jumping rope gets your heart moving.

跳繩讓心臟動起來。

Question 3

Where do you go if very sick?

生病很嚴重時要去哪裡？

Hospital — to see a doctor.

醫院——找醫生。

Question 4

'Well-being' means …

「well-being」是指……

Well-being = body + mind healthy.

福祉=身心都健康。

Question 5

What's a healthy school snack?

健康的學校點心是？

Fresh fruit beats sugar every time.

新鮮水果勝過糖。
