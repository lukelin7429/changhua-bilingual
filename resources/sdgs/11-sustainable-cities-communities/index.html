SDG 11 · Prosperity · 繁榮

Sustainable Cities & Communities

永續城鄉

In one sentence 一句話

Make our towns safer, cleaner, and more welcoming.

讓城市與鄉村更安全、乾淨、宜居。

Vocabulary 單字

English中文Definition · 釋義
city城市a large town.
大的城鎮。
community社區people living in one area.
住在同一區域的人。
park公園a green space for everyone.
給大家用的綠地。
safe安全free from danger.
沒有危險。

Classroom Activity 課堂活動

Map my township

繪製我的鄉鎮

Draw a simple map of your township. Mark with stars: parks, libraries, temples, your favourite shop. With a different color, mark one place that could be better. Discuss what would improve it.

畫一張你鄉鎮的簡單地圖。用星星標出公園、圖書館、廟、最愛的店。用不同顏色標出可以改善的一處。討論怎麼變好。

Discussion prompts 討論題

  • What's the prettiest place in your township? Why?
  • How could your school playground be safer?
  • Suggest one small change that would make your neighborhood greener.
