SDG 06 · Planet · 地球

Clean Water & Sanitation

潔淨水與衛生

In one sentence 一句話

Clean water for drinking, washing, and farming — for everyone.

讓每個人都有乾淨的水可以喝、洗、灌溉。

Vocabulary 單字

English中文Definition · 釋義
water水the clear liquid we drink.
可以喝的透明液體。
clean乾淨free from dirt or germs.
沒有髒污或細菌。
waste廢水/垃圾unwanted dirty material.
不想要的髒東西。
river河a large stream of flowing water.
大量流動的水。

Classroom Activity 課堂活動

Water diary

用水日記

For one day, tally each time you use water: brush teeth, wash hands, flush, shower, drink. Multiply by family members. Discuss: where could you save?

一天內記錄每次用水：刷牙、洗手、沖水、洗澡、喝水。乘以家人數。討論：哪裡可以省水？

Discussion prompts 討論題

  • Track your water use for one day. Where could you save?
  • How does Changhua's water travel from the mountains to your tap?
  • What happens to the dirty water after it goes down the drain?
