Clean Water & Sanitation

潔淨水與衛生

Five questions · 五題選擇題. Pick the best answer for each. When you're done, hit Submit.

五題選擇題。每題選最佳答案，全部完成後按 Submit。

Question 1

Where does Changhua's drinking water come from?

彰化的飲用水從哪裡來？

Rivers + wells/groundwater.

河川與地下水。

Question 2

Why turn off the tap while brushing?

刷牙時為什麼要關水？

A running tap wastes ~6 L/min.

水龍頭開著每分鐘約浪費 6 公升。

Question 3

'Sanitation' means …

「sanitation」是指……

Sanitation = clean conditions.

衛生=保持乾淨。

Question 4

Where does dirty water go after the drain?

髒水進排水孔後去哪裡？

Wastewater goes to a treatment plant.

廢水送到汙水處理廠。

Question 5

Goal 6 is …

目標 6 是……

Goal 6 = Clean Water & Sanitation.

目標 6 是潔淨水與衛生。
