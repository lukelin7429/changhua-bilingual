SDG 12 · Planet · 地球

Responsible Consumption & Production

責任消費與生產

In one sentence 一句話

Buy less, use longer, throw away less.

少買、耐用、少丟棄。

Vocabulary 單字

English中文Definition · 釋義
consume消費to use up; to buy things.
用掉；買東西。
reduce減少to use less.
用少一點。
reuse重複使用to use something again.
再次使用。
recycle回收to make new things from old.
用舊東西做新東西。

Classroom Activity 課堂活動

One-week reuse pact

一週重複使用挑戰

For one week, bring a reusable bottle, lunch box, and shopping bag instead of buying new. Tally how many disposable items you avoided. Share class total.

連續一週使用環保水壺、餐盒與購物袋，不買新一次性用品。算出避免了多少件，全班加總。

Discussion prompts 討論題

  • How many things in your school bag are reusable?
  • What's one item your family threw away that could have been used longer?
  • Plan a class 'swap day' where everyone trades books or toys.
