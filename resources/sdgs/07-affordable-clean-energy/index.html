SDG 07 · Prosperity · 繁榮

Affordable & Clean Energy

可負擔的潔淨能源

In one sentence 一句話

Use energy that doesn't pollute and that everyone can afford.

使用不污染、人人負擔得起的能源。

Vocabulary 單字

English中文Definition · 釋義
energy能源power for lights, devices, cars.
讓燈、設備、車運作的力量。
solar太陽能energy from the sun.
來自太陽的能源。
clean潔淨doesn't make pollution.
不會造成污染。
save節省to use less of something.
少用一些。

Classroom Activity 課堂活動

Light audit

燈光稽查

Walk around your classroom and home. Count every light and device that's on. Are they all needed right now? Make a list of 3 you'll turn off.

走一圈教室與家裡。數開著的燈與設備。現在都需要嗎？列出 3 個你會關掉的。

Discussion prompts 討論題

  • Count the lights and devices on at your home right now. Could any be off?
  • What's the difference between solar energy and coal energy?
  • If your school had a solar roof, what could it power?
