SDG 05 · People · 人類

Gender Equality

性別平等

In one sentence 一句話

Boys and girls should have the same chances, choices, and respect.

男生女生應該擁有同樣的機會、選擇與尊重。

Vocabulary 單字

English中文Definition · 釋義
gender性別being a boy, girl, or any identity.
男生、女生或任何身份。
equal平等the same as each other.
彼此一樣。
fair公平treating everyone justly.
公正地對待每個人。
respect尊重treating someone with care and honor.
用心、尊敬地對待別人。

Classroom Activity 課堂活動

Hidden helpers

看不見的幫手

List 10 things that get done at home each week (cooking, dishes, repairs…). Mark who does each. Discuss: is the load shared fairly? What would be fairer?

列出家裡每週要做的 10 件事（煮飯、洗碗、修東西⋯）。寫下誰做。討論：分擔公平嗎？怎樣比較公平？

Discussion prompts 討論題

  • Are there activities at your school that only boys or only girls do? Why?
  • Name 3 women in science, sports, or leadership you admire.
  • What does fair sharing of housework look like at your home?
