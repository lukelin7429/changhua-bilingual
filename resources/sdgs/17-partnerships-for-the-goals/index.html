SDG 17 · Partnership · 夥伴關係

Partnerships for the Goals

多元夥伴關係

In one sentence 一句話

Work together — schools, families, governments — to reach all the goals.

學校、家庭、政府一起合作，達成所有目標。

Vocabulary 單字

English中文Definition · 釋義
partner夥伴someone working with you.
與你一起工作的人。
global全球的everywhere in the world.
世界各地。
cooperate合作to work together.
一起努力。
goal目標something you aim to do.
想達成的事。

Classroom Activity 課堂活動

Class SDG pick

班級 SDG 認養

As a class, pick ONE of the previous 16 goals to focus on this term. Plan 3 concrete actions you'll do together. Track progress on a wall chart.

全班一起選之前 16 個目標中的一個當作本學期重點。規劃 3 個一起做的具體行動。在牆上做進度表。

Discussion prompts 討論題

  • Who are your school's partners (e.g. the Education Department, MCC, foreign teachers)?
  • What's one goal your class could tackle together this year?
  • Pick one SDG from above and plan a class project around it.
