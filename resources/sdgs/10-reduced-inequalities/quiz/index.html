Reduced Inequalities

減少不平等

Five questions · 五題選擇題. Pick the best answer for each. When you're done, hit Submit.

五題選擇題。每題選最佳答案，全部完成後按 Submit。

Question 1

'Inequality' means …

「不平等」是指……

Inequality = uneven shares.

不平等=分配不均。

Question 2

To 'include' someone …

「包容」一個人就是……

Include = invite in.

包容就是邀請進來。

Question 3

Reducing inequality means …

減少不平等就是……

Close the gap.

把差距拉近。

Question 4

A welcoming class is one where …

歡迎人的班級是……

Everyone included.

每個人都被包容。

Question 5

Goal 10 is …

目標 10 是……

Goal 10 = Reduced Inequalities.

目標 10 是減少不平等。
