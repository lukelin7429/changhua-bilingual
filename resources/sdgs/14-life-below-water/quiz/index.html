SDG 14 · Quiz · 測驗

Life Below Water

海洋生態

Five questions · 五題選擇題. Pick the best answer for each. When you're done, hit Submit.

五題選擇題。每題選最佳答案，全部完成後按 Submit。

Question 1

Changhua faces which sea?

彰化面對哪個海？

Taiwan Strait — between Taiwan and China.

台灣海峽——台灣與中國之間。

Question 2

Why is plastic in oceans bad?

為什麼海裡的塑膠不好？

Marine animals mistake plastic for food.

海洋動物會把塑膠誤當食物。

Question 3

To protect the ocean you can …

保護海洋你可以……

Pick up trash, use fewer straws.

撿垃圾、少用吸管。

Question 4

Most ocean trash comes from …

海洋垃圾大多來自……

Rivers carry land trash to the ocean.

河流把陸地垃圾帶到海。

Question 5

Goal 14 is …

目標 14 是……

Goal 14 = Life Below Water.

目標 14 是海洋生態。
