SDG 13 · Planet · 地球

Climate Action

氣候行動

In one sentence 一句話

Help slow down climate change before it's too late.

在來不及之前，一起減緩氣候變遷。

Vocabulary 單字

English中文Definition · 釋義
climate氣候long-term weather patterns.
長期的天氣型態。
carbon碳a gas that warms the planet.
讓地球變熱的氣體。
pollution污染harmful stuff in air/water.
空氣或水中的有害物。
plant種植to put a seed in the ground.
把種子種下去。

Classroom Activity 課堂活動

Family carbon list

家庭碳排清單

List 5 things your family does each week that release CO₂ (driving, eating beef, using AC). Choose ONE and try a low-carbon swap for a week. Report back.

列出家裡每週會排碳的 5 件事（開車、吃牛肉、開冷氣）。挑一項換成低碳做法試一週。回報結果。

Discussion prompts 討論題

  • How is the weather in Changhua different from your grandparents' time?
  • Name 3 things your family does that release CO₂.
  • Plan one 'no electricity' hour for your home each week.
