SDG 13 · Quiz · 測驗

Climate Action

氣候行動

Five questions · 五題選擇題. Pick the best answer for each. When you're done, hit Submit.

五題選擇題。每題選最佳答案，全部完成後按 Submit。

Question 1

What does CO₂ stand for?

CO₂ 是什麼？

Carbon dioxide — a greenhouse gas.

二氧化碳——溫室氣體。

Question 2

Climate change makes …

氣候變遷會讓……

More heat waves, storms, floods.

更多熱浪、暴風雨、洪水。

Question 3

Which helps reduce CO₂?

哪一項可以減碳？

Walking/biking emits no CO₂.

走路、騎自行車不排碳。

Question 4

Trees help climate because they …

樹幫助氣候是因為……

Trees absorb carbon dioxide.

樹會吸收二氧化碳。

Question 5

Goal 13 is …

目標 13 是……

Goal 13 = Climate Action.

目標 13 是氣候行動。
