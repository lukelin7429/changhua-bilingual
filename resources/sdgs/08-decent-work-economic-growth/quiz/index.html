Decent Work & Economic Growth

尊嚴就業與經濟成長

Five questions · 五題選擇題. Pick the best answer for each. When you're done, hit Submit.

五題選擇題。每題選最佳答案，全部完成後按 Submit。

Question 1

What does 'wages' mean?

「wages」是什麼意思？

Wages = payment for work.

薪水就是工作的報酬。

Question 2

A 'decent' job is …

「decent」工作就是……

Decent = safe + fair + livable wage.

尊嚴=安全+公平+夠生活。

Question 3

Career means …

職涯是指……

Career = long-term work.

職涯=長期的工作。

Question 4

Why is 'safe' work important?

為什麼工作要「安全」？

Worker safety is non-negotiable.

工人的安全是底線。

Question 5

Goal 8 is …

目標 8 是……

Goal 8 = Decent Work & Economic Growth.

目標 8 是尊嚴就業與經濟成長。
