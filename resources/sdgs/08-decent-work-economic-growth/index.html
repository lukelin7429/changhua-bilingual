SDG 08 · Prosperity · 繁榮

Decent Work & Economic Growth

尊嚴就業與經濟成長

In one sentence 一句話

Everyone should have a fair job that pays enough and is safe.

每個人都該有公平、安全、足夠的工作。

Vocabulary 單字

English中文Definition · 釋義
work工作doing a job to earn money.
做事賺錢。
safe安全free from danger.
沒有危險。
wages薪水money paid for work.
工作的報酬。
career職涯your work over many years.
多年來做的工作生涯。

Classroom Activity 課堂活動

Job in Changhua

認識彰化的職業

Choose a local job (farmer, shopkeeper, FET, baker). Interview them or research: What's a normal day? What skills do they need? Present a 2-minute report.

選一個在地職業（農夫、店主、外師、麵包師）。訪問或查資料：典型一天怎麼過？需要什麼技能？做兩分鐘簡報。

Discussion prompts 討論題

  • What do your parents do for work? What do they like about it?
  • What's one job in Changhua that didn't exist 20 years ago?
  • Interview a local farmer or shopkeeper about their work.
