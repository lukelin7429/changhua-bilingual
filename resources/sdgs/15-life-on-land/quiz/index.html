Life on Land

陸地生態

Five questions · 五題選擇題. Pick the best answer for each. When you're done, hit Submit.

五題選擇題。每題選最佳答案，全部完成後按 Submit。

Question 1

What is a 'habitat'?

什麼是「棲地」？

Habitat = the place where an animal lives.

棲地=動物生活的地方。

Question 2

Forests are important because they …

森林重要因為它們……

Trees produce the oxygen we breathe.

樹製造我們呼吸的氧氣。

Question 3

What does 'biodiversity' mean?

「biodiversity」是指？

Bio = life, diversity = variety.

Bio=生命、diversity=多樣。

Question 4

When a forest is cut down …

森林被砍掉時……

Cutting forests removes habitats.

砍森林=動物失家。

Question 5

Goal 15 is …

目標 15 是……

Goal 15 = Life on Land.

目標 15 是陸地生態。
