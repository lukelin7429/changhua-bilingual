Zero Hunger

消除飢餓

In one sentence 一句話

Everyone deserves enough food — and the right kind of food.

每個人都應該擁有足夠且營養的食物。

Vocabulary 單字

English中文Definition · 釋義
hunger飢餓the strong need for food.
想吃食物的強烈需求。
nutrition營養the goodness in food we eat.
食物中對身體有益的成分。
farm農場a place where food is grown.
種食物的地方。
waste浪費throwing away things we could use.
丟掉本來可以用的東西。

Classroom Activity 課堂活動

Track your plate

盤中食追蹤

For three school lunches in a row, note what you don't eat and throw out. At the end, total it up. Discuss: which foods get wasted? How can we waste less?

連續三天午餐，記錄丟掉的食物。三天後加總。討論：哪些食物最常被丟掉？我們可以怎麼少浪費一些？

Discussion prompts 討論題

  • How does your school's lunch program reduce food waste?
  • Where does the rice on your plate come from? (Hint: probably a Changhua farm!)
  • Plan a 'sharing meal' where each student brings something to share.
