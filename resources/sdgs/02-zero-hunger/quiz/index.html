Zero Hunger

消除飢餓

Five questions · 五題選擇題. Pick the best answer for each. When you're done, hit Submit.

五題選擇題。每題選最佳答案，全部完成後按 Submit。

Question 1

What does 'hunger' mean?

「hunger」是什麼意思？

Hunger = needing food.

飢餓就是需要食物。

Question 2

Which food is from Changhua?

哪樣食物來自彰化？

Changhua is Taiwan's rice basket.

彰化是台灣的米倉。

Question 3

'Nutrition' means…

「nutrition」是指……

Nutrition = what your body needs.

營養是身體需要的東西。

Question 4

To waste LESS food, you can …

想少浪費食物，你可以……

Take what you'll eat — the simplest rule.

拿吃得完的量——最簡單的規則。

Question 5

Goal 2 is called …

目標 2 叫做……

Goal 2 = Zero Hunger.

目標 2 是消除飢餓。
