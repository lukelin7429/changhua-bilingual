SDG 04 · People · 人類

Quality Education

優質教育

In one sentence 一句話

Every child gets to go to school and learn well.

讓每個孩子都能上學並好好學習。

Vocabulary 單字

English中文Definition · 釋義
education教育the process of learning.
學習的過程。
teacher老師a person who helps you learn.
幫助你學習的人。
school學校a place for learning together.
一起學習的地方。
bilingual雙語using two languages.
使用兩種語言。

Classroom Activity 課堂活動

Interview a senior

訪問長輩

Interview a parent or grandparent. Ask: (1) What was YOUR school like? (2) Did you learn English? (3) What's one thing today's students should appreciate? Bring back 3 sentences to share.

訪問家長或祖父母，問三個問題：(1) 您小時候的學校長什麼樣？(2) 有沒有學英文？(3) 現在的學生最該珍惜什麼？帶回三句話分享。

Discussion prompts 討論題

  • Why is bilingual education important for your future?
  • What's one subject you wish your school taught more of?
  • Interview a senior in your family — what was school like in their time?
