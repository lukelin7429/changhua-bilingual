Quality Education

優質教育

Five questions · 五題選擇題. Pick the best answer for each. When you're done, hit Submit.

五題選擇題。每題選最佳答案，全部完成後按 Submit。

Question 1

What does 'bilingual' mean?

「bilingual」是什麼意思？

Bi = two, lingual = language.

Bi 是「兩」，lingual 是「語言」。

Question 2

Education means …

教育的意思是……

Education = learning.

教育就是學習。

Question 3

Why is 'quality' education important?

為什麼「優質」教育重要？

Quality means learning really happens.

優質=真正在學習。

Question 4

Goal 4 is called …

目標 4 叫做……

Goal 4 = Quality Education.

目標 4 是優質教育。

Question 5

Changhua's name means …

彰化縣名意思是……

建學立師以彰雅化 — education in the very name.

建學立師以彰雅化——教育在彰化的本名裡。
