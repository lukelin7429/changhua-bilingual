Lesson 02 · Sigang Soccer Programme
Let's Dribble!
帶球前進 · 用腳控制球
I.

Today's Agenda

今天的四件事
01

What is Dribbling?

Moving the ball with little kicks while you run.

一邊跑、一邊用小腳步推球前進。

02

Small Touches

Keep the ball close — never far from your foot.

球要黏在腳邊，不能離太遠。

03

Eyes Up

Look at the field, not just the ball.

眼睛要看場上，不能只盯著球。

04

Both Feet

Use your left and your right foot.

左腳和右腳都要會用。

II.

The Ball is Your Friend

球是你的好朋友

Dribbling means you move while keeping the ball close to your feet. Good dribblers use small touches — just a gentle tap, again and again. If you kick the ball too hard, it runs away. If you kick it too softly, you cannot move.

The secret? Eyes up. Beginners look down at the ball — but the best players look up at the field. They see who is open, who is coming, and where the goal is. Practice slow at first, then faster.

Also: don't only use one foot! Soccer players need to be two-footed. Today, try your left foot, then your right foot, then both. It will feel strange at first — that's normal. Smile and keep going.

「帶球（dribble）」就是一邊移動、一邊讓球緊貼你的腳。厲害的球員都用「小觸球」——輕輕地點一下，再點一下。踢太大力球會跑掉，踢太小力你又動不了。秘訣是「眼睛抬起來」：初學者盯著球，但最厲害的球員看場上——看誰有空檔、誰跑過來、球門在哪。先慢，再快。還有：不能只用一隻腳！要練左腳、右腳、兩腳都用。一開始會怪怪的——這很正常，笑一笑繼續練。

IV.

Two Dribbling Drills

兩個帶球練習
🚩
Cone Weaving
穿越三角錐
What
Put 5 cones in a line. Dribble in and out.
擺 5 個三角錐成一直線，左右繞著帶球。
Why
To practice small touches and quick changes of direction.
練習小觸球與快速變向。
Coach says
"Eyes up. Slow first. Don't hit the cones!"
「眼睛抬起來。先慢慢來。不要撞到三角錐！」
🔄
Left-Right Switch
左右腳交換
What
Dribble 5 steps with your left foot, then 5 with your right.
左腳推 5 步，再換右腳推 5 步。
Why
Soccer players need both feet — never just one.
真正的球員兩隻腳都要會用。
Coach says
"Control the ball. Don't let it run away!"
「控球！不要讓球跑掉！」
V.

Roleplay Scripts

三個情境腳本 · 點 🔊 聽逐句範讀
SCENARIO 01
First Cone Run
Goal: Try the cone weaving drill.
第一次挑戰穿越三角錐。
Coach
Your turn! Dribble through the cones.
You
Like this? Small touches?
Coach
Yes! Eyes up. Don't look at the ball.
You
Oops — I hit a cone!
Coach
No problem. Try again — slow and steady.
SCENARIO 02
Left or Right?
Goal: Switch feet while dribbling.
換腳練習。
Coach
Now use your left foot only.
You
It feels weird! I always use my right.
Coach
That's why we practice! Both feet, every day.
SCENARIO 03
Fast or Slow?
Goal: Find your good speed.
找到適合自己的速度。
Friend
You're going too fast! The ball ran away!
You
OK — I'll go slow first, then speed up.
Friend
Nice control! That's much better.
