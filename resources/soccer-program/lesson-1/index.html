II.

A Game the Whole World Plays

全世界都在玩的一場遊戲

Soccer (in Britain, people say football) is the most popular sport on Earth. More than 250 million people play it in over 200 countries. The game is simple: two teams try to kick a round ball into the other team's goal.

Soccer is played on a big grass field. Each team has eleven players, including one goalkeeper who stays near the goal. Players run, pass, and try to score, but they cannot touch the ball with their hands — only the goalkeeper can.

In Taiwan, soccer is growing fast. Many schools — like ours at Sigang — now have a soccer team. You don't need to be tall. You don't need to be the fastest. You just need a ball, a friend, and a smile.

足球（英國人說 football）是全世界最熱門的運動。在 200 多個國家、超過兩億五千萬人都在踢。規則很簡單：兩隊用腳把圓球踢進對方的球門。比賽在一片大草地上，每隊 11 個人（含一位顧球門的 goalkeeper 守門員）。除了守門員，其他人都不能用手碰球。在臺灣，足球運動正在快速成長，很多學校——包括我們西港——都已經有自己的足球隊。你不必很高、不必跑最快，只要有一顆球、一個朋友、一個笑容，就可以開始。

IV.

Two Easy Drills

兩個簡單的暖身練習
🏃
Jog Around the Field
繞場慢跑暖身
What
A slow run around the whole field — one loop.
圍著整個場地，慢慢跑一圈。
Why
To warm up your body and your team.
讓身體和隊友都熱起來。
Coach says
"Jog with me — slow and steady. Don't sprint!"
「慢慢跟我跑，不要衝刺！」
⚽
First Kicks Line
排排站第一腳
What
Stand in a line. Kick the ball gently to the coach.
排成一直線，輕輕把球踢給教練。
Why
Feel the soccer ball with your foot — for the first time.
第一次用腳感覺足球。
Coach says
"Soft foot. Watch the ball. Smile — it's fun!"
「腳放輕、看球、笑一個——很好玩的！」
V.

Roleplay Scripts

三個情境腳本 · 點 🔊 聽逐句範讀
SCENARIO 01
Joining the Soccer Team
Goal: Ask the coach if you can join.
第一次跟教練說「我想加入」。
Student
Hi, Coach! Can I join the team?
Coach
Of course! What's your name?
Student
I'm Mei-Ling. I've never played, but I love it already.
Coach
Great! Grab a jersey. Today is your first day on the field.
SCENARIO 02
Getting Ready
Goal: Put on your gear before practice.
練習前把裝備穿好。
Friend
Are your cleats ready?
You
Yes! And my socks too. My jersey is number 7.
Friend
Cool! Let's run to the field together!
SCENARIO 03
First Touch of the Ball
Goal: Listen to the coach's first instruction.
教練教你的第一個動作。
Coach
OK team! Stand in a line. Watch the ball.
Student
Like this? Soft kick?
Coach
Perfect! Big smiles, everyone. Soccer is fun!
