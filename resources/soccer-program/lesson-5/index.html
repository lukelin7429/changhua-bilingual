II.

No One Wins Alone

沒有人是一個人贏的

You may have seen famous soccer stars on TV. But here's the secret: even the best player needs ten other people. One team — that's how soccer is played. If you only kick alone, you cannot win.

On the field, each player has a position. The defender stays near our goal and stops the other team. The midfielder runs the most — connecting defense and attack. The forward stays up front, ready to score. The captain wears a special armband and helps everyone stay calm.

The most important thing on the field is not kicking — it's helping. Help your friend up when they fall. Cheer them on. Pass when they're open. That's what a real team player does. The score on the board doesn't matter as much as the team you build.

你可能在電視上看過很厲害的球星，但真正的秘密是：再厲害的球員，也需要另外十個隊友。「一個隊伍（one team）」，這才是足球。一個人踢，是踢不贏的。場上每個人都有「位置（position）」：後衛（defender）守在自家球門前；中場（midfielder）跑最多，連結防守與進攻；前鋒（forward）站最前面，準備射門；隊長（captain）戴著特殊袖標，幫大家穩住情緒。場上最重要的事不是「踢」，是「幫忙（help）」——朋友跌倒了扶他起來、為他加油、有空檔時把球傳給他。這才叫團隊球員（team player）。記分板上的分數，沒有你和隊友建立的友情重要。

IV.

Two Team Drills

兩個團隊練習
🧭
Position Circle
位置認識圈
What
Stand in a circle. Coach calls a position; that player runs into the middle.
圍成一圈，教練喊一個位置，被叫到的同學跑到中間。
Why
To learn the names of all positions on the field.
學會場上每個位置的英文名字。
Coach says
"Defender! ... Captain! ... Forward!"
「後衛！⋯隊長！⋯前鋒！」
🎙️
Call and Pass
喊球傳球
What
In groups of four, you must call ("I'm open!") before anyone can pass to you.
4 人一組，先喊「我有空！」才能接到傳球。
Why
To practice loud, clear support on the field.
練習在場上大聲清楚地溝通。
Coach says
"No call, no pass! Cheer when your friend scores!"
「沒喊就不傳！朋友進球要為他歡呼！」
V.

Roleplay Scripts

三個情境腳本 · 點 🔊 聽逐句範讀
SCENARIO 01
Choosing Positions
Goal: Decide who plays where.
分配位置。
Captain
Mei-Ling — you're fast. Want to be a forward?
Mei-Ling
Yes! I'll try to score!
Captain
Tian — you're strong. Can you be a defender?
Tian
Yes! I'll protect our goal!
SCENARIO 02
Calling for the Ball
Goal: Tell your team where you are.
告訴隊友你在哪裡。
You
I'm open! On your left!
Teammate
Got it! Cover me — I'll pass!
You
I'm right behind you. Go!
SCENARIO 03
When a Friend Falls
Goal: Help a teammate get up.
朋友跌倒了，你要扶他。
Friend
Ouch! I tripped!
You
Are you OK? Let me help you up.
Friend
Thanks! You're a true teammate.
You
Come on! Let's cheer for the team!
