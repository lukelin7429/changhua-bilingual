II.

Why January 1?

為什麼是 1 月 1 日

The day we call New Year's Day — January 1 — has a longer history than you might think. About 2,000 years ago, the Roman ruler Julius Caesar decided to fix the old calendar and chose January as the first month. Many centuries later, in 1582, Pope Gregory made a few small changes — and that calendar, the Gregorian calendar, is the one most of the world uses today.

For Chinese families, the most important "new year" is still the Lunar New Year in February. But January 1 is a special day too — a chance to look back on the year that just ended, and to make a quiet promise to yourself for the year that is starting.

People all over the world celebrate New Year's Eve with fireworks, music, and watching the clock count down from ten. Some kiss someone they love at midnight. Some write a list of resolutions — things they will try to do better this year. Some just go to bed early and hope tomorrow is a better day. It's all part of the same simple idea: every year, every person, can start fresh.

1 月 1 日這一天比想像中還要古老。約兩千年前，羅馬統治者凱撒大帝改革曆法，把一月訂為第一個月。十六世紀，教宗格雷格里十三世微調，成為今日全世界通用的「格里曆」。對華人來說，最重要的新年仍是農曆春節，但 1 月 1 日這一天也很特別——它是回顧過去一年、許下新希望的時刻。各國都有跨年夜習俗：煙火、倒數、午夜時刻的擁抱、寫下新年計畫。或者，也有人乾脆早睡，希望明天會更好。共同的精神都一樣——每個人，每一年，都能重新開始。

IV.

Six New Year Traditions

六大跨年習俗 · 注意金色標記的單字
🌃
The Midnight Countdown
午夜倒數
What
A live countdown from 10 to 1 on TV.
When
From 11:59:50 to midnight on December 31.
Why
To count together is to feel the new year arrive.
🎆
Fireworks Over the City
城市煙火
What
Big fireworks shows from Taipei 101 or Sydney Harbour.
When
At midnight, right after the countdown.
Why
To celebrate the new year with light and sound.
✨
Sparklers on the Sidewalk
仙女棒
What
Kids light sparklers in the yard or street.
When
Right after the fireworks end.
Why
Small, safe, and just as excited as the big fireworks.
📝
Writing Resolutions
寫新年新希望
What
A short list of three resolutions for the year.
When
New Year's Day morning, after waking up.
Why
To plan a small step every day toward a better self.
📺
Watching New Year Eve Shows
看跨年晚會
What
Music, comedy, and live performances on TV.
When
From 8pm until past midnight.
Why
A warm way to spend the last hours of the year with family.
🍽️
Family New Year Meal
跨年家庭聚餐
What
A long, slow dinner with the people you love.
When
December 31, before the countdown.
Why
To start the year feeling hopeful and connected.

Roleplay Scripts

SCENARIO 01
The Countdown at Home
Goal: Count down with the family at midnight.
在家裡和家人一起午夜倒數。
Dad
Quick! It's almost midnight. Ten seconds to go!
Child
Let's count together! 10, 9, 8, 7…
Dad
3, 2, 1 — Happy New Year!
Child
I'm so excited for this year! Look at the fireworks!
SCENARIO 02
Writing Resolutions with a Friend
Goal: Share your new year promises.
和朋友互相分享新年新希望。
Maya
I made my resolutions last night. Want to hear them?
Owen
Yes! What are they?
Maya
Number one — read three English books. Number two — call Grandma every Sunday.
Owen
I'll plan mine too. A fresh year, a fresh start!
SCENARIO 03
A Phone Call to a Cousin
Goal: Wish a cousin in another city a happy new year.
打電話跟外地的表親拜年。
Cousin Lin
Hi! Happy New Year! How did you celebrate?
Cousin Hao
We watched the Taipei 101 fireworks from our rooftop.
Cousin Lin
Wow, lucky you! What's your resolution this year?
Cousin Hao
To visit you in Tainan in summer! I'm so hopeful about this year.
Cousin Lin
Me too. May your year be a beautiful one!
