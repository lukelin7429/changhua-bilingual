II.

A Day for Book Lovers

愛書人的節日由來

The 23rd of April is a very special day for book lovers around the world. On this exact day in 1616, two of the most famous writers in history — William Shakespeare from England and Miguel de Cervantes from Spain — both died. Many years later, in 1995, UNESCO decided to honor them by making April 23 World Book Day.

Today, more than 100 countries celebrate this day. In some places, children dress up as their favorite book characters. In others, schools hold book swaps, library visits, and "read-aloud" mornings. In Catalonia, Spain, people give each other roses and books as gifts — a romantic tradition that spread around the world.

The big idea of World Book Day is simple: books open doors to other worlds. Whether you read about a brave girl, an old wizard, or a tiny mouse, every page is an invitation to be curious, to imagine, and to dream a little bigger.

每年 4 月 23 日是全球愛書人的節日。1616 年的這一天，英國劇作家莎士比亞與西班牙小說家賽萬提斯同日辭世。1995 年聯合國教科文組織為紀念他們，把這天訂為「世界閱讀日」。今天超過 100 個國家慶祝這一天——有些學校讓孩子扮成書中角色，有些舉辦圖書交換、朗讀會、圖書館導覽。在西班牙加泰隆尼亞，人們互送一本書與一朵玫瑰，是一個浪漫又溫柔的傳統。

Essential Vocabulary

Reading Words · 閱讀名詞

✍️
Author
/ˈɑː.θɚ/ · n.
作者
The person who wrote a book.
"My favorite author writes funny stories for kids."
👓
Reader
/ˈriː.dɚ/ · n.
讀者
A person who reads books.
"A good reader finishes one book every week."
📑
Chapter
/ˈtʃæp.tɚ/ · n.
章節
One part of a book, with its own title and pages.
"I finished chapter three before dinner."
🏛️
Library
/ˈlaɪ.brer.i/ · n.
圖書館
A quiet place full of books to borrow.
"Our school library has 5,000 books."
📖
Novel
/ˈnɑː.vəl/ · n.
小說
A long story book, often made up by the author.
"This novel has 20 chapters and a happy ending."
🏪
Bookstore
/ˈbʊk.stɔːr/ · n.
書店
A shop that sells new and old books.
"On Book Day, bookstores give a rose to each visitor."

Reading Actions & Feelings · 動作與感覺

👍
Recommend
/ˌrek.əˈmend/ · v.

📥
Borrow
/ˈbɑːr.oʊ/ · v.

🧚
Imagine

🤓

💡

Curious

IV.

Six Ways to Celebrate

六種慶祝閱讀日的方式 · 注意金色標記的單字
🔄
Book Swap
圖書交換
What
Bring one book from home, take a different one back.
When
All day in the classroom or school hall.
Why
To recommend a favorite to someone new.
🎤
Read-Aloud Morning
朗讀晨會
What
Students take turns reading a story aloud to the class.
When
The first 15 minutes of the school day.
Why
An inspiring way to share a favorite passage.
🏛️
Library Tour
圖書館導覽
What
A walk through the school library with the librarian.
When
In a special class hour on April 23.
Why
To learn what to borrow and how to find any book fast.
🌟
Author Spotlight
作家專題
What
Reading one author's books for the whole week.
When
The week of April 23.
Why
A focused way to enjoy a single writer's style.
🌹
A Rose and a Book
玫瑰與書
What
Friends or family exchange one rose and one book.
When
All day on April 23.
Why
A tradition from Spain — small, gentle, and very bookish.
📝
My Top 3 Books
我的三本好書
What
Writing a short list of three books you really love.
When
A simple homework for the night before.
Why
A way to make a curious friend pick up your favorite novel.
V.

Roleplay Scripts

三個情境腳本 · 點 🔊 聽逐句範讀
SCENARIO 01
Recommending a Book to a Friend
Goal: Use a simple template to share why you love a book.
用簡單句型向朋友推薦一本書。
Ann
Hey, I just finished a really inspiring book. Want me to recommend it?
Bo
Sure! What is it about?
Ann
It's a novel about a girl who can talk to forest animals.
Bo
Who is the author?
Ann
A British writer. The first chapter is short — try it tonight!
SCENARIO 02
Borrowing Books from the Library
Goal: Practice library English with the librarian.
在圖書館跟館員借書。
Student
Excuse me, can I borrow three books today?
Librarian
Of course! You can borrow up to five. What are you curious about?
Student
I love stories with dragons. Any recommendations?
Librarian
Try this novel. Each chapter is short and very exciting.
Student
Perfect — I'll bring them back next Friday.
SCENARIO 03
Sharing a Book at Home
Goal: Tell a family member what you read today.
回家後跟家人分享今天讀的書。
Child
Dad, today is World Book Day! Our teacher gave us a list of inspiring stories.
Dad
Tell me your favorite one. I want to imagine the story too.
Child
It's about a bookish boy who builds a paper rocket and flies to the moon.
Dad
Sounds wonderful. Let's go to the bookstore this weekend and buy a copy.
