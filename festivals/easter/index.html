II.

A Story of Spring

春日節慶的由來

Easter is a holiday that comes in spring. The word "Easter" comes from an old word for the spring season, when flowers bloom and baby birds hatch from their eggs. For many Christian families in the West, Easter Sunday is a special day to remember an important story.

But Easter is also a happy holiday for children around the world. Long ago, families in Germany started telling kids that a friendly bunny came every Easter morning to hide colorful eggs in the garden. Children would wake up early, take their baskets, and run outside to hunt for the eggs.

Today, families everywhere celebrate Easter with painted eggs, chocolate bunnies, and big springtime meals. It is a cheerful day to welcome the new flowers, the longer afternoons, and the warm sunshine of April.

復活節是西方春天的節日。「Easter」一詞源自古英語「春天」的意思。對許多基督徒家庭來說，復活節星期日是紀念耶穌復活的特別日子。但對全世界的孩子而言，復活節也是充滿樂趣的節日——德國最早出現了「復活節兔子在花園藏蛋」的故事，孩子們提著籃子尋找彩蛋。今天的復活節有彩蛋、巧克力兔、春日大餐，是迎接春天的快樂時刻。

III.

Essential Vocabulary

Festival Words · 節慶名詞

🥚
Egg
/eɡ/ · n.
蛋
The round white or brown thing laid by a hen.
"Easter eggs are painted in bright spring colors."
🐰
Bunny
/ˈbʌn.i/ · n.
兔子（小朋友用語）
A small rabbit, often used as a friendly word for kids.
"The Easter Bunny hides eggs in the garden."
🔎
Hunt
/hʌnt/ · n.
尋找比賽 · 找蛋遊戲
A game of looking for hidden things.
"The Easter egg hunt starts at ten o'clock."
🧺
Basket
/ˈbæs.kɪt/ · n.
籃子
A container with a handle for carrying things.
"My basket is full of pretty eggs!"
🍫
Chocolate
/ˈtʃɑːk.lət/ · n.
巧克力
A sweet brown food made from cocoa beans.
"I got a chocolate bunny inside my basket."
🌷
Spring
/sprɪŋ/ · n.
春天
The season after winter when flowers grow.
"Easter always falls in early spring."

Easter Actions & Descriptions · 動作與描述

🙈
Hide
/haɪd/ · v.
藏
To put something where people cannot see it.
"My dad hides the eggs the night before."
✨
Find
/faɪnd/ · v.
找到
To discover something that was hidden.
"I found ten eggs in twenty minutes!"
🎨
Dye
/daɪ/ · v.
染色
To change the color of something using paint or color water.
"We dye the eggs pink, yellow, and blue."
😊
Cheerful
/ˈtʃɪr.fəl/ · adj.
開心的 · 愉快的
Happy and full of bright energy.
"Easter Sunday is a cheerful family day."
🎀
Pastel
/pæˈstel/ · adj.
粉色系的 · 淡色的
Soft, light colors like baby pink and baby blue.
"Easter eggs are usually painted in pastel colors."
🐣
Hatch
/hætʃ/ · v.
孵化
When a baby bird comes out of its egg.
"The chick will hatch from its shell in spring."
IV.

Festival Highlights

六大復活節習俗 · 注意金色標記的單字
🥚
The Egg Hunt
尋蛋活動
What
Kids search the garden for colorful hidden eggs.
When
Easter Sunday morning, just after breakfast.
Why
To find the eggs the bunny hides overnight.
🎨
Egg Dyeing
染蛋
What
Boiled eggs dipped in cups of bright color water.
When
The afternoon before Easter Sunday.
Why
To dye eggs in beautiful pastel colors as a family.
🐰
The Easter Bunny
復活節兔子
What
A friendly bunny who delivers eggs to good children.
When
During the night before Easter morning.
Why
A cheerful story that started in Germany long ago.
🧺
The Easter Basket
復活節提籃
What
A pretty basket for collecting eggs and small gifts.
When
Carried during the egg hunt and after.
Why
A traditional basket to hold every egg you find.
🍫
Chocolate Eggs
巧克力蛋
What
Hollow eggs made of milk or dark chocolate.
When
Eaten on Easter Sunday with the family.
Why
A sweet treat — chocolate shaped like a real egg.
🌷
Easter Sunday Brunch
復活節家庭午餐
What
A big family meal with ham, eggs, and spring vegetables.
When
Late Sunday morning after the egg hunt.
Why
A warm welcome to the new spring with everyone you love.
V.

Roleplay Scripts

三個情境腳本 · 點 🔊 聽逐句範讀
SCENARIO 01
The Egg Hunt with a Friend
Goal: Talk about finding hidden eggs using festival vocabulary.
和朋友一起尋找彩蛋。
Mia
Look! My dad hid ten eggs in the backyard this morning!
Leo
My basket is ready! Let's go and find them.
Mia
There's a blue one under the bush!
Leo
And a yellow chocolate one in the tree!
Mia
This is the most cheerful morning of the year!
SCENARIO 02
Dyeing Eggs with Mom
Goal: Describe colors and steps using festival vocabulary.
和媽媽一起染彩蛋。
Child
Mom, what color shall we dye this egg?
Mom
How about a soft pastel pink? Dip it for one minute.
Child
Now I want to draw little flowers on it!
Mom
Beautiful. Tonight Dad will hide it somewhere very tricky.
Child
I will find them all tomorrow morning!
SCENARIO 03
Meeting the Easter Bunny
Goal: Have a friendly conversation at a community Easter fair.
在社區復活節園遊會上跟兔子聊天。
Child
Hello, Mr. Bunny! Are you the one who hides the eggs?
Bunny
Yes, I am! Are you ready for the great egg hunt?
Child
My basket is empty. Where did you hide them all?
Bunny
Some are high, some are low. Look for the bright pastel colors!
Child
Thank you! Happy Easter and happy spring!
