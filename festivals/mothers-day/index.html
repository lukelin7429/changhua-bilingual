II.

The Story of Anna Jarvis

Anna Jarvis 與第一個母親節

Mother's Day started in the United States in 1908. A woman named Anna Jarvis wanted to honor her own mother, who had spent years caring for sick people during the American Civil War. Anna held the first Mother's Day at a small church in West Virginia, and gave white carnations to every mother who came — because carnations were her own mother's favorite flowers.

The idea spread quickly. In 1914, the U.S. President made the second Sunday of May an official holiday for all mothers across the country. Today, more than 50 countries celebrate Mother's Day on this same day.

In Taiwan, families give carnations on Mother's Day — pink for moms who are still with us, white in memory of those who have passed away. Children make cards, cook breakfast in bed, or simply say two of the most important words in any language: "Thank you, Mom."

母親節起源於 1908 年的美國，Anna Jarvis 為了紀念照顧過南北戰爭傷兵的母親，在西維吉尼亞的小教堂舉辦了第一場母親節，並送給每位母親一朵白色康乃馨——那是她媽媽最愛的花。1914 年美國總統正式定五月第二個星期日為母親節，全美一同慶祝。今天超過 50 個國家共同慶祝這一天。在台灣，孩子們會送康乃馨：粉色代表健在的母親，白色則紀念已故的母親。除了花，一張卡片、一份親手做的早餐，或一句「謝謝媽媽」，都是最深的祝福。

IV.

Six Ways to Celebrate

六種慶祝母親節的方式 · 注意金色標記的單字
💐
Carnations
送康乃馨
What
A single pink or white carnation, or a small bouquet.
When
Given in the morning, before any meal.
Why
Pink for living moms; white in memory of those we miss.
💌
Handmade Card
手作卡片
What
A folded paper with a kid's own drawing and a short message.
When
Made the day or week before.
Why
A card is small but the most thoughtful kind of gift.
🍳
Breakfast in Bed
床邊早餐
What
Toast, fruit, and a warm drink on a tray.
When
Early Sunday morning, before Mom wakes up.
Why
A quiet way to surprise the most devoted person at home.
📞
A Long Phone Call
遠距離的長電話
What
A call to Mom when she lives far from you.
When
Any time during Mother's Day.
Why
Distance is no excuse — a child must thank their mom.
🍽️
Family Brunch
家庭早午餐
What
A relaxed meal at home or in a quiet restaurant.
When
Late Sunday morning.
Why
A brunch table is where every family cherishes each other.
🤗
Saying Thanks Out Loud
親口說謝謝
What
A real, eye-to-eye "thank you, Mom."
When
Right before going to bed on Sunday night.
Why
A long hug and three little words mean more than any gift.
V.

Roleplay Scripts

三個情境腳本 · 點 🔊 聽逐句範讀
SCENARIO 01
Buying Flowers at the Florist
Goal: Order a carnation bouquet using polite English.
在花店買康乃馨花束。
Student
Hello! I'd like to buy a bouquet for my mom, please.
Florist
Of course. Pink or white carnations?
Student
Pink, please. My mom is the most caring person I know.
Florist
Here you are — twelve carnations with a small card.
Student
Thank you so much. She is going to be so surprised!
SCENARIO 02
Making Breakfast for Mom
Goal: Plan a quiet morning surprise with a sibling.
和兄弟姊妹計畫一個早晨的驚喜。
Big Sister
Mom is still sleeping. Let's surprise her with breakfast in bed.
Little Brother
Good idea! What should I make?
Big Sister
You make the toast. I'll prepare the fruit and warm milk.
Little Brother
Don't forget the card we made yesterday!
Big Sister
Of course. This is the most thoughtful Mother's Day plan ever.
SCENARIO 03
A Phone Call to Mom
Goal: Say "thank you" out loud when you cannot be home.
在無法回家的時候，用電話對媽媽說謝謝。
Child
Hi Mom! Happy Mother's Day!
Mom
Oh, sweetheart, thank you for calling!
Child
I miss you so much. I want to thank you for everything you do.
Mom
I am so proud of you. Eat well and stay safe.
Child
I will. I cherish every phone call with you. Big hug!
