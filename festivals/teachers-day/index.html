II.

The Story of Confucius

孔子的故事

Confucius (Kǒngzǐ, 孔子) was born about 2,500 years ago in a small kingdom called Lu, in eastern China. As a young man, he saw that society around him was full of fighting and confusion. He believed people could live in peace if they learned to respect each other, behave well, and never stop learning.

He spent his whole life travelling from kingdom to kingdom, teaching anyone who came to him — rich or poor, smart or slow. He had over three thousand students, and his ideas became the foundation of Chinese education for the next two thousand years. Even today, kids in Taiwan, Korea, and Japan grow up reading his short, wise sayings.

In Taiwan, September 28 is celebrated as Teachers' Day, because this is the day Confucius was born. Special ceremonies are held at Confucius temples across the country. But for most schoolkids, this is also a day to do something very simple: thank your own teacher for a whole year of patience.

孔子（Kǒngzǐ）約兩千五百年前出生於東方小國「魯」。年輕時他看到社會紛亂，相信只要人們懂得彼此尊重、行為端正、永不停止學習，就能和平共處。他一生周遊列國授課，無論貧富智愚都願意教導，門下弟子三千。他的思想成為華人教育兩千年的基礎。在臺灣，每年 9 月 28 日是教師節，因為這是孔子的生日。全臺各地的孔廟會舉行祭孔典禮，但對小朋友來說，這也是一個簡單而美好的日子——跟自己的老師親口說一聲「謝謝」。

IV.

Six Ways to Honor a Teacher

六種感謝老師的方式 · 注意金色標記的單字
🍎
A Small Thank-You Gift
小小謝禮
What
An apple, a flower, or a hand-picked piece of fruit.
When
Given quietly at the start of class.
Why
A simple way to show your teacher you noticed her work.
💌
A Handwritten Card
手寫卡片
What
A folded paper card with your real words inside.
When
Prepared the night before, given on the morning of 9/28.
Why
A patient teacher remembers a real card for years.
🏯
Visiting a Confucius Temple
參觀孔廟
What
A family or school visit to the local temple.
When
Morning of September 28.
Why
To learn how Taiwan has honored teaching for centuries.
📜
Reading a Confucius Saying
讀孔子的名言
What
One short saying, read out loud in class.
When
During morning meeting on 9/28.
Why
A way to share the wisdom that started it all.
🎓
Saying Thank You in Class
在教室親口道謝
What
A clear, eye-to-eye "Thank you, teacher."
When
At the end of class, before lining up.
Why
Two words can encourage a tired teacher more than any gift.
📚
Helping a Younger Student
幫忙低年級的同學
What
Spending a recess teaching a younger kid.
When
During morning or lunch break.
Why
To teach someone else is to thank every teacher you ever had.
V.

Roleplay Scripts

三個情境腳本 · 點 🔊 聽逐句範讀
SCENARIO 01
Giving Teacher a Thank-You Card
Goal: Hand a card to your teacher with a few sincere sentences.
親手把卡片交給老師。
Student
Good morning, Teacher Wang. Happy Teachers' Day!
Teacher
Good morning! Oh, is that for me?
Student
Yes! I wrote you a card. You are the most patient teacher I have ever had.
Teacher
Thank you so much. This means more than you know.
Student
You always encourage me, even when I make mistakes.
SCENARIO 02
Visiting Confucius Temple
Goal: Ask questions about the temple with a family member.
和家人去孔廟，提問與聆聽。
Child
Dad, why is this temple so quiet today?
Dad
Because it is Teachers' Day. People come to honor Confucius.
Child
Did he really have three thousand students?
Dad
Yes — and he was humble enough to keep learning from them too.
Child
That is real wisdom.
SCENARIO 03
Helping a Younger Student
Goal: Be a small teacher for a day.
當小老師一天，教低年級的同學。
Big Brother
Need help with that math problem? Let me teach you.
Younger Kid
I keep getting it wrong. I am so bad at math.
Big Brother
No, you just need a different way. Watch — count one, two, three.
Younger Kid
Oh! I see it now!
Big Brother
You see? You can learn anything if you stay calm.
