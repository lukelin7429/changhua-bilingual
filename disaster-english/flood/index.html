Flood Safety

水災安全

"When the water rises — Watch, go to high ground, stay away."

I.

Today's Lesson

今日課程

Heavy rain and typhoons can cause floods. Rivers get higher. Roads disappear under water. Today we learn how to stay safe: Watch the water, go to high ground, stay away from the current.大雨和颱風會造成水災。河水變高，馬路被淹沒。今天我們學習如何保持安全：看好水位、往高處走、不要靠近水流。

🎯 Today's Goal

By the end of this lesson, you can name three steps for a flood and explain why we never walk through floodwater.下課時，你能用英文說出水災三步驟，也能解釋為什麼絕不能涉水。

II.

Words to Know

10 個必備單字

Word 01
flood (n)

Word 02
river (n)
河
The river is getting higher.河水越來越高。
Word 03
rain (n)
雨
Heavy rain can cause a flood.大雨會造成水災。
Word 04
high ground (n)

Go to high ground to stay safe.往高處走才安全。
Word 05
current (n)

Word 06

III.

The 3 Steps

救命三步驟
1
👀
Watch
注意觀察
Listen to weather news. Watch how high the water is.聽氣象新聞，注意水位。
2
⛰️
High Ground
到高處
If water is rising, move to high ground. Don't wait.水位上升時，立刻到高處——別等。
3
🚫
Stay Away
不要靠近
Never walk or swim in floodwater. The current is strong.絕對不要在水中走或游——水流非常強。
IV.

Do & Don't

可以做、不可以做

✅ Do

  • Listen to weather warnings.聽氣象警報。
  • Go to a higher floor or hill.往樓上或山坡走。
  • Keep important things dry.重要東西保持乾燥。
  • Wait until the water goes down.等水退了再行動。

❌ Don't

  • Don't walk in floodwater.不要在淹水中走。
  • Don't drive through water.不要開車穿越積水。
  • Don't swim in a flooded river.不要在淹水的河裡游泳。
  • Don't go close to the riverbank.不要靠近河岸。
V.

Class Roleplay

課堂情境練習

🎬 Scene: After a heavy typhoon, water is rising near the school

Teacher
Look — the water is getting higher. We need to go upstairs.
看，水越來越高，我們要往樓上。
Student A
Can I go home to get my dog?
我可以回家拿我的狗嗎？
Teacher
No. The roads are flooded. Stay here where it's safe.
不行，馬路淹水了。留在這裡比較安全。
Student B
I want to see the river. It's so big now!
我想看看河，現在好大！
Teacher
No, please. Stay away from the water. The current is very strong.
不要喔，遠離水。水流非常強。
