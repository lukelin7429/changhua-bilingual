Fire Safety

火災安全

"If you smell smoke — Stay Low, Find Exit, Stay Out."

I.

Today's Lesson

今日課程

Fires can start anywhere — kitchens, schools, even cars. When you see flames or smell smoke, you have to act fast. Today we learn three simple steps that keep us safe: Stay Low, Find Exit, Stay Out.火災可能發生在任何地方——廚房、學校、甚至車裡。看到火或聞到煙，要立刻行動。今天我們學三個簡單的英文：Stay Low（蹲低）、Find Exit（找出口）、Stay Out（不要回去）。

🎯 Today's Goal

By the end of this lesson, you can name three steps for a fire and act them out when your teacher calls 'Fire drill!'下課時，你能用英文說出火災三步驟，當老師喊「Fire drill!」時做得出動作。

II.

Words to Know

10 個必備單字
點每個單字旁邊的 🔊 按鈕可以聽英文發音
Word 01
fire (n)
火
There is a fire in the kitchen.廚房裡起火了。
Word 02
smoke (n)
煙
Smoke goes up — that's why we stay low.煙會往上飄——所以我們要蹲低。
Word 03
alarm (n)
警報器
When the fire alarm rings, listen carefully.火警警報響時，要仔細聽。
Word 04
exit (n)
出口
Find the exit sign on the wall.找牆上的出口標誌。
Word 05
escape (v)
逃生
We have to escape the building now.我們現在必須逃出這棟建築。
Word 06
stay low (v)
蹲低
Stay low so you can breathe.蹲低才能呼吸到乾淨空氣。
Word 07
fire drill (n)
消防演習
Our school has a fire drill every term.我們學校每學期都有消防演習。
Word 08
burn (v)
燃燒
Hot things can burn your skin.熱的東西會燙傷皮膚。
Word 09
cool (adj)
涼的
Put cool water on a small burn.小燙傷用涼水沖。
Word 10
safe (adj)
安全的
Outside the building is safe.建築物外面是安全的。
III.

The 3 Steps

救命三步驟
1
🦵
Stay Low
蹲低
Get down low. Smoke is at the top of the room.蹲低或爬行。煙在房間上方，下方空氣較乾淨。
2
🚪
Find Exit
找出口
Look for the exit sign. Walk fast but don't run.找到出口標誌。快走但不要奔跑。
3
🛑
Stay Out
不要回去
Once outside, stay outside. Don't go back in.出來後就別再進去——書包、玩具都不重要。
IV.

Do & Don't

可以做、不可以做

✅ Do

  • Listen to the alarm.聽警報器。
  • Stay low under the smoke.蹲低，躲開煙。
  • Follow the exit signs.跟著出口標誌走。
  • Tell a teacher you're safe.告訴老師你安全了。

❌ Don't

  • Don't run or push.不要奔跑或推擠。
  • Don't hide in a closet.不要躲在衣櫃裡。
  • Don't use the elevator.不要搭電梯。
  • Don't go back inside.不要回去拿東西。
V.

Class Roleplay

課堂情境練習

🎬 Scene: A fire alarm rings during class

火警警報！蹲低跟我到出口！
Student A
I can see smoke. I'll stay low.
我看到煙了。我會蹲低。
Student B
I'm holding my friend's hand. We're heading for the exit.
我牽著朋友的手，往出口走。
Teacher
Good. Walk fast but don't run.
很好，快走但不要奔跑。
Student C
We're outside. Everyone is safe!
我們出來了！大家都安全！
