First Aid & CPR

急救與心肺復甦

"If someone is hurt — Call 119. Stay calm. Get help fast."

I.

Today's Lesson

今日課程

When someone is hurt or stops breathing, we don't panic — we help. Today we learn three steps: Call 119, Check, and Press Hard. CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation) can save a life.有人受傷或停止呼吸時，我們不慌張——我們幫忙。今天學三步驟：Call 119（撥119）、Check（檢查）、Press Hard（用力按）。CPR（心肺復甦術）能救人。

🎯 Today's Goal

By the end of this lesson, you can call 119 in English-style basic phrases, and explain the three steps of helping someone.下課時，你能用簡單英文撥打 119，也能說出救人三步驟。

III.

The 3 Steps

救命三步驟
1
📞
Call 119
撥 119
Tell them where you are and what happened.告訴他們你在哪裡、發生什麼事。
2
👀
Check
檢查
Is the person breathing? Are they hurt?這個人有呼吸嗎？哪裡受傷？
3
💪
Press Hard
用力按壓
If they are not breathing, do CPR — press hard and fast in the middle of the chest.若沒呼吸，做 CPR——胸口中央用力快速按壓。
IV.

Do & Don't

可以做、不可以做

✅ Do

  • Stay calm.保持冷靜。
  • Call 119 right away.立刻打 119。
  • Tell a grown-up.告訴大人。
  • Stay with the hurt person.陪在受傷的人身邊。

❌ Don't

  • Don't move someone with a hurt neck.不要移動脖子受傷的人。
  • Don't leave the person alone.不要丟下受傷的人。
  • Don't feed them or give them water if they can't talk.不能說話時不要餵水餵食。
  • Don't panic — stay calm to help.不要慌張，冷靜才能幫忙。

冷靜。他有呼吸嗎？
