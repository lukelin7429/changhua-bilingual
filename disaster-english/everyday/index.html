Everyday Safety

日常安全

"Small habits keep us safe every day — at school, at home, and online."

I.

Today's Lesson

今日課程

Big disasters get the headlines, but everyday safety is what protects us most. Today we cover three areas: roads, strangers, and the internet.大災害上新聞，但日常的小習慣才是真正保護我們的關鍵。今天學三件事：道路安全、陌生人、網路。

🎯 Today's Goal

By the end of this lesson, you can name a safety habit for each area: street, stranger, and online.下課時，你能說出三個安全習慣：道路上、遇到陌生人、上網時。

II.

Words to Know

10 個必備單字
點每個單字旁邊的 🔊 按鈕可以聽英文發音
Word 01
safety (n)
安全
Safety is the most important thing.安全最重要。
Word 02
helmet (n)
安全帽
Wear a helmet when you ride a bike.騎腳踏車要戴安全帽。
Word 03
sidewalk (n)
人行道
Walk on the sidewalk, not in the road.走人行道，不要走馬路。
Word 04
crosswalk (n)
斑馬線
Cross at the crosswalk.要從斑馬線過馬路。
Word 05
traffic light (n)
紅綠燈
Wait for the green traffic light.等綠燈再走。
Word 06
stranger (n)
陌生人
A stranger is someone you don't know.陌生人就是你不認識的人。
Word 07
online (adj)
線上
Be careful online.上網要小心。
Word 08
password (n)
密碼
Don't share your password.不要告訴別人密碼。
Word 09
trust (v)
信任
Trust your parents and teachers.信任父母和老師。
Word 10
tell (v)
告訴
Tell a grown-up if something feels wrong.感覺不對就告訴大人。
III.

The 3 Steps

救命三步驟
1
🚴
Helmet On
戴安全帽
Wear a helmet on your bike. Use the sidewalk.騎車戴安全帽，走人行道。
2
👀
Look Both Ways
看左右兩邊
Stop at the crosswalk. Look left, look right, then cross.在斑馬線停下，看左右，再過馬路。
3
🗣️
Tell a Grown-up
告訴大人
A stranger or online — if it feels wrong, tell a grown-up you trust.陌生人或網路——感覺不對就告訴信任的大人。
IV.

Do & Don't

可以做、不可以做

✅ Do

  • Wear a helmet on a bike or scooter.騎腳踏車或滑板車戴安全帽。
  • Look both ways before you cross.過馬路前看左右。
  • Keep your password secret.密碼要保密。
  • Tell a parent or teacher you trust.告訴你信任的父母或老師。

❌ Don't

  • Don't talk to strangers alone.不要單獨跟陌生人講話。
  • Don't share your address online.不要在網路上分享地址。
  • Don't run across the road.不要在馬路上奔跑。
  • Don't click links from strangers.不要點陌生人傳的連結。
V.

Class Roleplay

課堂情境練習

🎬 Scene: Walking home, a stranger asks for directions

Stranger
Hi! Can you come help me find my dog?
嗨，可以幫我找狗嗎？
Student
I'm sorry, I have to ask my mom first.
不好意思，我要先問媽媽。
Stranger
It's just over there. Come quickly.
就在那邊，快來。
Student
No, thank you. I have to go now.
不用了，謝謝。我要走了。
Student (later, at home)
Mom, a stranger asked me to help him. I said no and came home.
媽媽，有個陌生人叫我幫他，我說不要就回家了。
