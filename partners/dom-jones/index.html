Mission 使命

為什麼 Dom 在台灣？

Why is Dom in Taiwan?

中文

Dom Jones 是美國加州國際知名的社會運動者、教育家與媒體人。目前她選擇長期定居台灣，全職投入服務台灣的青少年、學校與社區。

自 2025 年 12 月起，Dom 與人師教育協會（My Culture Connect）合作，展開全台灣巡迴學校集會計畫，走進彰化縣各中小學，以英語分享全球公民精神、同理心教育與聯合國永續發展目標（SDGs）。

「我立誓要成為自己未曾獲得的那份愛。我的使命，是將善意、團結與希望帶給每一位年輕人——讓他們知道，自己的聲音和夢想同樣重要。」
— Dom Jones
English

Dom Jones is an internationally recognized social advocate, educator, and media personality from California, USA. She has chosen to become a full-time resident of Taiwan, committed to serving youth, schools, and local communities through cultural exchange and global education.

Since December 2025, Dom has partnered with My Culture Connect (人師教育協會) to launch a nationwide school assembly tour across Taiwan — visiting schools in Changhua County to share global citizenship, empathy, English motivation, and the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

"Growing up without the love and guidance every child deserves, Dom made a vow to become the love she did not receive. Her life mission is to give kindness, unity, and hope — especially to young people who need to know their voice and dreams matter."
— Dom Jones

United Nations SDGs 聯合國永續發展目標

Dom 帶來的 SDGs 訊息

The SDGs Dom Brings to Every Classroom

Dom Jones 擔任聯合國協會橙縣分會政府事務大使，長期推廣聯合國 2030 永續發展目標。她走進台灣的每一間教室，用真實的人生故事讓學生理解：SDGs 不是遙遠的口號，而是每個人每天都能實踐的行動。

As a Government Affairs Ambassador for the United Nations Association (Orange County Chapter), Dom has dedicated her career to advancing the UN 2030 Agenda. In every school she visits, she brings the SDGs to life — showing students that global goals begin with local actions and personal choices.

Her Story 她的故事

從康普頓到全世界

From Compton to the World Stage

🇹🇼 中文版

Dom Jones 出生於美國加州康普頓（Compton）貧困的街頭，是十三個孩子的母親撫養長大的孩子。她在貧困與無家可歸的困境中成長，卻憑藉對教育的堅定信念，在美國中西部與南加州修讀了人文學、傳播學與女性研究。

近 20 年的健身、瑜伽與身心靈倡導經驗，讓她深刻理解健康對每個人的意義。她更學習了越南語與塔加洛語，以傳遞美國多元族群的聲音。

2022 年，她登上 CBS 《The Amazing Race》第 34 季，向全球觀眾展現跨文化的理解與同理心。在 2024 年美國大選期間，逾 10 萬名美國公民投票支持她出任民意代表。

如今，她選擇在台灣紮根，將這段不凡的生命旅程，化為給每一位台灣孩子的禮物。

🇺🇸 English Version

Dom Jones was born in the challenging streets of Compton, California, raised by a mother of thirteen children. Growing up in poverty and homelessness, she refused to be defined by her circumstances — pursuing higher education across the Midwest and Southern California in the humanities, communication studies, and women's studies.

With nearly 20 years in fitness, yoga, and wellness advocacy, Dom understands the deep connection between physical and emotional health. She even learned Vietnamese and Tagalog to amplify voices within diverse American communities.

In 2022, Dom appeared on CBS's The Amazing Race (Season 34), bringing her message of empathy and cross-cultural understanding to a global audience. During the 2024 U.S. elections, over 100,000 American citizens voted to have her represent them.

Today, she has chosen Taiwan as her home — turning an extraordinary life journey into a gift for every student she meets.
